General Information
Alexander: The Creator (Savage) is the third and final section of Alexander (Savage), the main 8-player raid in Heavensward. Released on September 27, 2016, Creator requires players with item level of 245 or higher to enter. Once unlocked, players can use Party Finder or other means of forming a premade party before entering the raid, which is located in Duty Finder → Raids → Savage Raids (Heavensward). They can also enter solo or with a party of fewer than 8 players using an Unrestricted Party.

Unlock
Completion of the level 60 quest Who Lives, Who Dies, Who Retells Your Story by talking to Wandering Minstrel in Mor Dhona (x21.7,y8.7). Players are required to first complete the side quest Of Endings and Beginnings.

Floors
Level Requirement: Disciple of War or Magic level 60
Party Size: Eight players

Item level restrictions do not apply when registering as a full party or an unrestricted party.

Rewards
Treasure coffers that appear upon completing areas in Alexander: The Creator (Savage) will yield accessories. In addition, players will receive a page of the Alexandrian Manifesto from each area, which can be exchanged for gear of your choosing.

Upon completing Alexander: The Creator (Savage) duties, a treasure coffer will appear containing an Alexandrian Coffer. Using this item transforms it into gear corresponding to the user's current job. In addition, the coffers that appear upon completing Alexander - The Soul of the Creator (Savage) yield weapons.
