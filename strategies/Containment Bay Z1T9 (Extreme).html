Nidhogg:

Phase 1:
- Auto-attacks: 🚫 Boss should always face away from party
- Platform Falls + 🔴 AoEs: Bait circles together, then 🏃 move away

Phase 2:

- Ice Pad: Spawns under boss, 🚫 can't move through
- Biting Halberd: Position directly behind boss after 3rd cleave
- Southern Cross: 🤝 Stack at rear to bait, then 🏃 move out

Phase 3 (Adds):
- Execrated Will: 🛡️ Tanked by tank, gains 🌟 Damage Up every 10s
- Execrated Thew: 🛡️ Small add, tanked by tank
- Execrated Wile: 🚫 Not tanked, face away during Meracydian Fear cast
- Execrated Wit: 🚫 Not tanked, kill before Meracydian Meteor finishes

Phase 4 Part 1:
- Tethers: 2 pairs each of 🔥 Infinite Fire & ❄️ Infinite Ice, stay close to partner
- Wave Cannon: 🎯 Targets random non-MT player, 🏃 stay away from target
- Tyrfing: 🛡️ 5 hits on MT + 🔥 damage, use cooldowns
- Southern Cross: 🤝 Stack under boss then 🏃 move with tether partner

- Broken Seal: 4 🔥 & 4 ❄️ meteors, match tether to meteor color
- Biting Halberd/Ciclicle/Tail End based on arena markers

Phase 4 Part 2:
- The Demon's Claw: 🛡️ High damage + Stun on MT, OT provoke. Position to avoid knockback
- Wave Cannon: 🎯 Targets MT, 🤝 stack in AoE box to share damage or use tank swap
- Repeat tethers, Wave Cannon, Tyrfing, Southern Cross, Broken Seal
- Flame Halberd: ⏳ Hard enrage, burn boss before cast

Position tips:
- Southern Cross: MT 🏃 right, party 🏃 left. Tethered to MT, go right
- Wave Cannon: Assign flanks for target & party
- Broken Seal: 1 🔥 & 1 ❄️ per flank, avoid other same-color pair
- Soar: Tanks N/S, DPS E/W, Healers with any. Go to safe spots based on Mirage pattern. Alternatively, use Tank LB before or Healer LB3 after, max 2 AoEs
