Eden's Promise Overview:
- 3rd section of Eden, the main 8-player raid in Shadowbringers
- Released on December 8, 2020
- Requires item level 485 or higher to enter
- Queued from the Duty Finder

Unlock Requirements:
- Complete all Eden Quests up through Life Finds a Way
- Clear all Eden's Gate and Eden's Verse raids
- Complete patch 5.3 MSQ Reflections in Crystal
- Begin level 80 quest chain starting with Worlds Apart in Amh Araeng (X:26.8, Y:16.7)

Floors:
- Eden's Promise: Umbra (E9N) - Boss: Cloud of Darkness
- Eden's Promise: Litany (E10N) - Boss: Shadowkeeper
- Eden's Promise: Anamorphosis (E11N) - Boss: Fatebreaker
- Eden's Promise: Eternity (E12N) - Boss: Eden's Promise

Rewards:
- Treasure coffers yield tokens that can be traded for gear
- No weekly loot restrictions as of patch 5.5
- No weekly restriction on entering Eden's Promise

Item Exchange:
- Trade tokens for gear with Ghul Gul in Amh Araeng (X:26.6 Y:16.4) or Yhal Yal in Eulmore (X:10.0 Y:11.5)
- Tokens drop from each floor and can be exchanged for specific gear slots
