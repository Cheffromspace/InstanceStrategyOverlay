Lamebrix Strikebocks
- Avoid electric field at arena edge that stuns, paralyzes, and kills with Electrocution debuff.
- Boss and players can activate traps: North (ice turrets), Middle (wrecking balls), South (spikes and Bleed debuff).
- Charge Attacks: Single, double or triple charges with indicators:
- Critical Wrath (blue/white circle): Spread out
- Bomb Toss (orange circle): Stack to mitigate damage on furthest player
- Gobswipe Conklops (green circle): 🔴 Donut AoE, move into boss hitbox
- Gobspin Whooshdrops (red circle with triangle): 💥 Point-blank AoE, move out

Phase 1
- Goblin Rush: 👊👊👊 Three-hit attack on main tank
- Gobrush Rush Gob: 🛡️ Tankbuster on main tank, use cooldowns
- Gobsway Rumblerocks: 💥 Raid-wide damage, mitigate and heal
- Single Charge: 🥊 Move in/out based on indicator
- Trap: Boss jumps to spike trap first, stunning players and inflicting Bleed
- Single Charge: Opposite of previous, move before spike stun
- Gobslash Slicetops: 🔗 Main tank tethered and Prey marked, off-tank provoke or main tank invuln. Avoid being in front.

Phase 2
- Trap: ❄️ Ice turrets, boss uses Discharge AoEs in center, move in after to avoid turrets
- Gobsnick Leghops: 👾 Clones spawn, stop attacking boss during Extreme Caution debuff
- Single Charge: 🔴 Donut AoE, follow boss in. Off-tank grabs clone.
- Separate boss and clone to avoid damage up tether buff.
- Gobswish Spraymops: 💥 Massive AoE from clone with small safe spot behind, burn clone quickly
- Trap: ⚰️ Wrecking balls and Discharge edge AoEs, stand under balls then move to safe zones
- Double Charge: 🥊🥊 In and out mechanics in random order
- Boss leaps, uses Single Charge or Gobsnick Leghops. Changes tactics at 60% HP.

Phase 3
- Boss leaps to one end, summons Gob Press R-VI that charges with Steamroller from other end
- Healers get Brighteyes, dropping fire puddles and getting Magic Vuln Up debuff, pass to others
- Illuminati Handcanon: Dodge AoEs
- Gobsway Rumblerocks: Healers be alert
- Kill add before running out of room.

Phase 4
- Goblin Rush and Gobrush Rush Gob
- Triple Charge: Out > Spread > In or In > Out > Stack
- Boss leaps leaving clone, Single Charge. Pull clone away and burn.
- Gobspin Zoomdrops: 🔗 Boss tethers player and spins towards them. Main tank lead to ice trap to stop.
- Gobsplice Mooncrops: 💥 Heavy damage and knockback, use spike trap to avoid knockback
- Boss leaps, Single Charge or Gobsnick Leghops.
- At 30%, wall saws divide arena, dodge them.
- Illuminati Handcanon, Brighteyes, point-blank Single Charge
- Gobslash Slicetops, ice turrets, wrecking balls
- 3 adds (Hedgeknight, 2x Goblin of Fortune) with Vuln Down buff, use wrecking balls to remove then burn
- Gobsway Rumblerocks during adds
- Wall saws return, avoid
- Triple Charge with 3 random attacks
- Phase repeats until boss defeated or enrage wipes party.
