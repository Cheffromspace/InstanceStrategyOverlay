Phantom Train
- 🚫 Ghost Block: Avoid impassable ghost obstacles in the center.
- 💥 Head-On Collision: Brace for unavoidable damage and knockback.
- Spotlights #1: 🔦 Static spotlights destroy ghosts.
- Spotlights #2: 🔦 🎯 Moving spotlights follow marked players, destroying ghosts.
- Diabolical Headlamp: ⚡️ 🤝 Line up to share stack marker damage.
- Diabolical Whistle #1: 🔗 Ghosts tether to players. Use spotlights to destroy them.
- Passenger Car: Micro-arena to fight ghosts or Siegfried if whisked away.
- Doom Strike: 🛡️ Tankbuster on main target.
- Diabolical Wind: 💨 🎯 Marked players inflict damage to overlapped players.
- Diabolical Whistle #2: 🚂 Ghosts whisk away collided players.
- Diabolical Light: 💥 Proximity markers inflict unavoidable damage.
- Acid Rain: 💥 Raidwide damage.

Add Phase
- 👾 📦 Defeat adds when Ghost Train arrives.
- 🚂 Board Ghost Train's roof and destroy Doom Chimney.
- 💨 Throttle: Unavoidable smog forces players to get whisked away.
- 🚃 🏃 Intentionally get whisked to Passenger Car to survive.

After the add phase, mechanics from earlier in the fight will repeat with some overlapping variations.
