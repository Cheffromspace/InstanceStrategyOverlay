Catastrophe
- Many mechanics only affect you if floating. Use Duty Action to float, which costs an oGCD.
- Assign each tank/healer and DPS a main cardinal direction (N/E/S/W). One tank/healer and one DPS at each.
- Enrage at 10:45, requiring ~20500 raid DPS.
- Antilight: 🔴💥 Stand in safe spots to avoid blue (ground) and yellow (air) sphere damage.
- Antilight Yellow: 💥 Floating players are not safe. Do not float until ground mechanic resolves.
- Antilight Blue and Yellow: 💥 Middle is safe for both grounded and floating players. For the first, only ground middle is safe.
- Tremblor: 💥 Small raidwide AoE indicating Earthquake is coming. Happens twice.
- Earthquake: 🏃 Instantly kills anyone on the ground when castbar resolves.
- 100Gs: 🎯 Top 2 enmity players forced to ground by blue circles. Always stack to ensure everyone reaches the ground.
- Gravitational Wave: 💥 Magical raid buster AoE. Mitigate heavily.
- Gravitational Manipulation: 🤝 One tank/healer and one DPS forced to float or ground, with stack AoE requiring 4 players each.
- Aetherial Rift: 🤢 Grounded players get 6 Fulms Under debuff. Float to remove or die when it resolves.
- Paranormal Wave: 🛡️ Tankbuster cleave on MT inflicting Gradual Petrification debuff. Swap after 2nd hit.
- Evilsphere: 🛡️ High damage magical tankbuster. Mitigate heavily.
- Maniacal Probe: 👾🏃 Marks tanks/healers or DPS with tentacles that later indicate Earthquake locations.
- Epicenter: 👾🏃 Edge where first tentacle stops glowing is safe from Earthquake.
- Death's Gaze: 🚫 Look away when castbar resolves to avoid instant death.
- -100Gs: 💥 Stand at N/S arena edges to avoid Antilight Blue/Yellow in center. Marks players with AoE.
- Unstable Gravity: 🎯💥 4 players marked, emitting large AoE damage when resolved. Position at cardinal points.
- Long Drop: 💨🤝 Stack marker knocking back floating players. Grounded are not knocked back.
