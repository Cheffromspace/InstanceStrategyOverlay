Antithesis Manifest: Voidwalker
It is possible to fall off the edge of the arena during the entire encounter. Fallen players can be raised after a moderate delay.

Phase 1
- Doomvoid Guillotine: ➡️⬅️ Massive line AoE through Voidwalker. 🏃 Avoid the line and the Nyx Demons that spawn along it.
- Doomvoid Slicer: 🔴 Donut AoE around Voidwalker. 🏃 Move into Voidwalker's hitbox to avoid the donut and the Nyx Demons that spawn.
- Shadowflame: 🛡️ Tank buster that hits both tanks simultaneously. Tanks should mitigate.
- Dark Fire III: 💥 Large AoE markers on 4 random players. 🏃<>🏃 Marked players spread out to avoid damaging others.
- Unholy Darkness: ⭕ Stack marker on a random player. 🤝 Group together to share the damage.

Phase 2
- Spell In Waiting: ⏳ The next ability cast will be delayed and gain a countdown timer.
- Dark Fire III (In Waiting) + Unholy Darkness (In Waiting): 💥⭕ Delayed AoE markers and stack marker. 💠 Marked players spread, others stack when the timers complete.
- Entropy: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide damage. Heal and mitigate.
- Hand of Erebos: 💨 Spawns on the edge of the arena and knocks players back. 🏃 Move close to the hand or use knockback immunity.
- Punishing Rays: 🌊 Pulsating pools appear around the arena. 🚶‍♂️ Stand in the pools to seal them and avoid raid damage, but beware the bleed.
- Shadow Eye: 👀 Places a look-away marker on a player. 🙈 Look away when it activates to avoid Petrification.
- Shadow Eye (In Waiting): 👀⏳ Delayed look-away tethers a player, activating during other mechanics like Doomvoid Slicer.

The mechanics will repeat until Voidwalker is defeated. Prioritize avoiding failed mechanics while executing the strategy for new mechanics as they appear.
