Mythic Creation: The Hippokampos
- Arena surrounded by instant-kill barrier. Assign "light parties" for stack mechanics.

Phase 1
- Murky Depths: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide AoE. Heal through.
- Doubled Impact: 🛡️ Shared tankbuster. Both tanks use mitigation.
- Sewage Deluge: 💥 Heavy raidwide AoE that floods arena. Mitigate and heal. Stand on platforms/grates. Avoid 🌊 water (inflicts 🤢 Dropsy debuff).
- Spoken Cataract/Winged Cataract: 🔴 Half-room AoE from head, ➡️⬅️ large line AoE from body. Avoid based on boss positioning.
- Coherence: 🔗 Boss tethers to player. Tank grabs tether, group stacks behind boss for split-damage line AoE.
- Ominous Bubbling: 🤝 Healers marked for stack, applies 🤢 Water Resistance Down II. Light parties stack.
- Shockwave: 🏃 Boss leaps to platform. Light party stack to absorb knockback. Use knockback resist or position on grates.
- Predatory Avarice: 3 players get 🔴 Mark of the Depths (large water AoE share) or 💨 Mark of the Tides (point-blank AoE knockback). Position accordingly.

Phase 2
- Channeling Overflow: Players get directional debuffs. Align to crash into opposite player, avoid walls. Use platform edges.
- Kampeos Harma: 🔗 Players tethered to boss get red/blue orbs. Reds (1-4) between platforms for head AoEs, blues (1-4) on opposite platforms for body leaps. Swap positions after each hit.
- Tainted Flood: During Channeling Overflow, some get 🔴 point-blank AoEs applying 🤢 Water Resistance Down II. Position on grates or inner platform corners.

Phase 3
- Dissociation: ➡️⬅️ Head fires line AoE through half arena. Move to safe side.
- Sewage Eruption: 3 sets of 🔴 AoEs under all players. Bait as group on head's side, then move.
- Tainted Flood: 🤢 Debuff requiring 🏃<>🏃 spread.
- Coherence and Doubled Impact: 🔗 Boss tethers to player. Solo tank tankbuster with invuln. Group stacks opposite.
- Channeling Flow: Similar to Channeling Overflow with Coherence. Stack behind boss after resolving debuffs.
- Ominous Bubbling and Shockwave: Repeated from earlier.
- ⏳ Enrage: Boss slow casts Sewage Deluge if not defeated in time.
