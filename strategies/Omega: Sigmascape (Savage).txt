<span class="note">Omega: Sigmascape (Savage)</span>
- 2nd section of Omega (Savage) raid in Stormblood
- Released on January 30, 2018
- Requires item level 340+ to enter
- Access via Party Finder or Duty Finder → Raids → Savage Raids (Stormblood)
- Can enter solo or with < 8 players using Unrestricted Party
- Unlock: Complete quest "Test World of Ruin", then interact with Magitek Terminal in Rhalgr's Reach (X:13.5 Y:12.4)

<span class="note">Floors</span>:
1. Sigmascape V1.0 (Savage) - Boss: <span class="boss">Phantom Train</span>
2. Sigmascape V2.0 (Savage) - Boss: <span class="boss">Demon Chadarnook</span>
3. Sigmascape V3.0 (Savage) - Boss: <span class="boss">Guardian</span>  
4. Sigmascape V4.0 (Savage) - Bosses: <span class="boss">Kefka (Phase 1)</span>, <span class="boss">Kefka (Phase 2)</span>

<span class="note">Item level restrictions do not apply for full/unrestricted parties.</span>
