Kaliya
Phase 1 - 100% - 60%
- Resonate: ➡️⬅️ Cleave attack, tanks share damage.
- Nerve Gas: 💥 📐 Three ground AoE patterns (cone + 2 semi-circles). Dodge left/right based on safe spots.
- Barofield: 💥 Damage + 🤢 Vulnerability Up + ⚖️ Heavy debuff if stepped in.
- Seed of the Sea: 🤝 Stack on player with red line.
- Seed of the River: 🏃‍♂️🏃‍♀️ Spread to avoid missiles on 2 random players.
- Watch for boss animation to determine Seed order (stack or spread first).
- Secondary Head: 🛡️ Tank swap mechanic, applies Vulnerability Up and stun.
- Primary Head: 💥💥💥 High damage to tank, use mitigation and shields.

Phase 2 - Adds - 2:30
- Three adds: Gravity Node (👾🟫 Cube), Weapons Node (👾🔴 Sphere), Electric Node (👾🥚 Egg).
- 🔗 Tether between nodes causes party wipe if they merge. Tanks separate Gravity and Weapons, avoid Electric.
- Gravity Node: 50% less physical damage, Void Zones 💥 Damage + ⏱️ Slow.
- Weapons Node: 50% less magical damage, Repelling Cannons 💥 Shared damage, stack behind it.
- Electric Node: Forked Lightning ⚡ 🤢 Paralysis, only damageable in melee range.
- Focus Weapons (3 DPS) and Gravity (1 caster) simultaneously, then Electric. Let group know HP %.
- Nerve Cloud: 💥 Raidwide damage, use mitigation.

Phase 3 - 59% - 0%
- All Phase 1 mechanics + Nanospore Jet: 🔗 Tether pairs must stay together and apart from other pair.
- Nerve Gas always starts with cone (any target) then semi-circles. Tethers run same direction to dodge.
- Seed of the Sea: Tethers don't move, non-tethers stack. Seed of the River: One tether moves to spread.
- Energy Field: 💥 Melee range required to damage boss.
- Nerve Cloud: 💥 Raidwide damage, mitigate. Fourth use is a wipe.

Solo Strategy
- 🗡️ Clear trash and energy fields. 💀 Suicide to open last barrier.
- 🏃 Avoid boss hitbox debuffs. 🎯 Kill Gravity Node first.
