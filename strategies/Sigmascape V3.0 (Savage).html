Guardian
- Avoid outer arena walls to prevent Paralysis debuff. Watch monitors for visual hints on upcoming mechanics.
- Magitek Ray: ➡️⬅️ Straight-line AoE attack inflicting damage and Vulnerability Up debuff. Avoid the line.
- Arm & Hammer: 🛡️ Tank buster attack. Tanks use defensive cooldowns.
- Atomic Ray: 🎯 Marks damage dealers with explosions leaving pulsing AoE orbs. Avoid the orbs.
- Prey Missile: 🎯 Marks a damage dealer for moderate damage and a flaming debuff. Spread out.
- Missile Simulation: 🚀 Missiles travel across the arena. Avoid the missiles.
- Diffractive Plasma: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide AoE damage. Heal through the damage.

Programs
- Load: 📺 Displays a program and unleashes an opening attack.
- Run: 👾 Summons an add based on the program.
- Skip: ⏭️ Skips the displayed program and runs the one on the small screen.
- Retrieve: ⏪ Retrieves and loads a previously skipped program.
- Copy: 📋 Makes a copy of the current program to paste later.
- Paste: 📎 Utilizes a previously copied program.

Program: Dadaluma
- Load: 💨 Knockback shockwave, Missile Simulation, Chakra Burst (☄️ meteors requiring players in circles).
- Run: 👾 Summons Dadaluma add that uses Aura Cannon (➡️⬅️ line AoE). Avoid the line AoE.

Program: Ultros
- Load: 🎨 Unavoidable ink AoEs, Tentacle Simulation (📐 column AoEs from tentacles).
- Run: 👾 Summons Ultros add that casts Stoneskin (interrupt required), Air Raid and Chain Cannon baited to a corner, Main Cannon 💠 proximity marker. Interrupt Stoneskin, bait corner AoEs, spread for proximity marker.

Program: Air Force
- Load: ⭕ Point-blank AoE. Avoid point-blank AoE.
- Run: 👾 Summons Air Force adds tethered to damage dealers, using Diffractive Laser (📐 cone AoEs), Bomb Deployment (💣 defuse red/blue wires). Avoid cone AoEs, defuse bombs.

Program: Bibliotaph
- Load: ⭕ Summoning circles requiring players to stand in them.
- Run: 👾 Summons Bibliotaph add that uses Thrub (💥 Tank buster). Tanks use defensive cooldowns.

Phase 2: Virus Combat Program
- Positive/Negative Charge: 🔆🔴 Raid split into + and - charges, tethered in pairs. Stay with tethered partner.
- Searing Wind: 🌪️ AoEs from opposite charge pairs (tank/healer, damage dealers). Avoid AoEs from opposite charge.
- Abandonment: 💔 Like charge pairs must stay together to avoid Hysteria debuff. Stay with like charge partner.
- Aether Rot: 💥 Transferable exploding debuff passed between players. Pass debuff to avoid explosion.
- Viral Weapon: 🎯 Partial control with rotating pointer during mechanics. Watch pointer and handle mechanics.
- Diffractive Radar: 💥 Unavoidable damage and Paralysis for moving during scan. Don't move during scan.

Phase 3: Paste Program
- Pastes a previously copied program (Air Force or Ultros).
- Then Dadaluma > Bibliotaph or Bibliotaph > Dadaluma programs.
- Finally, the remaining program (Air Force or Ultros).
- Mechanics overlap chaotically. Handle each mechanic carefully.
- Extermination Sequence: ⏳ Enrage.
