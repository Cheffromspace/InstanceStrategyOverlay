Halicarnassus
- Ribbit: 📐 🐸 Frontal cone AOE that turns players into frogs. Get behind the boss to avoid.
- Blizzard Blade: 🔴 🥶 Circular AOE that freezes players. Get out of the AOE.
- Thunder Blade: ➡️⬅️ 🌩️ Line AOE attack. Avoid the line.
- Fire Blade: 🍩 🔥 Donut-shaped AOE. Stay in the center or far outside.
- Holy Blade: 🤝 🏃<>🏃 🕊️ Marks players with spread or stack markers. Spread or stack as marked.
- The Game: 💠 🎲 Floor becomes role icons or frog icons. Stand on your role icon, or get hit by Ribbit and stand on frog icons.
- Dark Piece: 👾 🖤 Summons untargetable adds that make AOEs. Avoid cone attacks and stand on blue tiles when arena changes.
- Place Piece: 👾 🐉 Summons a dragon add. Off-tank picks it up, faces it away, and DPS stand on blue tiles.
- Forced March, Left Face, Right Face: 🏃 🚶‍♂️ Forces movement. Position appropriately for mechanics like stacks or line attacks.
- Later, Forced March is paired with a Holy Blade stack. Stack behind boss, then scatter left/right after it detonates to avoid Thunder Blade.
- Aetherial Tear: 🌀 Transports players to a sand arena. Walk through sand tiles to reach the tear.
