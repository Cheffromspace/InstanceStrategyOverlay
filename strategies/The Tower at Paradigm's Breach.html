Knave of Hearts
- Roar: 💥 Raid-wide damage at start.
- Colossal Impact: ➡️⬅️ Linear AOE attack with two variants. 🏃 Avoid the line AoE.
- Magic Artillery Beta: 💥 Splashing tankbuster on each alliance's tank. 🛡️ Tanks use mitigation.
- Replicate: 👾 Another boss appears on left or right side. First time always one clone, afterwards may be two.
- Stacking the Deck: 🔗 Boss tethers clones and marks attack order. Actual boss always goes first.
- Spheroids: 🔴 Orbs spit red balls that cause vulnerability stacks. 🏃 Avoid red balls.
- Magic Artillery Alpha: ⭕ AoE markers on players. Spheroids cast line AOEs across arena. 🏃 Avoid first cast, move into it to avoid second.
- Light Leap: ⭕⭕ Two proximity AoEs on left and right of boss. 🏃 Run close to boss for uptime. Boxes appear at AoE locations.
- Lunge: 💨 Knockback attack after Light Leap. 🛡️ Line up with white boxes to be knocked into them safely.

Hansel & Gretel
- Upgraded Shield: 🛡️ Creates shield around caster's rear and flanks. Boss with lower HP always uses this.
- Upgraded Lance: 🌟 Increases caster's damage dealt.
- Keep bosses apart to prevent tethering and gaining both buffs. Point shielded boss toward party for damage uptime.
- Wail: 💥 Raid-wide AOE.
- Crippling Blow: 💥 Tankbuster.
- Tandem Assault: Bloody Sweep: 📐 Damage on arena sides based on halberd positions. 🏃 Stand furthest from bosses if halberds inside, between bosses if outside.
- Seed of Magic Alpha: ⭕⭕⭕⭕ Targeted circle AoEs on four random players.
- Riot of Magic: 🔴 Stack marker on a fifth random player. 🤝 Stack for shared damage.
- Tandem Assault: Passing Lance: 🔥🔥🔥 Line of fireballs with gap. Bosses charge in wide line AoE. 🏃 Find and stand in fireball gap.
- Wandering Trail: 💫 Yellow spinning discs appear on arena. May move to center and back.
- Breakthrough: 🛡️🏹 Bosses charge into center from edges. Proximity AoE + extreme damage on halberd side. 🏃 Avoid charge path and halberd side.
- Seed of Magic Beta: ⭕⭕⭕⭕⭕⭕ Circle AoEs below six random players.
- Hungry Lance: 📐📐 Bosses in center launch wide cone AoEs in opposite directions.
- Lamentation: 💥 High damage to entire alliance. Cast repeatedly when one boss dies.

Red Girl
Part 1:
- Cruelty: 💥 Heavy raid-wide damage. Cast throughout fight.
- Kill zones appear/disappear around arena edge and middle. 🏃 Move out quickly if caught.
- Shockwave: ⭕⚫⚪ AOE markers on players, white or black. Ring shows detonation time. Don't stack markers.
- Generate: Barrier: ⚪⚫ White and black barriers form cross on arena. Players with Shockwave destroy barriers of opposite color.
- Ground AOEs (white/black) also destroy barriers. Variant makes box trapping players inside.
- Generate: Barrier: ⚪➡️⚫⬅️ Line of arrows on arena side. Position with barrier of arrow color between you and arrow.
- Recreate Meteor: ☄️ Meteor in box center. Position with same color barrier between you and meteor.
- Manipulate Energy: 💥 Splashing tankbusters on all alliance tanks.
- Replicate: 👾 Boss creates regular sized red girls around arena. Stick behind one and rotate with her.
- Sublime Transcendence: 💥 Cruelty plus phase change. Heal quickly before intermission.

Intermission:
- 🖥️ Hacking minigame from NiER: Automata.
- Be opposite color of barriers/pylons to damage them. Match color of pulsing ground AOE to avoid damage.
- Destroy pylons to damage Red Sphere.

Part 2:
- Child's Play: 🔗 Tethered players subjected to Forced March. 🏃 Follow arrow direction. Avoid AOEs after release.
- Shockwave: ⭕ Targeted AoEs on tethered and untethered players. Color irrelevant.
- Generate: Barrier: ⚪➡️⬅️⚫ Arrows appear in both colors/directions. Cast twice, arrows perpendicular on second.
- Recreate Meteor: ☄️⚫☄️⚪ Two meteors, one black and one white. Find safe spot with black/white barriers blocking respective meteors.

Xun-Zi & Meng-Zi
