Zodiark:
No set positioning, follow marked player. Can fall off arena edge.
Phase 1:
- Kokytos: 💥 Raidwide AoE, HP to 1, 🤝 stack for heals
- Paradeigma: 👾 Adds (Behemoths, Snakes, Quetzalcoatl), avoid AoEs
- Styx: 🤝 Stack for 6 hits, mitigates damage
- Ania: 🛡️ Tankbuster + 🤢 Vuln Up, ⚔ tank swap
- Exoterikos: Colored 📐 cone or 🔲 half arena AoE
- Algedon: Boss charges corner to corner, 💨 knockback
- Phobos: 💥 Raidwide AoE + 🤢 Bleed
- Astral Flow: 🔄 Rotates stage 90°, adds rotate but not Exoterikos runes
- Adikia: Massive 🔴 AoEs on E/W sides, small safe zones N/S
Phase 2:
- Defeat 4 Roiling Darkness orbs before boss gauge fills
- Esoteric Pattern: Avoid 📐 cone, ➡️⬅️ column, and 🔲 half arena AoEs
- Apomnemoneumata: Heavy damage if orbs defeated, wipe if not
Phase 3:
- Astral Eclipse: Star patterns indicate safe zones from meteors (3x3 grid)
- Triple Esoteric Ray: Move to safe zone, then dodge second blast
Phase 4:
- Fire wall spawns diagonally, 🔄 rotates with stage, avoid standing in path
- Paradeigma + Astral Flow: Position based on add and fire wall rotation
- Ania, Exoterikos, Algedon combos force movement
- Styx now hits 7-8 times, 🤝 stack and heal
- Astral Eclipse into Algedon, move to safe corner
- Phlegethon: Dodge 3 sequential 🎯 AoEs
- Phobos: 💥 Raidwide AoE + 🤢 Bleed
- Enrage: Defeat boss before final Apomnemoneumata cast
