Omega

Opening Phase
- Atomic Ray: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage.
- Mustard Bomb: 🛡️ Vicious tankbuster on Omega's primary target. Tanks should ensure non-tanks are not stacked on them.
- Ballistic Impact: 🔴 Marks all players with AoE circles. 🏃<>🏃 Spread out to avoid overlap. Leaves behind fire puddles inflicting Burns debuff.
- Flame Thrower: 📐 Spawns eight evenly-distanced cone attacks. 🏃 Avoid the cones. Happens simultaneously with Ballistic Impact.
- Wave Cannon: ➡️⬅️ Two variations, Larboard (left) and Starboard (right). 💠 Safe spots differ for each variation. Omega will spin to face the opposite direction for a second Wave Cannon.
- Peripheral Synthesis: 👾 Creates a wall of Rocket Punch adds across the arena. 🏃 Dodge the adds. Use Omega Jammer duty action to damage adds.

Add Phase
- Omega exits the arena at 70% HP, leaving behind deadly electro-puddles.
- Level Checker spawns and casts Executables inflicting Looper 🤢 debuff.
- Executable #1: 🔴 Spawns circles to stand in and dispel Looper. 💠 Each player must occupy a circle.
- Executable #2: 🔴 Spawns point-blank AoE on Level Checker, then 4 circles inside and ring AoE outside. 🏃💠 Dodge point-blank, run in for circles, 2 players per.
- Force Quit: 💥 DPS check to defeat the Level Checker.

Delta Attack Phase
- Use Omega Jammer on Conductive Focus add to build ⬆️ Electromagnetic Induction meter to 100% and reduce Delta Attack's damage.

Final Phase
- Omega returns and uses previous mechanics with additions:
- (Improved) Peripheral Synthesis: 👾 Spawns 1-2 giant Rocket Punch adds that launch across the arena. 🏃 Avoid their path.
- Electric Slide: 💥 Stack marker on a random player. 🤝 Stack on marked player to share damage.
- (Improved) Mustard Bomb: 🛡️ Every 2nd cast tethers to a random player. 🏃 Off-tank must grab tether and move away.
- ⭕ Cycles through previous mechanics in various combinations until defeated.
