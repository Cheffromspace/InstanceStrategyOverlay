Heritor of the Whorl: Leviathan
- Players can fall off the arena at any time. Fallen players can be raised after a moderate delay.
- Tidal Roar: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage that requires healing.
- Rip Current: 🛡️ Devastating straight-line tank buster across the arena. Main tank should position at an angle to avoid hitting other players.
- Temporary Current: ➡️⬅️ Diagonal AoE attack from Leviathan's head. Avoid the line AoE. Alternates left and right.
- Drenching Pulse: 🔴 AoE circles under players, followed by AoE markers. Spread out to avoid damage, then stack for follow-up AoEs.
- Tidal Wave: 💨 Pushback from the edge. Run towards the water spout to avoid being knocked off.
- Undersea Quake: 🔳 Shrinks the arena to the center. Stack in the middle to avoid instant death on the sides.
- Crashing Pulse: 🔴 AoE circles under players and 🎯 stack marker. Spread out to avoid AoEs, then stack on marked player.

Phase 2
- Tsunami: 💥 Raid-wide damage and 🤢 debuffs (Surging Tsunami, Smothering Tsunami, Maelstrom)
- Maelstrom: 🌪️ Leviathan disappears, 🔴 AoE puddles grow, and 🎯 dive-bomb attacks. Avoid puddles and dive-bombs.

Phase 3
- Undersea Quake: 💥 Destroys the center, leaving two platforms. Move to the sides to avoid instant death.
- Temporary Current: ➡️⬅️ Two consecutive diagonal AoEs. Move between top and bottom of each platform to avoid.
- After this, Leviathan cycles through previous mechanics until defeated.
