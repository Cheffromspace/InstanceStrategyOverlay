Mythic Creation: Proto-Carbuncle
The arena is surrounded by spikes that will instantly kill players. Failing mechanics applies stacking Vulnerability Up debuff.

Phase 1
- Crunch: 🛡️ Light tank buster.
- Sonic Howl: 💥 Raid-wide damage.
- Ruby Glow: 🔴 Divides room diagonally or into quadrants. Magic abilities hitting walls affect entire half/quadrant.
- Searing Ray: 📐 Boss jumps to corner, charges large 180° AoE. Stand behind boss or opposite side of wall.
- Topaz Stones: 🔴 Exploding stones with telegraphed AoEs. After Ruby Glow, leaves 1-2 safe quadrants.
- Acidic Slaver: 💥 Weak raidwide, stuns party and triggers phase transition cutscene.

Phase 2
Boss retains Phase 1 abilities except Crunch and Acidic Slaver. Arena is larger, surrounded by instant-kill poison.
- Toxic Crunch: 🛡️ Strong tankbuster, 🤢 applies uncleansable Poison debuff. Tank swap or heal through.
- Venom Squall: 🤝 🏃<>🏃 🔴 Grounded AoEs under some players > AoE markers on all players > Stack AoE. Stack, spread, stack.
- Topaz Stones:
-- 🔴 🤢 4 stones: 3 growing poison circles, 1 safe exploding AoE.
-- 🔴 🏃 After Ruby Glow quadrants, exploding stones require moving between safe zones.
-- 🔴 🤢 📐 After Ruby Glow split, 1 half poison stone, 1 exploding. Avoid growing poison, bait reflected Searing Ray.
-- 🔴 🔴 🤢 💠 After Ruby Glow quadrants, 2 exploding, 1 poison, 1 safe. Followed by Venom Squall.
- Starving Stampede: 🎯 🏃 Boss jumps between 5 markers (4 edges clockwise/counterclockwise then center), large AoE at each. Stand at 4th edge marker, move to 1st after jump.
After final Topaz Stone, boss repeats mechanics except Starving Stampede until killed. No apparent enrage.
