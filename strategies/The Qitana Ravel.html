Lozatl
- Stonefist: 🛡️ Tankbuster.
- Sun Toss: 🔴 Circle AoE under a random player.
- Lozatl's Scorn: 💥 One statue glows purple, dealing raid-wide damage to half the arena. 🏃 Move to the other half.
- Lozatl's Fury: 💥 One side of Lozatl glows red, dealing damage to half the arena Lozatl is facing. 🏃 Move to the safe half.
- Eventually combines Lozatl's Scorn and Lozatl's Fury. 🔳 Safe area varies in size. 🏃 Find and stand in the safe area.

Batsquatch
- Ripper Fang: 🛡️ Tankbuster.
- Soundwave: 💥 Moderate raid-wide damage.
- Subsonics: 🦶 Stomps create 🔴 AoEs and falling rocks. 🥊 Damage stacks up. 🗿 Largest AoEs leave pillars.
- Pillars: 💥 If pillars exist during Soundwave, they collapse in a 📐 cone AoE, inflicting 🤢 Vulnerability Up. 🏃 Avoid the cone AoE.

Lightwarden: Eros
- Rend: 🛡️ Tankbuster.
- Hound out of Heaven: 🔗 Tether on a non-tank player. 💥 Eros charges, inflicting 🤢 Vulnerability Up if tether isn't broken. 🏃 Break tether.
- Glossolalia: 💥 Moderate raid-wide damage.
- Viper Poison: 🔴 AoEs leave poison puddles. 🎯 One targets a player. 🏃 Avoid puddles.
- After Viper Poison, Inhale pulls players to Eros's center. 🏃 Move to avoid puddles.
- After Inhale, Heaving Breath 💨 knocks players away. 🏃 Avoid landing in puddles.
- Lightning Breath: ⚡ Side heads fire 📐 cones. 💥 Stack marker on a player. 🤝 Stand in front of Eros.
- Fire Breath: 🔥 Center head 📐 cone. 🔴 AoEs on 2 players. 💠 Stand beside Eros.

During the last trash pull, pulling enemies back can spawn the final gorillas early to save time.
