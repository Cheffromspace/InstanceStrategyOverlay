Rafflesia - Phase 1 (100% - 65%)
- Blighted Bloom: 🚫 Kills anyone who moves during cast. Happens 5 seconds into fight.
- Devour: 🎯 Vacuums towards player with yellow marker. Also does smaller 360 degree vacuum.
- Bulbs: 🔗 Form tether to Rafflesia if too close, giving boss -90% damage debuff. ⚔️ Kite boss away from bulbs.
- Bloody Caress: 💥 Stack marker. 🔄 Tank swap at every stack.
- Chains: 🔗 Tether mechanic. Same as Turn 6.
- Honey and Bees: 👾 Same as Turn 6. Optimally only let 1 bee die and 1 honey get devoured.

Rafflesia - Phase 2 (65% - 0%)
- Leafstorm: 💥 Raid-wide damage. Happens at 65% instead of 40%.
- Acid Rain: 🔴 Same as Turn 6. Tank kites boss away from raid.
- Swarm: 🤢 4 stack debuff on random player. 📖 Spread to reduce to 1 stack. 2+ stacks = heavy damage/death. Every 30s.
- Slugs: 👾 Phase to 50% HP, then kill transformed slug ASAP or wipes raid. All 3 slugs must die. Repeats.
- Chains: 🔗 Same as phase 1. Break tethers.
- Laser: ➡️⬅️ Same as Turn 6.
- Bloody Caress: 💥 Stack marker. 🔄 Tank swap still required.
- Stagger damage sources: Slug AOEs, Chains+Laser, Tank damage, 2+ Swarm stacks. Use Marker A for Swarm spreading.

Phase 2 Rotation: Leafstorm -> Acid Rain -> Swarm -> Slug 1 -> Big Slug 1 -> Chains+Laser -> Swarm -> Slug 2 -> Big Slug 2 -> Slug 3 -> Big Slug 3 -> Chains -> Swarm -> Repeat
