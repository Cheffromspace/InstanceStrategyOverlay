The Manipulator
The Burden of the Father
- Fight has 3 phases, with Phase 3 including all mechanics. Phase 1 and 2 involve destroying the boss's legs.
- Everything the boss does is splash damage, so range and melee DPS need to spread out. Focus legs one at a time.
- Throughout the fight, boss will target players for heavy splash damage, and 1 DPS + off-tank will be "Quarantined" and removed from the fight temporarily.

Phase 1 - Leg #1
- 🔵 Orbs spawn from edges, move to player with blue orb. 🏃 Run into orbs to explode them.
- After leg destroyed, boss becomes targetable briefly before Phase 2

Phase 2 - Leg #2
- 🧡 3 untargetable adds cast ➡️⬅️ orange line AoEs on 1 player each. 🤝 2+ players must intercept lines.
- Jagd Doll: 👾 3 dolls around edge deal AoE, stack 🤢 Luminous Aetheroplasm debuff (4 stacks = death). Kill ASAP.
- After leg destroyed, boss becomes vulnerable again before Phase 3

Phase 3 - The Manipulator
- All previous mechanics except Jagd Dolls and Quarantine. Spread out and heal through high damage.
- Carnage Zero: 💥 High splash damage on random players
- Judgement Nisi: 💥 5 stack debuff on healers, 🔵 one blue 🔴 one red. Healers must 🏃↔️🏃 stay on opposite sides to avoid exchanging (= death).
- Discoid: 🔴 Circular AoEs on 4 random players, then 4 more, then 🛡️ tank takes heavy damage (increases over time)
- Healers keep apart and heal self + tank. Use cooldowns as fight progresses.
