Perfect Imperfection: Hephaistos
- 🔥 Arena surrounded by damaging ring of fire. Players cannot be knocked off.
- 🤢 Failing mechanics or getting hit debuffs players with Vulnerability Up stacks.
- Genesis of Flame: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide AoE.
- Sunforge: 👾 Summons serpent familiar that fires ➡️⬅️ line AoE through center. 👾 Or phoenix that covers sides, leaving center safe.
- Flameviper: 🛡️ High damage tankbuster with ➡️⬅️ line AoEs around tank.
- Reforged Reflection: 🔗 Boss sheds chains, signaling a series of attacks based on freed part.

Gorgon Arm Phase
- Into the Shadows: 👾 Summons underground Gorgons. Don't face them when surfaced to avoid petrify. 💥 Gorgons cast raidwide Gorgoneion.
- Gorgospit: 📐 Cone AoE fired at random player.
- Tanks should grab Gorgons when targetable. Raid must kill them before Gorgoneion finishes casting.
- Ektothermos: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide AoE. Healers be ready.

Fourfold Flames Phase
- Fourfold Flames: 💠 Proximity markers in corners become 🔥 puddles boss 🔗 tethers to.
- Cthonic Vent: 🔥 Boss 🔗 tethers to puddles, summoning 🐍 fire serpents leaping between them causing eruptions. Avoid puddles serpents jump to.

Blazing Legs Phase
- Blazing Footfalls: 👟 Boss leaps to 💨 knockback markers around arena. Position with back to safe spot, knockback mitigation doesn't work.
- Rearing Rampage: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide AoE.

Volcanic Torches Phase
- Volcanic Torches: 🔥 Blue flames trace a path covering most of arena, leaving one safe segment. Move to safe edge based on flame path.
- Hemitheos's Flare: 🎯 Each player marked with 💥 exploding AoE. Spread to safe floor segment.

Fight loops with added mechanics:
- Gorgon Arm also summons 4 Illusory Hephaistos casting Gorgospit from corners. Stay center.
- Cthonic Vent serpents jump 3 times through puddles.
- Blazing Legs adds a 3rd knockback marker.
