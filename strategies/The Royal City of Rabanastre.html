Mateus, The Corrupt
- Kill Ice Azers before they detonate and leave damaging ice puddles.
- Unbind -> White Whisper: Gather on alliance marker. Pop Aqua Spheres before ice dancer reaches them. Collect blue orbs, avoid ice track. Destroy any Blizzard III pillars or players will turn to snowmen. Requires coordination to handle mechanics.
- Rebind -> Dualcast -> Blizzard III + Blizzard IV: Find safe spot during proximity AoEs. Healers prepare AoE heals for incoming damage. Mitigate damage and handle mechanics.
- Suffocation Phase: Kill Flume Toads ASAP. Run through air pockets they drop to remove Breathless debuff stacks. Keep line AoEs away from air pockets. Manage debuff stacks and adds.
- Ultimate Phase: Avoid tethered ice orbs and their cone AoEs. Kill Ice Azers. Tank Azure Guards apart to prevent tethering. Defeat guards before Aether bar fills. Manage multiple mechanics and adds simultaneously.

Rofocale
- Phase 1: Avoid Rofocale copy charges. Safe spots on far sides. Repeats at 75% and 55% HP.
- Phase 2: Kill your group's Archaeodemon add. Adds shield their area. Avoid Rofocale circling arena (damage + knockback). Aetherial Acceleration builds while adds up. Wipe at 100. Run to sides for Heavenly Subjugation at phase end. Coordinate add kills to minimize Acceleration.
- Phase 3: Trample now followed by Cry of Victory. Avoid Embrace tendril AoEs. For Dark Geas, activate a Dark Circle and survive bleeding debuff. Manage new mechanics and abilities.
- Avoid AoEs, kill adds, activate mechanics, survive ultimate. Starts repeating abilities after phase 3.

Argath Thadalfus
- Three phases: Pre-Emptiness, Emptiness, Post-Emptiness. Boss has divine (white) or demon (dark) face.
- Divine face: Do as commanded. Demon face: Do opposite of command.
- Divine Command: Turn - Divine: Face away. Demon: Don't turn.
- Divine Command: Flee - Divine: Keep moving/attacking. Demon: Stop moving/attacking.
- Failure inflicts damage and Unnerved debuff. 3 stacks = Crave (run as a chicken).
- Emptiness: Destroy Shards of Emptiness before meter fills to avoid wipe.
- Avoid outer edge of arena when glowing or gain deadly Transfiguration debuff.
- Phase 3: After 2nd Gnawing Dread, small safe zone appears. Run in arrow direction only.
- Manage positioning, adds, debuffs, and boss abilities. Divine/demon mechanic is key.
