Manor Claviger:
- Sweet Steel: 📐 Untelegraphed cone AoE, face boss away from party
- Void Fire II: 🚫 Interruptible AoE spell, typically targets ranged players
- Dark Mist: 💥 Large PBAoE, can be avoided by stunning boss

Manor Jester and Manor Steward:
- Focus Jester first, less HP and more damage
- Ice Spikes (Jester): 🚫 Interruptible spell
- Soul Drain (Steward): 💥 Large PBAoE, can be avoided by stunning boss

Lady Amandine:
- Void Fire III and Void Thunder III: 🚫 Interruptible spells
- Dark Mist: 💥 Large PBAoE, can be avoided by stunning boss
- Seduced debuff: 🏃 Slowly walk towards boss, turn around quickly to avoid AoE
- Can summon Manor Sentry and Lady's Handmaidens

Collect keys to progress: 🟩 Green Key, 🟨 Yellow Key, 📜 Bloody Parchment
