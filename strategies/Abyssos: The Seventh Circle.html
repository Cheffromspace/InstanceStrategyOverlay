Hemitheos: Agdistis
- 🚫 No arena border, getting hit inflicts Vulnerability Up stacks.
- Bough of Attis: 📐 ➡️⬅️ Avoid front/back AoEs and sweeping hand knockback. Move to the front of the opposite hand, past the arrows.
- Hemitheos's Holy: 🔴 🏃<>🏃 Spread out to avoid overlapping AoEs, then move to center.
- Hemitheos's Glare III: 🤝 Stack in the center safe zone.
- Immortal's Obol: 💥 Stand in safe zones (NW, NE, S) to avoid arena destruction. Move when "The shape of the platform changes!" appears.
- Hemitheos's Aero II: 🛡️ Tankbuster Tanks move to different platforms. Mitigation and healing required.
- Spark of Life: 🤢 🩹 Unavoidable roomwide AoE that inflicts Bleed. Heal through it.
- Forbidden Fruit: 👾 🔴 Avoid add AoEs. Immature Io: move to bridges. Immature Stymphalide: move to platform edges.
- Blades of Attis: 🔴 🏃 Move out of moving AoE markers that go across platforms. After 2nd set, move to originating edge.
- Burst: 💥 💠 Stand in meteor markers (1 NE, 1 NW, 2 S). At least one player per circle or massive damage/wipe.
- Hemitheos's Aero IV: 💨 Massive knockback on one platform. Use knockback resistance or align with bridges. Will still take damage and debuff.

Fight repeats mechanics, except Aero IV is replaced with an Immature Io + Immature Stymphalide combo and Bough on southern platform. Manage adds, avoid AoEs, and heal through damage until boss is defeated.
