Imdugud
- Prey: 🎯 Red marker on player. Healers shield marked player to prevent damage and debuff.
- Wild Charge: 🔵 Blue marker on player. Marked player runs behind south marker, others 💥 stack on ground marker to share damage.
- Heat Lightning: 💥 Targets 3 players with AoE damage and Vulnerability Up debuff. Affected players stand still, others stack for Wild Charge.
- Critical Rip: 🛡️ High damage tank buster. Use defensive cooldowns.
- Crackle Hiss: 📐 Frontal cone attack. Only tank should get hit.
- Spike Flail: 💥 Cone AoE from tail. Avoid standing behind boss/adds. Can be used to give healers breathing room.

Phase 1 (100% - 83%)
- Introduces Prey and Wild Charge mechanics. Practice positioning for later phases.

Phase 2 (83% - 83%)
- Electrocharge: 🌟 Makes Imdugud untargetable. Spawns 4 adds (2 Sons, 2 Daughters).
- Split adds between tanks. Focus Daughters first. Sons leave damaging ground effect on death.
- After adds die, Electric Burst 💥 deals damage based on Electrocharge stacks.

Phase 3 (83% - 54%)
- Introduces Heat Lightning with Prey and Wild Charge.
- Spread to avoid overlapping AoEs from Heat Lightning. Handle Wild Charge as before.

Phase 4 (54% - 54%)
- Similar to Phase 2 but with 2 additional adds spawning on west side after 20s. DPS check.
- Use caster LB3 on initial 4 adds. Burn west adds before new ones spawn. Then handle east adds.

Phase 5 (54% - 0%)
- Introduces Cyclonic Chaos: 🔗 Tether on random player. 💥 Tethered player moves to center, others stack on them.
- Arena space shrinks over time. Maintain high DPS to avoid enrage.
- Hardest mechanic is Wild Charge + Cyclonic Chaos. Coordinate using numbered markers.
