Quickthinx Allthoughts
- This fight has multiple phases with various mechanics that require careful attention.
- ⏳ Hard enrage timer of 12 minutes 30 seconds. Push DPS to meet checks.
- The boss gains a Highwire buff for each player death, significantly increasing damage. Avoid deaths.
- Before the fight, assign positions for mechanics and mark a spot to pass prey markers.

Phase 1
- Corporal Punishment: 🎯 Two random players targeted with large AoEs twice. Spread out.
- Sizzlespark: 🔴 Raid-wide damage and Lightning Resistance Down debuff. Mitigate and heal.
- Sizzlebeam: ➡️⬅️ Line AoE marks a player with Electrocution DoT. Move away from others.
- Bombs: 💥 All players tethered to a bomb. Move to assigned spots to avoid overlap. High damage and Magic Vulnerability Up if hit.
- Jails: 🎯 Pass tethers and prey markers to correct roles based on bomb drop. Avoid boss' Zoomdoom AoE.
- Red Jail: Caster DPS. Break padlock ASAP. Purple Jail: Melee DPS. White Jail: Tank. Green Jail: Healer.
- Handle Panzer Doll and Sturm Doll adds. Stun Sturm Doll's Kugelblitz cast.

Phase 2
- Uplander Doom: 🛡️ Multi-hit tankbuster with Physical Vulnerability Up. Swap after debuff.
- Avoid Weight of the World 🔴 balls. Keep boss away from Shanoa the Cat add.
- Flamethrower: 📐 Targets random healer with line AoE and Searing Wind knockback and damage pulse debuff. Healer stays away.
- Slow and kill True Heart add before it reaches boss or wipe.

Phase 3
- Handle Jails again based on tells. Burn adds and break caster jail padlock.
- Prepare for Sizzlebeam ➡️⬅️ and Sizzlespark 🔴 spam on player with Electrocution DoT.
- Uplander Doom 🛡️ tankbuster. Swap tanks. More Sizzlesparks and Sizzlebeam.
- Bombs: 💥 Move to assigned positions to avoid overlap.

Phase 4
- Uplander Doom 🛡️ 5-hit tankbuster then Flamethrower 📐 on healer.
- Move to small corner platforms. Healer with Searing Wind debuff on their own platform.
- Dodge Corporal Punishment 🎯 AoEs on platforms. Heavy raid damage, keep healed.

Phase 5
- Similar to Phase 2 with Flamethrower 📐, True Hearts, Weight of the World 🔴 balls and Shanoa add.
- Additional Sizzlebeam ➡️⬅️ and triple Sizzlespark 🔴 at end. Heavy damage.

Phase 6
- Final Jails. Burn adds and jail padlock based on tells.
- Sizzlebeam ➡️⬅️ then triple Sizzlespark 🔴. Huge damage on Sizzlebeam target.
- Defeat boss before enraged 10s Sizzlespark cast or wipe.
