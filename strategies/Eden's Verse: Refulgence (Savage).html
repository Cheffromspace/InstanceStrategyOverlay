Heritor of Frost: Shiva
- 💥 Aggressive difficulty raid encounter against Shiva.
- Players can fall off the arena edge to their doom throughout the fight.

Phase 1
- Absolute Zero: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage followed by Mirror, Mirror.
- Mirror, Mirror: 🔮 Summons mirrors reflecting Driving Frost (📐 cone behind Shiva) or Biting Frost (📐 cone in front) with delays based on mirror colors. 🏃 Avoid cones and move between mirrors to dodge reflections.
- Double Slap: 🛡️ Tank buster applying Physical Vulnerability, requiring swap.
- Redress (Holy Form): 👁️ Shiva transforms, 🙈 Look away. Casts Axe Kick (🔴 point-blank AoE) or Scythe Kick (🔴 donut AoE).

Mini-Phase #1 - Diamond Frost
- 🤢 Tank and healer get Freezing debuff requiring Esuna dispel.
- ☄️ Frigid Stone AoE markers on tank, healer, 2 DPS leave exploding star patterns.
- ❄️ Icicles drop around arena edges in a set order.
- Heavenly Strike: 💨 Knockback from center.
- 🔴 Frigid Eruption triple AoEs under unmarked DPS.
- 💠 Coordinate positioning to avoid overlapping mechanics and getting knocked off.

Mini-Phase #2 - Light Rampant
- 💥 Lightsteeped stacking debuff, 5 stacks wipes raid.
- Refulgent Chains: 🔗 Tethers tank, healer, 2 DPS, detonating if too close or far.
- 🛡️ Meteor Towers require soaking to avoid wipes.
- Bright Orbs: 🔴 Tether to unchained players, 🏃 Kite and shrink to pop safely.
- Path of Light: 📐 Shiva fires cones at chained players.
- 💠 Coordinate mechanics while managing Lightsteeped stacks.

Mini-Phase #3 - Shattered World
- 💥 Shiva shatters platform, dividing raid into two groups on separate platforms.
- 👾 Groups slay waves of adds with unique mechanics.
- ⏳ Avoid Flood of Light gauge from filling, grants damage buff if successful.

Phase 2
- Shiva merges with Hraesvelgr, gaining new abilities. Tanks must maintain top 2 enmity.
- Akh Morn: 💥 Blasts tanks with color debuffs, 🤝<>🏃 Raid splits and soaks by color.
- Morn Afah: 🛡️ Shareable tank buster to soak or invuln.
- Mirror, Mirror with color delays and Shiva's abilities:
- Hallowed Wing: 🌊 50% arena AoEs requiring positioning based on mirror delays.
- Banish: 🎯 Real or fake stack markers on DPS based on orbs over Shiva.
- Wyrm's Lament: 💫 Cleanse Wyrmclaw color debuffs by touching Dragon Head and light pools in an order while avoiding mechanics.
- Embittered Dance/Spiteful Dance: 🔄 Shiva's Scythe and Axe Kicks in set orders.

Mini-Phase #4 - Icelit Dragonsong
- Intense mechanic overlap:
- 💥 Lightsteeped stacking debuff
- Refulgent Chains
- 🛡️ Meteor Tower soaking
- ☄️ Frigid Stone exploding star patterns
- 🤢 Freezing debuffs
- Heavenly Strike
- Banish real/fake stack markers
- The House of Light 📐 conal attacks
- 💠 Carefully coordinate and position with limited Lightsteeped stacks.

Final Phase
- Mirror, Mirror into Redress (Ice Form) causing 🛷 Thin Ice sliding, then Redress (Hraesvelgr).
- Wyrm's Lament with two Dragon Heads and frequent Akh Morn/Morn Afah.
- 🚫 Red Mirror Hallowed Wing enrage if not defeated in time.
