Steam Room
- Metal blocks emit steam that deals damage and 💨 knockback
- Use knockback immunities like Arm's Length and Surecast to mitigate

Faust
- 🎯 DPS focus Faust, ignore adds
- 👾 Off tank picks up adds as they spawn and keeps them away from raid
- Kaltstrahl: Faust's ability

Oppressor
- 💠 Tank Oppressor in south side of room
- Fight has two phases

Phase 1
- Gunnery Pod: 💥 Unavoidable AoE, heal through
- Photon Spaser: ➡️⬅️ Large linear AoE, avoid
- Hydrothermal Missile: 🎯 Player with Prey moves away, healers heal them
- Resin Bomb: 🔴 Circular AoEs under players
- Emergency Deployment: 👾 Spawns Alarum adds, kite and kill ASAP. Leave puddle that minimizes
- 🎯 Deal as much damage as possible to Oppressor

Phase 2
- 👾 Second Oppressor 0.5 spawns after 90s, off-tank picks it up north
- 3000-Onze Missile: 👾 Kill Alarum under lasers to reduce damage
- Quick Landing: 💥 Pulsing AoE, move away
- Self Destruct: ⏳ Hard enrage if not killed
- 🎯 Kill both Oppressors at same time or LB one after other dies
