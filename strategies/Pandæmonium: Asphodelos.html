Pandæmonium: Asphodelos
- 1st section of Pandæmonium, the main 8-player raid in Endwalker
- Released on December 21, 2021
- Requires item level 565 or higher to enter
- Can be queued from the Duty Finder once unlocked
- Unlock by completing the level 90 quest chain starting with The Crystal from Beyond in Old Sharlayan (X:9.6 Y:11.9)
- 4 floors, each with a different boss
- No weekly loot restrictions as of patch 6.18

Asphodelos: The First Circle (P1N)
- Boss: Erichthonios
- 2 Unsung Helm, 1 Unsung Armor, 1 Unsung Gauntlets, 1 Unsung Chausses, 1 Unsung Greaves, 2 Unsung Ring tokens

Asphodelos: The Second Circle (P2N)
- Boss: Hippokampos
- 1 Unsung Helm, 1 Unsung Armor, 1 Unsung Gauntlets, 1 Unsung Chausses, 2 Unsung Greaves, 2 Unsung Ring tokens

Asphodelos: The Third Circle (P3N)
- Boss: Phoinix
- 1 Unsung Helm, 2 Unsung Armor, 1 Unsung Gauntlets, 2 Unsung Chausses, 2 Unsung Ring tokens

Asphodelos: The Fourth Circle (P4N)
- Boss: Hesperos
- All Unsung Blade tokens, 2 Unsung Armor, 1 Unsung Gauntlets, 2 Unsung Chausses, 1 Unsung Greaves, 2 Unsung Ring tokens

Item Exchange
- Trade tokens for gear with Mylenie in Labyrinthos (X:8.3 Y:27.6) or Djole in Radz-at-Han (X:10.3 Y:9.6)
