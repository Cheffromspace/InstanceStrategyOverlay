Ephemeral Justice: Themis
- 🏃 Boss has purple or gold rings, requiring players to move close or away respectively
- Eunomia: 💥 Raidwide attack with bleed 🤢
- Divisive Ruling: ➡️⬅️ Large line AoE from center. Move into AoE if purple ring, or away from center if gold ring 🏃
- Dark and Light: 🔴 Creates gold and purple circles on floor for next mechanic
- Sigils of Discord: 🎯 Stand in opposite color circle of your debuff to avoid heavy damage 🤢
- Dineis: 🤝 Stack in your color circle to bait AoEs, then move away 🏃
- Emissary's Will: 💥 Raidwide attack that removes light/dark debuffs
- Dismissal Ruling: 💨 Knockback followed by donut AoE (purple) or pointblank AoE (gold). Knockback immunity works 🔴 💥
- Dike: 🛡️ Dual tankbusters
- 🔴 Light/dark circles reappear, leaving less room to maneuver while combining with previous abilities
- Upheld Ruling: 🔗 If purple, tethered large tankbuster AoE followed by donut AoE. If gold, stack marker followed by boss jump pointblank AoE. Tether cannot be changed/broken 🛡️ 💥 🤝
- Styx: 🤝 Multihit group stack
- Dineis into Dismissal Ruling: 🔁 Repeat of previous mechanics
- Shadowed Messenger: 👾 ➡️⬅️ Cross line AoEs from cardinal clones. Move to intercardinals, then to where purple clone was 🏃
- Blinding Light: 🏃<>🏃 Spread AoEs on all players
- Pay close attention to the boss's ring color throughout the fight to know whether to move close or away for certain mechanics. Combine this with the light/dark circle debuffs and positioning requirements.
