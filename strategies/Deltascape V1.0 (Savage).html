Alte Roite
- Enrages at 9:45, requiring ~19500 raid DPS.
- Wyrm Tail: Tank crunch, moderate physical damage.
- Twin Bolt: 🛡️ Tankbuster on MT, shared with closest ally. OT stack and mitigate.
- Flame: 🔴 Raid AoE that summons orbs (Inside or Outside). Explode after a while, dealing damage and giving vuln stack.
- Clamp: Line AoE in front of boss, knocks players off arena.
- Roar: 💥 Magical raid buster, moderate damage.
- Blaze: 📐 Stack marker on healer, mitigate.
- Charybdis: Drops all players to critical HP.
- Levinbolt: ⚡ Lightning AoE centered on each player. Spread out.
- Downburst: 💨 Knocks back players from center. Stand just outside center. Instant death if too close, knocked off if too far.
- Breath Wing: Boss jumps to arena edge, knocks back players from landing spot.

The Classical Elements
- 🔄 Indicates a new phase and next Flame mechanic. Boss jumps to middle.

The Classical Elements Phase 1
- 🚫 Stay outside middle.
- 💨 After Downburst, move in to dodge Flame orbs.
- ⚡ Adjust for Levinbolt.

The Classical Elements Phase 2
- 🔍 Find missing cardinal direction in outer flame ring.
- 🧱 Stack there for Downburst.
- 💨 After Downburst, move in.
- ⚡ Adjust for Levinbolt.

The Classical Elements Phase 3
- 💠 Stand in middle between two Flame orbs for Downburst.
- 💨 After Downburst, move in.
- 🔥 Adjust for Blaze.

The Classical Elements Phase 4
- 👌 Same as Phase 1.
- 🔥 But with Blaze instead of Levinbolt.

Flame Enrage: At ~9:45, Flame orbs explode, wiping the group.
