Bone Dragon
- 👾 Kill 3 waves of skeleton adds before defeating the boss.
- 💥 Avoid letting skeletons reach the dragon, as each one deals 1500 unblockable raid damage.
- Tank the Bone Dragon on the center platform to evenly spread adds and minimize damage to DPS.

Phase 1
- 👾 Kill respawned skeletons before they reach the dragon.

Phase 2
- Floating Eyes: 🛡️ Tanks pick up the eyes, 🎯 DPS focus boss.

Atomos
- 🏃<>🏃 Split into 3 groups (A, B, C), one for each path with a platform.
- 💠 4 players stand on each colored platform to remove barriers from Atomos.
- 👾 Kill Atomos while 4 players remain on platforms. Kill adds.
- If a sector lacks enough people on the platform, Iron Giants will spawn and wipe the raid.

Thanatos
- 🩹 Heal the 3 Magic Pots in the corners. Wipe if all 3 die.
- Astral Realignment: 🌟 Buff from Magic Pots allows damage on boss. Group A gets it first.
- 👾 Players without buff kill adds charging the Pots.
- Tanks with Astral Realignment should swap aggro on the boss. Face boss away from group and pots.

Allagan Bomb
- 🛡️ Tanks pick up Vassagos sword adds (A left, B north, C right).
- Allagan Balloon: 👾 Kill before reaching center to prevent bomb enlarging.
- Napalm: 👾 Highest priority add to kill.
- 🎯 Damage boss once 3 adds are down.
- Non-tanks avoid standing in front of Vassagos due to untelegraphed cleaves.

King Behemoth
- 🛡️ Off-tanks pick up Iron Giant adds and face them away.
- 💠 Ranged DPS/Healers activate glowing towers.
- Ecliptic Meteor: ➡️⬅️ Drop comets outside boss hitbox, stand behind them for line of sight.
- 👾 Kill Iron Giant add trying to destroy comets.

Phlegethon
- 💠 Groups A, B, C take left, center, right positions before pull.
- Ancient Flare: 💥 At 40-50% and 5-15% HP, groups move to assigned platforms to block flare.
- Iron Hand: 👾 Kill adds that stun players.
- Non-tanks avoid standing in front due to untelegraphed cleave. Dodge telegraphed AoEs.
