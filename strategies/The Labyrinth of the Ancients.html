Bone Dragon:
- Tank on center platform for even add spread
- 👾 Kill skeletons before they reach dragon
- 🎯 Pick floating eyes, leave with tank

Atomos:
- 💠 Divide into 3 groups (A, B, C), 1 per path
- 4 people stand on each colored platform to remove barriers
- 👾 Kill adds spawned by Atomos

Thanatos:
- 🌟 Astral Realignment buff allows damaging boss
- ⚔️ Tank swap when gaining/losing Astral Realignment
- 👾 Kill adds, prioritize those tethered to Magic Pots
- 🩹 Healers keep Magic Pots alive

Allagan Bomb:
- 💠 Each group takes a Vassago add (A left, B north, C right)
- 👾 Kill priority: Napalm > Balloon > Sword
- 👾 Kill Balloons before they reach center to prevent wipe

King Behemoth:
- ⚔️ Off-tanks pick up Iron Giant adds, face away from group
- 🎯 Targeted players drop Ecliptic Meteors outside boss hitbox
- 👾 Kill adds attempting to reach towers
- 💠 Hide behind Meteors during Ecliptic Meteor cast

Phlegethon:
- 💠 Groups position: A left, B center, C right
- 🔴 Avoid telegraphed AoEs
- Ancient Flare: Move to assigned platform to create barrier
- 👾 Kill adds, Iron Hands stun nearby targets

Complete objectives in order, defeat bosses to progress through the raid.
