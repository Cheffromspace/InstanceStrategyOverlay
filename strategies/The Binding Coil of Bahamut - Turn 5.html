Phase 1 (100% - 85%)
- 💠 Off tank pulls Twintania and 3 Scourges of Meracydia. Main tank positions Twintania, off tank positions adds.
- 👾 Liquid Hell: Adds cast AOE fire attack on player's position, move to avoid.
- 🛡️ Death Sentence from Twintania requires tank swap.
- 🏃<>🏃 After adds die, split into 2 groups of 4 (1 healer, 1 melee, 1 ranged, 1 tank/pet) behind Twintania.

Phase 2 (85% - 55%)
- 🔴 Fireball: Targets 1 player, splits damage. Groups of 4 spread out to soak damage.
- 🔵 Conflagration: Traps and damages player. Targeted player moves into Neurolink under Twintania. DPS Conflagration ASAP.
- Fireball resets Conflagration timer if it targets trapped player. Save DPS cooldowns for Conflagrations without Fireballs.
- 🛡️ Death Sentence continues, tanks must swap during cast.
- At 60%, move Twintania to other side of room to drop 2nd Neurolink away from 1st.

Phase 3: Divebombs
- 🏃 At 55%, Twintania flies up. Move to safe spot behind where she was tanked.
- 💚 Divebomb targets 1 player. When targeted, group runs to top of slope then back to safe spot.
- 👾 Asclepius and 2 Hygeia spawn. DPS Asclepius while 1 DPS gets Hygeia to 75%. Defeat adds before 2nd set of Divebombs.
- For 2nd Divebombs, main tank right side with Asclepius, off tank left kiting Hygeia. Move same as 1st Divebombs.
- 2 more Hygeia spawn after Divebombs. Tanks grab adds, BLM/SMN LB3, then burn adds. 4 Disseminate debuffs on Asclepius increase damage taken.
- Burn Asclepius near 1st Neurolink. Group stands in 2nd Neurolink.

Phase 4 (55% - 30%)
- 💥 Ultimate ability: Must be in Neurolink to survive. Use mitigation.
- ➡️⬅️ Twisters: Targets 3 random players. Move away from Twister location during cast or instant death. Stay spread to avoid knockbacks.
- 👾 Dreadknights: Spawns mid, stuns 1 player and runs to them. Chain stun and DPS down before it reaches target for Caber Toss instakill.

Phase 5 (30% - 0%)
- 💠 Hatch: Spawns orb under Twintania that flies to target. Off tank stands in 3rd Neurolink to absorb Hatch.
- 🔴 Liquid Hell: Casts 5 in a row on targeted player. Kite along edges, don't overlap with group. Main tank can take 2 then Hallowed, off tank careful with Hatch if targeted.
