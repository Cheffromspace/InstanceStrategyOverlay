Omega
Players can fall off the edge of the arena. Omega Jammer is unavailable.

Opening Phase
- Atomic Ray: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide damage.
- Mustard Bomb: 🛡️ Vicious tankbuster. Tank swap/cooldowns. Applies Bleed.
- Flame Thrower + Afterburner: 📐 Cone AoE + 💥 blast in same direction. Avoid cone, then blast.
- Wave Cannon (Larboard and Starboard): 💨 High damage + 🤢 Vulnerability Up from left/right side. Omega spins to opposite side for second.
- Peripheral Synthesis #1: 👾 Spawns blue/yellow explosive rocket-punch adds. Avoid same-colored adds colliding. Opposite colors drop damage buff.

Add Phase
- Executable #1: 🔄 Applies 14s Looper debuff. Soak meteors to dispel or lose control.
- Storage Meteors: ☄️ Spawns 4 soak circles.
- Ferrofluid: 🔗 Tethers players, pushes/pulls based on +/- charge. Adjust positions accordingly.
- Executable #2: 🔄 Applies 12s, 21s or 29s Looper debuffs. Soak meteors to dispel in debuff duration order.
- Chain of Memory: 🔗 Tethers farthest players, confusing line AoE between them. Avoid line AoE. Tethered players get stacking debuff when close.
- Reset: 💥 Point-blank AoE under Level Checker during tower soaks.

Delta Attack
- Force Quit: 💀 Wipes raid if Level Checker not defeated.
- Delta Attack: 💥 Unavoidable raid damage. Use Tank LB3 and shields/healing to survive.

Post Delta Attack
- Ballistic Missiles: 🔴 Marks players with AoE puddles that leave Burns.
- Flame Thrower + Afterburner: 📐 Cone AoE + 💥 blast in same direction.
- Wave Cannon (Larboard and Starboard): 💨 High damage + 🤢 Vulnerability Up from left/right side.
- Mustard Bomb: 🛡️ Tethered tankbuster. Mitigate and take away from group.
- Blaster: 💥 Tethered massive AoE damage on healer. Off-tank intercepts. Applies HP Penalty and Doom if not full HP.
- Atomic Ray: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide damage.
- Peripheral Synthesis #2: 🚀 4 large tethered rocket-punch adds launch across arena. Avoid getting hit.
- Electric Slide: 🤝 Stack marker Omega charges into.
- Update Program: ⬆️ Increases auto-attack damage and updates mechanics.
- (Updated) Flame Thrower + Afterburner: 🔥 Initial blast now applies Burns.
- (Updated) Wave Cannon: 💨 Immediate untelegraphed blast on opposite side after Larboard/Starboard.
- Pantokrator #1: 💥 Periodic raidwides, 🔴 4 sets player AoEs, 🤝 stack marker, ➡️⬅️ line AoEs through closest players.
- Long Needle Kyrios: 💥 Proximity blast in center. Move to edges.
- Peripheral Synthesis #3: 🚀 8 same-colored explosive rocket-punch adds. Collide 2 to survive, more is lethal.
- Pantokrator #2: 💥 Periodic raidwides, 📐 rotating cones, 🎯 Guided Missile on farthest player, 🔴 4 sets player AoEs, 🤝 stack marker, 📐 tank cones, ➡️⬅️ line AoEs through closest players.
- Charybdis: ☠️ Reduces all players to 1 HP.
- LOOP: ⏳ Hard enrage if not defeated.
