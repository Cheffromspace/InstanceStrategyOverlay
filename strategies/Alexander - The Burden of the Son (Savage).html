Brute Justice
- 🔀 Multiple phases with various mechanics requiring precise execution.

Phase 1
- Hydrothermal Missile: 💥 Party-wide damage, avoid standing near tank.
- Seed of the Sky: 🔴 Avoid circle AoEs.
- Megabeam: 📐 Avoid cone AoE, watch for boss turning.
- Execution: 💠 Handle Steam Regulator A/B mechanic in black squares.
- Perpetual Ray: 🛡️ Tankbuster, tank swap required.
- Legislation: Remove Final Punishment stacks by taking damage. Push boss to 1% to transition.

Phase 2
- 👾 Handle adds from The Cuff of the Son Savage fight.
- Focus Blaster, then Brawler, Swindler, and Vortexer. Avoid pushing multiple adds to 50% simultaneously.

Phase 3
- Flarethrower: 📐 Avoid cone AoE from boss front.
- Double Rocket Punch: 🛡️ Tankbuster, split damage between tanks.
- Short Needle: 💥 Unavoidable party-wide damage.
- Seed of the Sky: 🔴 Avoid circle AoEs.
- 🎯 Prey Markers: Spread for Long Needle.
- Super Jump: Off-tank baits boss jump to west wall.
- Apocalypse: 📐 Avoid cone AoE from boss front.

Phase 4
- J Kick: 💥 High party-wide damage.
- 100 Megatonze Shock: 💥 Proximity party-wide damage, spread to walls.
- 👻 Mirages: Marked players spread along wall to drop AoEs.
- Double Buster: 🛡️ Tankbuster, party stacks to mitigate.
- Steam Chakrams: 🔴 Stand in safe zones between Chakrams.
- Earth/Ice Missiles: 🔴 Drop growing AoEs/puddles near edges.

Phase 5
- 💪 Repeat of Phase 3 with harder-hitting attacks.

Phase 6
- J Kick: 💥 Party-wide damage, stack south to heal through.
- Onslaughter: 💥 Proximity party-wide damage, spread out.
- Brawler: 👾 Handle Double Drill and Mines mechanics.
- Megabeam: 📐 Avoid cone AoE, dodge south.
- 👻 Mirages: Handle same as before.
- Steam Chakrams: 🔴 Avoid circle AoEs.
- Ultra Flash: 💥 Party-wide damage, hide behind Onslaughter legs.
- 💠 Enumeration Circles: Correct number of players per circle.

Phase 7
- Verdict: Handle Final Judgment and Final Punishment debuffs.
- J Storms: 💥 Reduces HP to 1, use to handle debuffs.
- Steam Regulator A/B: 💠 Kill respective add for debuff.
- Link-Up: 🔗 Compressed Water tether, sacrifice player to skip.
- Final Punch: 🛡️ Tankbuster that stuns, use cooldowns.
- Final Apocalypse: 💥 High party-wide damage.
- Final Beam: 📐 Party stacks to split damage.
- Long Needle, Seed of the Sky, 🎯 Prey Marker: Handle simultaneously.
- 💠 Enumeration Circles: Stack correctly while handling other mechanics.
- Steam Chakrams, Megabeam, Super Jump, Flarethrower: Avoid and mitigate.
- J Kick, J Wave: 💥 Increasing party-wide damage, heal through.
- ⏳ Enrage: Defeat boss before party is defeated.
