Nael Deus Darnus
- The arena has a deadly barrier. Fight has a 13 minute hard enrage.
- Ravensbeak: 🛡️ Tankbuster that applies Raven Blight debuff. Explodes on expiry, requires tank swap.
- Raven Dive: Boss jumps to player, uses Iron Chariot, then jumps back. Both jumps deal damage.
- Iron Chariot: Targets random ranged player, deals damage and knockback.
- Ravensclaw: Heavy-hitting frontal cleave.
- Thermionic Beam: 👥 Marks random player, fires laser. Stack to mitigate damage.
- Lunar Dynamo: 20y donut AoE with lifesteal.
- Stardust: 🔴🟡 Random player marked for meteor drop. Keep red and yellow meteors close but not overlapping.
- Meteor Stream: 4 players marked for clone drops that deal high damage.
- Dalamud Dive: Hits main target after Meteor Stream.

Phase 1: 100% - 65%
- Burn boss while handling meteor mechanics. Avoid Raven Blight explosions.

Phase 2
- 3 players marked for meteor drops. Boss summons 2 sets of 3 Dalamud Spawn adds.
- Feed 3 meteors to each add. Don't overfeed or let them tether.
- 🔴 Red = Damage Up, 🟡 Yellow = Haste
- Megaflare: 💥 High raid damage. Defeat adds before cast.

Phase 3: 65% - 47%
- Heavensfall: 📐 Drops massive pillar in arena center with conal AoEs.
- Bahamut's Claw: Heavy-hitting multi-hit on main target.
- Garrote Twist: Debuff on random player, stacks to 9 and kills.
- Supernova: Targets 2 random players, drops persistent AoEs with Heavy debuff.
- ⚰️ Ghost of Meracydia add appears after each Heavensfall. Kill it to remove Garrote stacks.
- 💥 Use caster LB3 after 2nd Heavensfall to push boss to 47%.

Phase 4: 47% - 0%
- Bahamut's Favor: Boss gains stacking Damage Up buff.
- 3 dragons appear and periodically use Chain Lightning, Fireball, and Iceball.
- Stack in Fireball, in/out for Iceball. Fireball removes Frostbitten, Iceball removes Scorched.
- Boss cycles attack combos with Bahamut's Claw, Raven Dive, Lunar Dynamo, Iron Chariot, Thermionic Beam, Supernova.
- Dragons use Divebomb AoEs on players.
- Survive and defeat boss within 13 minutes before Megaflare enrage wipe.
