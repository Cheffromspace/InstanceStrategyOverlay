Heritors of Fury: Garuda & Ifrit
- 🚫 Touching the edge of the circular arena will instantly kill you.

Phase 1: Garuda
- Ferostorm: 📐 Spawns feather blades that shoot conal AoEs. 🏃 Avoid the AoE cones.
- Superstorm: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage. 🛡️ Use shields/heals.
- Aerial Stack: 🎯 Marks 2 players. 🤝 One unmarked player must stack with each marked player to split damage.
- Central Knockback: 🔴 Spawns central circle. ➡️ Move close to the circle to minimize knockback.
- Storm of Fury: 🔴 AoE under Garuda. 🔗 Tethers 2 players. 🏃 Tethered players run apart, others avoid tethers.
- Vacuum Slice: 💨 Slices arena in half with persistent AoE line. 🚫 Do not cross the line.
- Occluded Front: 💨 Spawns wind orbs, knockback pushes players/orbs. 🏃 Reposition between orbs, avoid orb AoEs.

Phase 2: Ifrit
- Ifrit Arrives: 🔴 Proximity marker. 🏃 Move away from marker before Ifrit lands.
- Hands of Flame: 🔗 Tethers main target. 🛡️ Tankbuster, mitigate.
- Hands of Hell: 🔗 Tethers 1 player with 3 tethers. 📌 Pass tethers to split damage.
- Instant Incineration: 🎯 Tankbuster marker. 🛡️ Tank mitigates.
- Eruption: 🔴 Flaming AoE circles. 🏃 Avoid circles.
- Hot Foot: 🔥 Ifrit kicks flaming orb. 🤝 Stack near Ifrit's location.
- Infernal Howl: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage. 🛡️ Use shields/heals.

Phase 3: Garuda & Ifrit
- Vacuum Slice: 💨 Slices arena in half with persistent AoE line. 🚫 Do not cross the line.
- Eruption: 🔴 Flaming AoE circles. 🏃 Avoid circles.
- Occluded Front + Hot Foot: 💨🔥 Wind orbs, Ifrit kicks orb, knockback. 🏃 Reposition, avoid orb/kick AoEs.
- Aerial Stack: 🎯 Marks 2 players. 🤝 One unmarked player must stack with each marked player.
- Hands of Hell: 🔗 Tethers 1 player with 3 tethers. 📌 Pass tethers to split damage.
- Ferostorm: 📐 Spawns feather blades that shoot conal AoEs. 🏃 Avoid AoE cones.

Phase 4: Raktapaksa
- Flaming Circles: 🔴 5 circles engulf arena. 💠 Dash to 3 safe spots.
- Ferostorm: 📐 Spawns feather blades that shoot conal AoEs. 🏃 Avoid AoE cones.
- Hands of Flame: 🔗 Tethers main target. 🔥 Explosions on sides. 💠 Stack in center line.
- Aerial Stack + Hands of Hell + Eruption: 🎯 Marks 2 players. 🔗 Tethers 1 player. 🔴 Flaming circles. 🤝 Stack/pass tethers. 🏃 Avoid AoEs.
- Central Knockback: 🔴 Spawns central circle. ➡️ Move close to minimize knockback.
- Conflag Strike: 🔴 Flaming circles cover arena. 📐 Frontal cone AoE. 💠 Stay behind boss.
