Trinity of Souls
- 🔴 Colored rings appear around wings.
- Line AoEs: ➡️⬅️ to left and right in order rings appeared.

Paradeigma

- ➡️⬅️ 2 cast long line AoEs across arena.
- 🔗➡️⬅️ 2 tether to players and fire line AoE beams.

Superchain Theory

Parthenos
- Line AoE through boss: ➡️⬅️ with late telegraph.
- 💥💨 Moderate damage and knockback.
- 👀 Watch boss animation to avoid.

Unnatural Enchainment

- 🤢 Debuff prevents jumping while arena is broken.

Ultima Blades
- 💥 High raid damage, safe spot in center.
- 👾 4 targetable Anthropos adds spawn.
- 🚫 Boss untargetable, Athena's Power charges.
- ⚔️ Kill adds before Power reaches 100%.

Palladion

- Boss dashes: 🏃➡️ to targeted players for high damage.

Clear Cut

- ➡️⬅️ After adds die, line AoE destroys center arena panels.

Theos's Ultima

- ☠️ Wipe if Athena's Power reached 100%.
