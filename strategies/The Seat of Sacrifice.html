Warrior of Light: Elidibus
🚫 Don't use Limit Break while boss is casting, or he will use Hallowed Ground to become invulnerable for 8s

Phase 1:
- Terror Unleashed: Reduces party HP to 1, inflicts True Walking Dead 🤢 (heal to full to remove)
- Solemn Confiteor: 🔴 4 circle AoEs under random players
- Coruscant Saber: Ring AoE (only center safe) if boss has rings around sword, else 🔴 circle AoE centered on boss
- Absolute Fire III: "Pause" marker, deals damage + 🤢 Pyretic for 3s
- Absolute Blizzard III: "Play" marker, deals damage + 🤢 Deep Freeze if not moving
- Imbued Absolute Blizzard/Fire III: Imbues next attack with untelegraphed version of spell
- Imbued Coruscant: Coruscant Saber + Absolute Blizzard III or Absolute Fire III simultaneously
- Sword of Light: 🏃 Avoid triangle AoE on floor
- Summon Wyrm: 👾 Shadowy Bahamut attacks half of arena with Dive Bomb
- The Bitter End: 🛡️ Tankbuster
- Elddragon Dive: 💥 High party-wide damage
- Ascendance: Light damage, traps party in Fetters
- Absolute Teleport: Teleports party to Aetherial Rift

Intermission:
- Active Time Maneuver: Mash buttons for 15s to keep Will meter above 0 to escape Fetters (wipe if any fail)
- Familiar Ascian appears, returns party to arena with 3 LB bars
- Ultimate Crossover: Boss uses Level 4 LB
- Use Level 3 Tank LB when prompted to survive (checkpoint if hit boss and ≥1 survives)

Phase 2:
- Specter of Light: Boss summons untargetable adds for attacks
- Spectral WHM/BLM Twincast: 2 players soak each of 4 meteor circles (or 💥 + 🤢 Vuln Up)
- Spectral DRK Brimstone Earth: 🏃 Avoid 2 expanding 🔴 AoEs in opposite corners
- Spectral BRD Deluge of Death: 🎯 Targeted player proximity AoE
- Summon Wyrm during DRK ability, then Absolute Holy during BRD
- Absolute Holy: 🤝 Stack marker on random player
- Spectral NIN Suiton: San: 🏃 Avoid tidal wave pushing from arena edge
- Katon: San: 🤝 Stack marker on random player during tidal wave
- Spectral SMN: 4 tethered Egi Demi-Bahamuts 📐 cone attack in tether direction after Flare Breath
- Spectral WAR Perfect Decimation: Avoid first set of 4 📐 cones from center covering arena
- To the Limit: Gives boss LB charges
- Radiant Braver (LB1): 📐 Cone attacks on 3 marked players
- Radiant Desperado (LB2): Healer line stack markers (one after the other), inflicts 🤢 Physical Vuln Up
- Radiant Meteor (LB3): 4 large 🔴 AoEs on random players
- Sword of Light: Now summons 2 swords with same effect
