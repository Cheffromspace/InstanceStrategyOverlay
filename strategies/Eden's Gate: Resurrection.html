Keeper of Knowledge: Eden Prime
The outer edge of the arena will kill any player that comes in contact with it.

Phase 1
- Spear of Paradise: 🛡️ Two-hit tankbuster on main target.
- Eden's Gravity: 💥 Raidwide damage.
- Vice & Virtue (Orbs): 🎯 Marks 4 players with orbs that detonate. 🚶‍♀️🚶‍♂️ Avoid puddles left behind.
- Eden's Flare: 🔴 Donut AoE around Eden's location. 🕳 Stand in the middle.
- Vice & Virtue (Tank Blasts): 🔗 Tethers to tanks and fires line blasts. 🏃 Tanks move apart.
- Pure Light: 🔳 Teleports to corner and casts devastating AoE. 🏃 Run away from corner.
- Delta Attack: 💥 Triple AoE mechanic with safe corners. 🧭 Stand in safe corners.
- Dimensional Shift: 🌌 Transforms arena.
- Pure Beam: ➡️⬅️ Fires beams from orbs at players. 🚶‍♀️🚶‍♂️ Avoid beams.
- Paradise Lost: 🔴 Circle AoEs under players. 🏃 Move out of circles.
- Pure Light: 🔳 Repeated from earlier. 🏃 Run away from corner.
- Dimensional Shift: 🌌 Transforms arena back to original form.
- Fragor Maximus: 🔄 Transitions to Phase 2.

Phase 2
- 🚀 Eden's true form descends as a gigantic spaceship.
- Guardians of Paradise: 👾 Two adds that must be tanked and killed.
- Guardian Force: 💥 Blasts raid into a black hole.
- 🔄 Phase 1 mechanics repeat in a loop until Eden or raid is defeated.
