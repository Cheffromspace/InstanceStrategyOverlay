<span class="boss">Gilgamesh</span>:<br>
<span class="note">3 phases, uses abilities from previous phases in later ones</span><br>

<span class="boss">Phase 1</span>:<br>

<span class="boss">Phase 2</span>:<br>
- <span class="mechanic">Waves of 👾 adds between boss rooms, AoE down</span><br>

<span class="boss">Phase 3</span>:<br>
- <span class="mechanic">Phase 1 abilities return, targeting multiple players</span><br>
- <span class="ability">Whirlwind Slash</span>: 💥 AoE centered on boss<br>
- <span class="ability">Giga Jump</span>: 🎯 Marks player, leaps after ~6s, 💥 AoE damage<br>
- <span class="ability">Whirlwinds</span>: 🔴 Spawns on ground, explode on touch<br>
- <span class="note">Keep regens up, avoid Whirlwinds especially while Toaded</span><br>
