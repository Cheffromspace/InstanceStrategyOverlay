Heritor of Levin: Ramuh
- Be careful not to fall off the edge of the arena throughout the fight.
- Pick up Surge Protection Orbs to absorb damage from certain mechanics (marked as furious).
- Use the lane patterns on the arena floor to handle various mechanics, especially Tribunal Summons.

Phase 1
- Judgement Volts: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage that spawns Surge Protection Orbs.
- Stratospear Summons: 🔴 Stand in the corner with the smaller AoE circle to avoid massive damage.
- Executor Summons: 🔗 Tethers to the player with the most Surge Protection stacks (ideally off-tank with 2-3 stacks). Moves slowly towards tethered player, auto-attacking. Kills player if it reaches them. Uses a tank buster before dispersing.
- Fury's Bolt: 🌟 Buffs Ramuh for the next mechanic, indicated by 3 purple lightning orbs circling him.
- (Furious) Judgement Volts: 💥 Stronger version that requires at least one Surge Protection stack to survive.
- Crippling Blow: 🛡️ Tank buster on main target, small AoE around them.
- Stormcloud Summons: 🔲 4 furthest players drop stormclouds, 4 closest get Hated of Levin debuff. Spread clouds apart. Debuffed players stand under cloud to remove, or get feared. Avoid overlapping AoEs.
- Levinforce: 💨 Knockback from Ramuh's location. Stack close and aim to stay on platform.
- Tribunal Summons: 💨 Will of Ramuh charges through arena center, knocking players back. Stack beside charge line if buffed, at dotted line if unbuffed. Avoid Ixions on sides.

Phase 2
- Thunderstorm: 🔘 Barrage of random AoE circles. 4 players marked for AoE blast, then tanks/healers. Spread and have Surge Protection.
- Executor Summons: 🔗 Same as before, but lasts longer.
- (Unbuffed) Stepped Leader: 🔘 AoE circles under players. 🏃 Move a few steps to avoid.
- Fury's Fourteen: 🔘 14 stratospheres (7 per side), Will of Ixion makes one side unsafe. Players knock Ramuh to destroy spheres on safe side. Then avoid horizontal charges in safe lane.
- (Furious) Stepped Leader: 🍩 Donut AoEs around players. 🤝 Stack or 🏃<>🏃 spread far apart.
- Chain Lightning: 🤢⚡ 8-stack debuff on healers, passed to closest player. Causes System Shock. 🚫 Avoid passing to shocked player. Coordinate passing until gone.
- Judgement Volts: 💥 Multiple casts, including Furious version. Healers be ready!
- Stratosphere Summons and Judgment Jolt: 🗡️ Defeat Ramuh before Judgment Jolt cast finishes or wipe.
