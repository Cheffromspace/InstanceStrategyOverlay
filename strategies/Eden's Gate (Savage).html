Eden's Gate (Savage):
- 🔓 Unlocked by completing the quest "The Next Piece of the Puzzle" at level 80
- 🗺️ Speak with Lewrey in Amh Araeng (X:26.8 Y:16.4) to access
- 🎚️ Item level requirement: 440+ (no restrictions for full/unrestricted parties)
- 👥 8-player raid, accessible via Party Finder or Duty Finder → Raids → Savage Raids

Floors:
1. Eden's Gate: Resurrection (Savage) (E1S)
- Boss: Eden Prime
- ⏱️ Time limit: 90 min
2. Eden's Gate: Descent (Savage) (E2S)
- Boss: Voidwalker
- ⏱️ Time limit: 90 min
3. Eden's Gate: Inundation (Savage) (E3S)
- Boss: Leviathan
- ⏱️ Time limit: 90 min
4. Eden's Gate: Sepulture (Savage) (E4S)
- Bosses: Titan and Titan Maximum
- ⏱️ Time limit: 120 min

Released on July 30, 2019 as part of Shadowbringers expansion.
