Blaster
- 🤢 Magic Vulnerability Up debuff on main tank, but boss deals mostly physical damage.
- Mind Blast: 💥 Confuses all players. 🚫 Interrupt this cast to avoid wipe.
- Mines: 💣 4 mines (2 🔴, 2 🔵). 🔴 Avoid red mines. 🛡️ Off-tank must destroy invisible blue mines dealing magic damage.
- Mirages: 🎯 4 marked players, mirage enemies drop and dash. 🏃 Move out of splash damage and Damage Down debuff.
- Mirage Ads: 👾 5 mirage ads cast heavy Damage Down debuff. 🗡️ DPS check to defeat ads before cast finishes.
- Tethered Mirages: 🔗 Tethered to mirages. 👀 Face away if raised hands, face mirage if crouched. Failing wipes party.
NOTE: As of Patch 6.2, Mind Blast may no longer cause confusion. This needs further investigation to determine if it's a bug or intentional change.

Brawler
- Boss deals magic damage, non-debuffed tank pulls.
- Tank Buster: 🛡️ 1 blaster - face away. 👥 2 blasters - group soak.
- Slam: 🔨 1 drill - run to edge. ⚡ 2 drills - bait closest/furthest.
- Steam Chakrams: 🔄 Avoid moving chakrams dealing damage and Paralysis.
- Power Plasma Orbs:
- 🅰️ Alpha - Stun/slow. Debuffs if reaching boss.
- 🅱️ Beta - Unstunable, 💥 raid-wide damage. ⌛ Stun one Alpha to split Beta damage.
- 🆓 Gamma - 🛡️ Off-tank burst. 💥 Avoid splash damage.

Swindler
- 🟣 Purple debuff - stand on lower floor. 🔴 Red debuff - stand on higher floor.
- Enumeration: 🎯 Tank/healer targeted, orbs indicate soakers needed.
- Height: 🔺 High/Low Arithmetic debuffs, stand at correct height.
- Bio Arithmetics: 💥 High raid-wide damage, mitigate and heal.
- 👾 Ads rush main tank, giving boss buffs. 🔁 Off-tank provoke boss away from ads.
- Midan Gunners: 🔫 4 gunners fire, increasing damage. 🎯 Prioritize gunners.

Vortexer
- Brute Force: 🛡️ Tankbuster, Physical Vulnerability Up stacks. 🔄 Tank swap at 2 stacks.
- Elemental Jammer: 🎯 2 players marked with Compressed Lightning or Water.
- Fire AoEs: 🔥 Drops fire puddles with Dropsy DoT and Heavy.
- Fire Beam: 🎯 4 marked players, ➡️⬅️ line AoE. Spread out.
- Super Cyclone: 🌪️ Knocks back players and deals damage.
- Ice Missiles: 🎯 2 players marked, expanding ice puddles.
- Ultraflash: 💥 Hides behind ice pillar. 🧊 Removes puddles/pillars/debuffs.
- 💧 Water Spout: Tethers with Drainage. ⚡ Lightning stacks, 💧 Water splits.
Designate set locations to exchange each debuff to plan movement for mechanics. Avoid dropping fire puddles near water spout. Drop ice puddles in range of water spout for Ultraflash. Dissolve water spout with fire, avoid hitting ice pillar. Stack for 3rd debuff to be pushed into position by Super Cyclone.
