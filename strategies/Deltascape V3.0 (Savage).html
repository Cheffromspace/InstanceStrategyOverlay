Halicarnassus
The fight has multiple phases based on the arena panels: Standard, Forest, Cave, Library, and a Final mixed phase.

Core Mechanics
- Critical Hit: 🛡️ High damage tank buster, always crits. Tanks use defensive cooldowns.
- Spellblade: Holy: 🔴 3 AoEs + 🤝 stack marker. Stack for stack marker, spread for AoEs. Handle Pole Shift tether swap.
- Fire 3: 💥 AoE around boss. Stand under boss.
- Blizzard 3: 💥 AoE around boss. Move away from boss.
- Thunder 3: ➡️⬅️ Line AoE.
- Panel Swap: Changes arena type, affecting next mechanic.
- Queen's Waltz: Effect changes based on panel boss is on.
- Haste/Haste 3: 🏃 Increases cast time of next mechanic(s).
- Dimensional Wave: 💥 Raid-wide AoE damage.
- Playing Field: Swaps tiles to job roles, later 🐷/🐸/👹. Stand on correct tile.
- The Game: Triggers Playing Field mechanic.
- Oink: 🔺 Red triangles. 🤝 Stack to avoid pig transformation.
- Ribbit: 📐 Frontal cone AoE. Avoid to prevent frog transformation.
- Squelch: Look away mechanic. Don't face boss to avoid 👹 imp transformation.
- Place Token: 👾 Spawns hard-hitting adds based on phase.
- Mind Jack: Gives forced march directions. Adjust position based on direction.

Library Phase
- Spellblade: Holy: 🤝 2 stacks, 🔴 4 AoEs. 4 AoEs in green books (physical vuln down), 2 stacks in blue books (magic vuln down).

Final Mixed Panel Phase
- Dark Tokens: Mind Jack + 🔗 tethers. Face to stay central, maximize tether distance.
- Place Token: 👾 Spawns Iron Giant + Ninjas. Tanks separate, use caster LB.
- Mechanics repeat with increasing frequency until enrage around 11:30.
