Myrmidon Princess:
- Mandible Bite: 📐 Frontal cone AoE
- Silence: 🚫 Silences a party member
- Haste: 🌟 Buffs adds' attack speed
- At 50%, summons adds:
- Myrmidon Marshal: 🎯 Kill first, has 🩹 healing buff for boss
- Myrmidon Guard: 🎯 Kill second, has 📐 frontal AoE
- Myrmidon Soldier: 🎯 Kill last, basic attacks only

Giant Tunnel Worm:
- Sandstorm: Instant frontal attack
- Sand Cyclone: 🎯 Targets random player, 🤢 DoT debuff
- Sand Pillar: 💥 Damages players on sand traps when burrowed
- Earthbreak: 💥 Emerges from ground, heavy damage near impact
- Bottomless Desert: 🌪️ Pulls players to center, then emerges
- Sludge: 🎯 Single-target 🤢 heavy DoT, remove ASAP
- 🏃<>🏃 Spread out when worm burrows to avoid AoEs

Chimera:
- Lion's Breath: 📐 Frontal cone cleave
- Ram's Breath: 🥶 Right-side cone, inflicts Heavy
- Dragon's Breath: ⚡ Left-side cone, inflicts Paralysis
- At 75%:
- Dragon's Voice: 🟣 Eyes glow violet, 🏃 move close to avoid ⚡ AoE + Paralysis
- Ram's Voice: 🔵 Eyes glow blue, 🏃 move away to avoid 🥶 AoE + Heavy
- Cacophony: 🎯 Targets player with 💥 exploding orb, 🏃 move away
- Ram's Keeper: 🎯 Targets player with 🥶 ice orb, inflicts Deep Freeze

💡 Most attacks inflict Paralysis, use Esuna to cleanse. Low HP players be cautious!
