Shifting Gymnasion Agonon:
- Use arcane sphere to perform invocation and trigger roulette wheel
- Roulette summons 1 or more creatures for each trial:
  - Lesser summon
  - Greater summon
  - Elder summon
  - 💀 Kalypso summon: Expels party, small chance to stay if killed by ⚡
- Defeat summoned enemies to spawn treasure chest
- Only portal opener can access treasure chest
- Claim treasure, then use arcane sphere for next trial
- Arcane circles may shift after some summons

Duty ends when either:
- 5️⃣ summons completed
- 💀 Kalypso summoned and expels party
- 💀 All party members KO'd
