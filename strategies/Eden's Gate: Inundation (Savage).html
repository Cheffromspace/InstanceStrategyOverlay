Heritor of the Whorl: Leviathan
- It is possible to fall off the edge of the arena during the entire encounter. Fallen players can be raised after a moderate delay.
- Tidal Roar: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide AoE damage
- Rip Current: 🛡️ Tank buster, tank swap or use immunity cooldown. Aim away from group.
- Tidal Wave and Undersea Quake: 💨 Move to edge of platform being destroyed to avoid knockback and instant death.
- Temporary Current: ➡️⬅️ Move to opposite side of Leviathan's diagonal AoE blast.
- Drenching Pulse and Freak Wave: 🔴 Group up middle for Drenching Pulse, then spread for Freak Wave AoEs.
- Maelstrom: 💦 Navigate between growing puddles and avoid Leviathan's dive-bombs from watery portals.

Leviathan - Tsunami Phase
- Tsunami: 💥 Raid-wide damage and applies debuffs:
- Splashing Waters: Lethal debuff, cleansed by knockback from Sundering Tsunami
- Sundering Tsunami: Knocks back players in front, cleansing Splashing Waters
- Smothering Tsunami: Healers share damage with nearby players
- Swirling Waters: Tanks and DPS create donut AoEs
- Group middle, Sundering Tsunami player knocks back group to cleanse debuff. Split to sides to handle other debuffs.

Leviathan - Refreshing Shower
- Refreshing Shower: 🌟 Buffs and enhances Leviathan's mechanics
- Tidal Rage: 💥 Enhanced Tidal Roar, mitigate and heal through
- Enhanced Temporary Current: ➡️⬅️ Leviathan blasts one side then immediately the other
- Enhanced Freak Wave: 🔴 Spread AoEs now leave persistent puddles. Place carefully.
- Enhanced Maelstrom: 💦 Three dive-bombs instead of two

Leviathan - Enhanced Mechanics
- Stormy Horizon + Breaking Wave: 🔴⤫ Bait circles and avoid Temporary Current. Tank with Breaking Wave splits to back.
- Plunging Wave: 🤝➡️⬅️ Stack in line to absorb 5 AoE blasts
- Enhanced Tsunami: 🧭 Additional debuffs: Scouring (tank PBAoE), Sweeping (conal AoE), Surging (knockback). Coordinate group to handle.
- Killer Wave + Breaking Wave: 🤝🛡️ Tanks take corners, group splits to soak stack markers
- Black Smokers + Hydrothermal Vents: 📐💦 Tanks aim conal attacks away. Players rotate soaking tethered puddles.

Leviathan Enrage
- The Calm and The Storm: ⏳ Soft enrage, Leviathan's damage increases. Defeat before The Storm or wipe.
