Chaos
- Players can fall off the arena but can be raised after a delay.
- The elemental phase is determined by Chaos' opening ability after the 🛡️ tankbuster.
- Chaotic Dispersion: 🛡️ Tankbuster, use mitigation cooldowns.
- Damning Edict: 📐 180° frontal attack, move behind Chaos. Triggers Tsunami & Earthquake phase.
- Latitudinal/Longitudinal Implosion: 💥 AoE attack in two directions, then unmarked directions. Avoid marked areas, then move to detonated areas.

Tsunami & Earthquake Phase
- Tsunami: 💥 Unavoidable raid damage, inflicts Dynamic Fluid debuff. 🤝 Stack in center for donut AoEs.
- Knockdown & Big Bang: 🎯 Proximity markers on healers. Healers move N/S, raid stays center. Avoid Big Bang AoEs.
- Fiendish Orbs: 🔗 Tethers to players. Tanks take tethers.
- Earthquake: 💥 Unavoidable raid damage, blocks south side.
- Knockback: 💨 Pushes players south. Move to north edge.
- Knock Down x2: 🎯 Stack markers. Split into two groups, soak markers separately.
- Big Bang: 🔴 AoEs where Knock Down was soaked. Avoid AoE circles.

Inferno & Cyclone Phase
- Inferno: 💥 Unavoidable raid damage, safe circle in center. Stay in circle, spread for Entropy AoEs.
- Knock Down x2: 🎯 Stack markers. Split into two groups, soak markers separately.
- Big Bang: 🔴 AoEs where Knock Down was soaked. Avoid AoE circles.
- Fiendish Orbs: ➡️⬅️ Line AoEs from orbs. Avoid line AoEs.
- Cyclone: 💥 Unavoidable raid damage, avoid center tornado.
- Knockback: 💨 Pushes players out. Move to corners to avoid falling off.
- Damning Edict: 📐 180° frontal attack. Move behind Chaos, avoid tornado.
- Knockdown & Big Bang: 🎯 Proximity markers on healers. Raid in one corner, healers in other corners. Avoid Big Bang AoEs.
- Fiendish Orbs: 🔗 Tethers to players. Tanks take tethers.

Enrage
- Bowels of Agony: ☠️ Reduces HP to 1, inflicts Dynamic Fluid (tanks/healers) or Entropy (DPS). Tanks/healers stack, DPS spread.
- Dark Crystal: 💎 DPS check, destroy crystal before gauge fills. Burn crystal to avoid Soul of Chaos wipe.
- Fight repeats mechanics until Chaos is defeated.
