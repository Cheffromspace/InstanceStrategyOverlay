Leviathan:
Phase 1:
- 🎯 Boss targets tank, attack until 90% or phase change
- 🌊 Watch for water spout, indicates boss's next location
- Body Slam: Boss slams deck, 💥 party-wide stun and knockback

Phase 2:

- 🎯 Target head (casters) or tail (physical ranged), share HP
- Dread Tide: 🛡️ Tankbuster on main threat target
- Aqua Breath: Boss casts 💥 point-blank AoE periodically
- Water Spout: 🎯 Targets random players for moderate damage
- Tidal Roar: 💥 Unavoidable party-wide damage
- 👾 Prioritize killing adds: Wavespine Sahagins (cast 🔴 Hydroshot) and Gyre Spumes (drain Elemental Converter)
- 🌊 Move to opposite end of ship when boss dives
- Grand Fall (60%): 🎯 Marks player with AoE, inflicts 🤢 Heavy debuff
- Spinning Dive: Boss leaps across ship, 💥 heavy damage and knockback
- Activate Elemental Converter to survive Tidal Wave

Phase 3:
- Similar to Phase 2, with additional mechanics:
- Grand Fall: Boss leaps over ship, dropping AoE on players
- 🌊 Boss moves randomly, watch water spouts
- 👾 New adds: Wavetooth Sahagins (cast 📐 Dreadstorm, inflicts 🤢 Hysteria)
- Repeat until boss is defeated
