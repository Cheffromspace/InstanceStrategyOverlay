Lady of Bliss: Lakshmi

First Phase:
- Adds cast Tail Slap (frontal cleave), Inner Demons (gaze attack), and Stotram (add heal + 💥 raid damage)
- Hand of Grace + The Pull of Light: 🔴 Avoid AoEs, 🛡️ tank swap
- The Path of Light: 📐 Avoid frontal cleave on 2nd aggro player
- Alluring Arm: 🎯 Soak + AoE, 🏃 to safe spots
- The Pall of Light: 🤝 Stack on marked player
- Chanchala: 🌟 Massive boss buff, abilities change
- Use 💠 Vril to survive mechanics

Second Phase:
- 4 adds (2 Brahmin, 2 Shudra) appear, cast Water III (🔴 AoE)
- Kill 3 adds with 💠 Vril bubble to restore Vril charges
- Lakshmi casts ultimate when gauge reaches 0

Third Phase:
- Divine Doubt: 💫 Raid-wide confuse (💠 Vril reduces duration)
- Vril Restoration: Collect 💠 Vril in Z or straight line
- Divine Desire: 💥 Raid-wide pull + bleed (💠 Vril negates pull)
- Stotram: 💠 Vril to survive, kills those with 🤢 bleed or low HP
- ⏳ Enrage: Lakshmi spams Divine Desire until wipe
