Locksmith:
- Gold Rush: 🤢 Stacking debuff, remove by clicking Morbol Fruit
- Gold Dust: 🔴 Circular AoE under random player
- Tanks may need to stun boss to click fruit

Coincounter:
- 100-Tonze Swipe (1 Hand): 📐 Frontal cone AoE
- 100-Tonze Swing (2 Hand): 💥 Circular AoE around boss
- Glower: 🎯 Laser on random player, inflicts Paralysis
- Eye of the Beholder: ➡️⬅️ Partial donut AoE, safe spots behind boss and close in
- Stun 100-Tonze Swipe, 100-Tonze Swing, and Eye of the Beholder

Miser's Mistress:
- Bad Breath: 📐 Frontal cone AoE, inflicts multiple 🤢 debuffs
- Hooked Burrs: Stacking 🤢 Burrs debuff, remove by clicking Morbol Fruit
- Burr Burrow: Damage based on Burrs stacks
- At 50% HP, spawns 5 Morbol Fruits that must be destroyed quickly
- Use Morbol Fruit to remove Bad Breath debuffs and Burrs stacks
