Abyssal Abhorrence: Cloud of Darkness
The battle takes place on a large platform. Players who fall off the edge will die but can be raised by a healer after a delay.

Phase 1
- Ground-Razing Particle Beam: 💥 Unavoidable room-wide AOE. Mitigate and heal through.
- Art of Darkness: 🔆 Glowing tentacles - DPS stack with partner for line AOEs. 🔅 Non-glowing tentacles - Players spread to assigned locations to avoid overlap.
- Devouring Dark/Zero-Form Particle Beam: 🛡️ Main tank hit with Magic Vulnerability Up debuff, then tank buster. Tanks swap after debuff or use invulnerability. Others avoid tank.
- Obscure Woods: 💥 Room-wide damage. Changes platform for Phase 2.

Phase 2
- Flood of Obscurity: 💥 Room-wide damage and knockback, creates damaging puddles. Use knockback resistance and stack puddles at inter-cardinals near boss to create safe zones.
- Rejuvenating Balm: 🌳 Boss tethers to roots causing massive AOEs on sides. 🌱 Players tether to seeds and each other. Tanks/healers run to one safe zone, DPS to the other to break tethers.
- Anti-air Phaser Unlimited: 💥 Point-blank, tank circle busters, healer splits, wide conal AOEs. Split into assigned groups. Tanks move to arena edge.
- Wide-angle Phaser Unlimited: 📐 Large conal, tank line buster, healer splits, point-blank AOEs. Similar to Anti-air but reversed order.
- Second Art of Darkness: 🔆/🔅 Move to safe side based on glowing tentacle, then stack or spread.
- Empty Plane: 💥 High damage room-wide AOE. Changes platform, marks tiles for destruction.

Phase 3
- Flood of Emptiness: 💀 Destroys marked tiles, creates death-zone under boss. Applies Curse of Darkness and Cloying Condensation debuffs.
- Curse of Darkness: ➡️⬅️ Players fire line AOE from front after debuff. Aim away from others.
- Cloying Condensation: 🔳 Stand on yellow tiles. Move between tiles before they collapse. Two players on same tile causes instant collapse.
- Hypercharged Condensation: ☁️ Clouds move to boss, giving Damage Up. Stand in front to inflict Heavy and slow them.
- Full Perimeter Particle Beam: 💥 Large donut AOE. Stand on edge of boss hitbox.
- Second Art of Darkness: 🔆/🔅 Attacks half the stage, then stack or spread.
- Deluge of Darkness: 💥 Unavoidable room-wide AOE. Mitigate and heal. Returns arena to normal.

Phase 4
- Summon: 👾 Boss summons clones that copy Art of Darkness, creating safe wedge. Devouring Dark after.
- Third Art of Darkness: 🔆🔅 Rapid Art of Darkness (left/right attack, stack/split, opposite side).

Mini Phase 1
- Particle Concentration: ☄️ Eight meteor markers. Stand in them or massive raid damage and debuff.
- Break tethers, then handle meteors, then heal.
- Anti-air Phaser or Wide-Angle Phaser: Dodge accordingly. Move boss to center.
- Ground-Razing Particle Beam and Rejuvenating Balm with Second Art of Darkness: Find safe side, then stack or spread.
- Devouring Dark: Mitigate or swap. Move to final phase.

Mini Phase 2
- 🔄 Repeat of Phase 3 mechanics, but longer.
- 💥 Deluge of Darkness enrage wipe if boss not defeated.
