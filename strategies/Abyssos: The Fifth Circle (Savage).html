Pandæmonium Abyssos (The Fifth Circle)
- Players assigned clock positions, light parties (tank, healer, 2 DPS), and partner groups. Arena surrounded by lethal poison. Failing mechanics inflicts 🤢 Damage Down debuff.
- ⏳ Satiety gauge decreases over time. 0 Satiety triggers 🌋 Starving Stampede. Refill with Lively Bait or wipe.
- Sonic Howl: 💥 Raidwide medium damage.
- Ruby Glow: 💥 Raidwide high damage. 🧱 Summons reflective walls.
- Topaz Stones: 💥 Exploding AoEs or 🧪 Expanding poison puddles. Identify safe quadrant.
- Venomous Mass: 🛡️ Tankbuster with 🤢 Poison and Physical Vulnerability Up. Followed by Toxic Crunch.
- Toxic Crunch: 🛡️ High damage tankbuster with 🤢 Poison. Swap tanks.
- Caustic Pools: 🌊 Puddles require specific player count. 🤢 Toxicosis DoT. Failure spawns 💥 Venom AoE and 👾 Lively Bait add.
- Double Rush: 🏃 Charges across room with ➡️⬅️ cleave and 💨 knockback. Use knockback immunity.
- Topaz Clusters: 💥 Detonating AoEs in specific order. Remember order and move to safe zones.
- Venom Squall: 🏃<>🏃 Spread > 🎯 Bait AoE > 🤝 Stack. Overlapping debuffs with 🔥 Magic Vulnerability Up is lethal.
- Venom Pool: 🤝 Healer stack. 🔥 Magic Vulnerability Up. Can convert Topaz Stones.
- Venom Surge: Reverse of Venom Squall. 🤝 Stack > 🎯 Bait AoE > 🏃<>🏃 Spread.
- Claw to Tail/Tail to Claw: 🗡️ Front and back cleaves. Move based on attack name.
- Starving Stampede: 💥 Exploding AoE markers. 🚷 Immobilizes if hit. Leads to Devour. Move opposite red marker, circle around, then double back. Ignore poison puddles until resolved.
- After Starving Stampede, tanks stack and mitigate failing a Caustic Pool to spawn Lively Bait and refill Satiety.
- Searing Ray: 🔥 Beam attack reflecting off walls. Hide behind walls.
- Raging Claw: 🗡️ Seven front cleaves.
- Use Venom Pool on Topaz Stones to create safe zones. Spread and stack for Venom Squall/Surge.
- Sonic Shatter: 💥 Soft enrage raidwides. High mitigation required.
- Acidic Slaver: 💥 Enrage. Wipe if not defeated in time.
