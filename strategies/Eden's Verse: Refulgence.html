Heritor of Frost: Shiva

Phase 1
- Absolute Zero: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage
- Redress: 🎯 Gaze attack inflicting Stun. 👀 Turn away to avoid.
- Axe Kick: 💥 Massive point blank AoE. 🏃 Move away.
- Scythe Kick: 💥 Massive donut AoE. 🚶 Move into her hitbox.
- Thin Ice: 🧊 Covers arena in slippery ice. 🚫 Stand still.
- Biting Frost: 📐 Frontal AoE cone. 🏃 Slide behind Shiva.
- Driving Frost: 📐 Conical attack behind Shiva. 🏃 Slide in front or to flanks.
- Double Slap: 🛡️ Tank buster
- Diamond Frost: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage
- Frigid Stone + Frigid Water: 🔴 AoE ice circles. 🏃<>🏃 Marked players spread out.
- Chasing Ice: 🔴 Mobile AoE circles following marked players
- Heavenly Strike: 💨 Knockback. 🛡️ Use immunity cooldowns.
- Mirror, Mirror: 💥 Mirrors mimic Biting Frost or Driving Frost. 🏃 Adjust positions.

Phase 2
- Raid split onto small platforms with Hydaelyn crystal.
- 👾 Elemental adds spawn and move towards crystal.
- 🔗 Tethered adds increase Flood of Light gauge.
- 🚫 Prevent gauge from filling to avoid wipe.

Phase 3
- Holy: 💥 Proximity marker explosion
- Light Rampant: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage
- Path of Light: 💥 Stack towers. 🚫 Avoid orange marker cones.
- Mirror, Mirror: 💥 Mirrors mimic Axe Kick or Scythe Kick. 🏃 Adjust positions.
- Twin Stillness: 📐 Biting Frost and 📐 Driving Frost in random order. 🏃 Slide accordingly.
- Absolute Zero spam until defeated

Phase 4
- Ryne frozen, charging Endless Ice-Age.
- ⏳ DPS check to destroy ice before Endless Ice-Age wipe.
