The Thousand Maws of Toto-Rak

Level: 24 (Sync: 27)  
Difficulty: Normal
Party size: Light Party 4 man
Unsyncing: Allowed
Time limit: 90 minutes  
Req. quest: Into the Beast's Maw
Entrance: South Shroud (X:19, Y:18)

Named after a man-eating creature from ancient Padjali folklore, the Thousand Maws of Toto-Rak was built on the site of a natural cave system beneath Silent Arbor. Until recently, it held all of Gridania's foulest criminals, from arsonists to poachers, but the completion of a new gaol closer to the city heralded its abandonment.

Objectives:
- Activate Confession Chamber terminal
- Activate the Fool's Rest terminal  
- Open the Abacination Chamber door
- Defeat Graffias

Enemies:
Prison Pteroc, Mun-Tuy Sapling, Cell Mite, Tainted Louse, Prisoner's Delight, Warden's Whip, Prison Pudding, Mitetrap, Lightning Sprite, Comesmite, Graffias's Tail

Bosses:

Coeurl O' Nine Tails: Encountered twice, the second time with two adds. Acid Mist poisons nearby party members.

Graffias: Green goo puddles inflict Leaden debuff, slowing movement. Attacks include Realm Shaker (instant circle AoE), Silkscreen (line AoE), Sticky Web (spawns exploding Fleshy Pod adds). At 50% HP, summons 4 Comesmite adds and unlocks Deadly Thrust (large poison puddle).

Lore:
Includes notes from the Toto-Rak expedition team, details on the Coeurl O'Nine Tails and Graffias bosses, and background on the dungeon's history as a prison.

Achievements:
Mapping the Realm: Thousand Maws of Toto-Rak - Discover all locations.
