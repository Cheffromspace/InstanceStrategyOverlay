Here is the cleaned up version of the Omega: Alphascape (Savage) strategy guide:

General Information

Omega: Alphascape (Savage) is the 3rd section of Omega (Savage), the main 8-player raid in Stormblood. Released on September 18, 2018, Alphascape (Savage) requires players with item level of 370 or higher to enter. Once unlocked, players can use Party Finder or other means of forming a full premade party before entering the raid, which is located in Duty Finder → Raids → Savage Raids (Stormblood). They can also enter solo or with a party of fewer than 8 players using an Unrestricted Party.

Unlock

To access Omega: Alphascape (Savage), players must interact with the Magitek Terminal in Rhalgr's Reach (X:13.5 Y:12.4) after completing the following quest: To Kweh under Distant Skies.

Rewards

Upon completing Omega: Alphascape (Savage) duties, a treasure coffer will appear containing an Omega Coffer. Using this item transforms it into gear corresponding to the user's current job. In addition, the coffers that appear upon completing Alphascape V4.0 (Savage) yield weapons.

Item Exchange

In addition to gear, players will receive an Alphascape Datalog from each area, which can be exchanged for gear of your choosing.
By speaking with Gelfradus in Rhalgr's Reach (x13.7,y12.0), players can exchange Alphascape datalogs from Omega: Alphascape (Savage) for corresponding gear.
