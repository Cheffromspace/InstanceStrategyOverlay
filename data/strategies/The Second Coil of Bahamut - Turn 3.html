The Avatar - Phase 1
- Diffusion Ray: 📐 Large cone AOE that applies a DoT. 🧹 DoT can be cleansed with Esuna.
- Gaseous Bomb: 🎯 Marks a player with a non-transferable icon. 💥 Marked player will be hit with a huge damage AOE. 🔖 Damage is split between all targets hit.
- Homing Missile: 🔩 Draws a line between The Avatar and a random player (not MT). 🔄 Line can be transferred by having another player intercept it. 💥 Target will be hit with an AOE for high damage.

The Avatar - Phase 2
- Brain Jack: 🧠 Causes MT to attack nearest ally for x seconds. ⏰ Cast every 30 seconds, followed by Diffusion Ray. 🛡️ Place a DPS near MT to prevent boss from turning.
- Ballistic Missile: 🔗 Binds 2 players in place and summons circles around them. 🧍‍♂️🧍‍♀️ 2 players in small circle(s), 3 players in large circle(s) after delay, or all players in circles will die.

The Avatar - Phase 3
- Allagan Field: 🛡️ Debuffs a player for 30s, 10s off, repeat. 💥 If debuffed player takes damage, raid takes increased damage when debuff wears. 💉 Maintain Adloquium on debuffed target.
- The Avatar casts Homing Missiles after Allagan Field. Brain Jack and Ballistic Missile are no longer cast.

Biotowers
- 🗼 Towers located at N, E, S, W activate in sets of 2 or 3.
- 🔋 Towers start with 1 charge, gain 1 charge every 15s, up to 4 charges.
- 💥 If 2 towers charge at same time, The Avatar gains stacking buffs increasing defense and damage.
- 💀 If 2+ towers reach max charges, party wipe.
- 🦶 Stepping in a tower gives 1 charge and debuffs player, increasing tower damage taken.
- ⚠️ Not recommended to reach 2 stacks of debuff. Lasts 90s.

Tower Special Abilities
- North Tower: 👾 Summons 1 Clockwork Dreadnaught. 🗡️ Dreadnaught has conal AOE and enrages if not killed quickly, causing wipe.
- East Tower: 💣 Spawns 3 landmines in random areas. 💥 Touching a mine causes knockback and ~2000 AOE damage. 💀 Any remaining mine after 15s causes wipe.
- South Tower: 💥 Defensive Reaction (Raidwide AOE damage).
- West Tower: 💥 Defensive Reaction (Raidwide AOE damage).

Strategy - Phase 1 (0s - 2m40s)
- 🔄 Set 1 (Dread + West Tower): 🏃<>🏃 Rush Dread tower, send 2 DPS. 🛡️ Tank grabs Dread, uses all cooldowns.
- 🔄 Set 2 (Mines + West Tower): 🏃<>🏃 Rush Mines tower. 🎯 Assign 3 DPS for landmines.

Strategy - Phase 2 (2m40s - 7m or 45% HP)
- 🔄 Set 3 (Dread + West Tower): See Set 1 above.
- 🔄 Set 4 (Mines + West Tower): See Set 2 above.
- 🔄 Set 5 (Dread + Mines Tower): 🤔 Choose to rush Dread or Mines.
💣 Rushing Dread: Handle Ballistic Missile and Mines.
🤖 Rushing Mines: Handle Ballistic Missile and Dread.

Strategy - Phase 3 (7m - 11m or 45% to 0% HP)
- 🔄 3 towers activate per wave now. 🏃<>🏃<>🏃 3 players must push towers.
- ⏰ Tower charge rate increased to 20s.
- 🚫 Player with Allagan Field should not push towers or hit mines.
- 🔄 Set 6 (Mines + West + South Tower): 🏃<>🏃 2 players to Mines, 1 to South.
- 🔄 Set 7 (Mines + Dread + South Tower): 🏃<>🏃 2 players to Dread, 1 to Mines.
- 🔄 Set 8 (Mines + West + South Tower): See Set 6 above.

Solo Unsynced Strategy
- ⚠️ If Avatar is pushed below ~75% before first towers, he will cast unavoidable Homing Missile.
- 💡 Wait until first towers spawn ~15s in, then dip into one tower for 1 stack (recommend North).
- 💥 Burst Avatar under ~60% to push phase before first 2 towers go off.
- 🆗 This avoids Homing Missile and allows burning Avatar to death.
