Warrior of Light: Elidibus

Assign clock positions: tanks N/S, healers E/W, DPS intercardinals. Form 2 groups: 1 tank, 1 healer, 2 DPS each.

Terror Unleashed: Heal all to full HP or die
To The Limit: Boss charges 1-3 LB bars, prepare for Limit Break
Solemn Confiteor + Absolute Stone III: 🤝 Stack to bait AoEs, then 🏃<>🏃 spread to clock positions
Limit Break (3): 4 meteors on tanks/healers or DPS, 🏃<>🏃 spread in pairs to corners
Imbued Saber x2: 1st 🏃<>🏃 spread (stone), 2nd 🚫 stop (fire) or 🏃 move (blizzard)
Imbued Coruscance: 🔴 In for donut AoE or 💥 out for point-blank, handle imbued elements
Sword of Light + Absolute Holy: Find safe edge, 🤝 stack for share damage
Limit Break (2): Healers get shared 📏 line attack, split into 2 groups
Limit Break (1): Tank, healer, DPS targeted, 🏃<>🏃 spread by role (tanks N, DPS S, healers E/W)
The Bitter End: 🛡️ Tankbuster + ⚔️ tank swap
Summon Wyrm: Bahamut in N, 🤝 stack for Solemn Confiteor then move opposite side, 👁️ gaze attack on 1 player
Elddragon Dive: 💥 Raid-wide damage

BRD: 🎯 Flare markers on DPS, 🏃<>🏃 spread to corners
WHM/BLM: Soak meteor towers (2 players each)
NIN: 🤝 Stack markers on healers, share with 1 other
SMN: Bahamut-egi tethers, turn breath attacks to corners
Destroy adds before Limit Break gauge fills. Use Tank LB3, shields, heals for Ultimate Crossover.
