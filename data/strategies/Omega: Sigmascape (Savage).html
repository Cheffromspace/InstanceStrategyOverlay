Omega: Sigmascape (Savage)
- 2nd section of Omega (Savage) raid in Stormblood
- Released on January 30, 2018
- Requires item level 340+ to enter
- Access via Party Finder or Duty Finder → Raids → Savage Raids (Stormblood)
- Can enter solo or with < 8 players using Unrestricted Party
- Unlock: Complete quest "Test World of Ruin", then interact with Magitek Terminal in Rhalgr's Reach (X:13.5 Y:12.4)

Floors:
1. Sigmascape V1.0 (Savage) - Boss: Phantom Train
2. Sigmascape V2.0 (Savage) - Boss: Demon Chadarnook
3. Sigmascape V3.0 (Savage) - Boss: Guardian
4. Sigmascape V4.0 (Savage) - Bosses: Kefka (Phase 1), Kefka (Phase 2)

Item level restrictions do not apply for full/unrestricted parties.
