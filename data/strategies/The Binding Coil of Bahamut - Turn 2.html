This is a gauntlet fight with multiple ADS-type bosses. Kill all bosses before the timer runs out to avoid a raid wipe.

Global Mechanics (used by all nodes):
- High Voltage: 💥 Moderate raidwide damage and applies Paralysis (periodic Stun). Interrupt this.
- Repelling Cannon: 💥 Pointblank AoE, avoid it.
- Cleave: 🪓 Frontal cone attack that applies Vulnerability Up stack. Tank swap around 4 stacks.
- Vacuum Wave: 💨 Low raidwide damage. Healers be aware.

Monitoring Node
- Upon death, remaining mobs gain 💥 Physical Damage Up buff

Defense Node
- Chain Lighting: ⚡ Blasts random player, jumps to nearby players. 🏃<>🏃 Spread out.
- Upon death, remaining mobs gain 🛡️ Slashing Resistance (-25% slash damage)

Disposal Node
- Firestream: ☄️ 5 star AoE lines, damage + DoT. 🏃 Avoid AoEs.
- Upon death, remaining mobs gain 🧙‍♂️ Magic Resistance (-25% magic damage)

Quarantine Node
- Allagan Rot: 💥 Debuff on random player, explodes if expires. 📡 Pass debuff, stay spread.
- Upon death, remaining mobs gain 🐇 Haste (-50% cast/recast time)

Attack Node
- Gravity Field: 🌌 Damaging slow field on players
- Upon death, remaining mobs gain 🛡️ Blunt Resistance (-25% blunt damage)

Sanitary Node
- Ballast: 💥 3 ring AoEs, damage + knockback. 🏃 Behind boss or run.
- Upon death, remaining mobs gain 🛡️ Slashing Resistance (-25% slash damage)

ADS (final boss)
- Uses global mechanics + abilities from alive nodes
- If Quarantine Node dead: Gravity Field/Firestream at 75% HP, Ballast/Chain Lightning at 50% HP
- If Quarantine Node alive: Allagan Rot at 75% HP, others at 50%/25% HP
- 💥 Vacuum waves return, higher damage
