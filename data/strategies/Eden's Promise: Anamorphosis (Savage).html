Dread Hope: The Fatebreaker
- The battle takes place on a large platform. Players who fall off the edge will die but can be raised. Assign clock positions before the fight. Healers pair up with 2 DPS each.
- All attacks are accompanied by a glowing dragon that adds a random effect: blue (lightning), orange (fire), or yellow (light).

Phase 1
- Elemental Break: 💠 Move to assigned clock positions to avoid cleave overlap.
- Burnt Strike: ➡️⬅️ Move to the side to avoid frontal/rear cleave.
- Bound of Faith: 🔗 Tethered player bound in place and attacked based on dragon color.
- Burnished Glory: 💥 Raid-wide damage and Bleed debuff. Heal through.
- Powder Mark: 🛡️ Tank buster followed by 🔴 exploding AoE debuff. Tank swap required.
- Shifting Sky: 🐲 Observe dragon color. Boss leaps, leaving 💨 knockback and 🔴 color-coded circles. Stand between safe-colored circles.

Phase 2
- Boss switches to yellow (light) aura attacks. Elemental Break targets healers and tanks for split damage.
- Burnt Strike: ➡️⬅️ Avoid frontal/rear cleave, then stack to bait AoEs.
- Bound of Faith: 💥 Tethered player bound with explosive AoE debuff. Party stands opposite.
- Right of the Heavens: 🐲 Dragon aura summons 🔴 color-coded panels. Stand in safe panels opposite dragon color.

Phase 3
- Sundered Sky: 🐲 Dragon aura summons 💨 knockback marker. Avoid center puddle and conal AoE.
- Turn of the Heavens/Elemental Break: 🐲 Stand in safe 🔴 color circles opposite dragon aura during cleave.
- Right of the Heavens/Bound of Faith: 🐲 Stand on safe 🔴 color panels. 🛡️ Powder marked tank to left safe zone. 🔗 Orange tether stacks, yellow tether to right safe zone.

Phase 4
- Prismatic Deception: 👻 Fog covers arena. Move to clock positions. Avoid ➡️⬅️ line attacks from 👾 3 of 8 clones. Stack at safe positions.
- Cycle of Faith: 🔄 Rapid rotation of 💠 Elemental Break, ➡️⬅️ Burnt Strike, 🔗 Bound of Faith with random elements. Healers be ready.
- Boss enrages with final Burnished Glory. Defeat before cast ends or wipe.
