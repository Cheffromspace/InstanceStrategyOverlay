Tural Vidraal: Valigarmanda
General Mechanics
- Watch for body animation attacks with late 💥 AoEs.
- Strangling Coil: 💥🍩 Donut AoE, middle safe.
- Slithering Strike: 💥 Point-blank AoE, 🏃 out of melee range.
- Susurrant Breath: 💥📐 Wide conal AoE, 🏃 top corners safe.
- Ice Talon: 🛡️ Dual tank AoE busters.
- Skyruin: 💥💥 Party-wide damage, transitions arena.

Thunder Phase
- 4x3 grid arena with levitation tiles.
- Thunderous Breath: 💥 Arena-wide AoE, 🏃 stand on levitation tiles.
- Hail of Feathers: 💥💥 Party-wide damage from 4 feathers.
- Blighted Bolt: ⚡💥 Lightning on tiles and feather AoEs.
- Arcane Lightning: 💥📏 Line AoEs across platform, 🏃 find safe gaps.

Ice Phase
- Snowy arena.
- Avalanche: 💥💨 Late AoE from SE/SW, 🏃 avoid knockback.
- Freezing Dust: 🤢 Keep moving or be frozen.
- Arcane Chilling Cataclysm: 💥📏 Cardinal/intercardinal line AoEs, 🏃 find safe spot.

Normal Arena
- Ruinfall: 🛡️ Dual tank tower, 💨 knockback from N, 💥 circle AoEs at S.
- Disaster Zone: 💥💥 Party-wide damage, reverts arena.

Beacon Phase
- Ruin Foretold: 👾 Summons 3 Beacons, destroy quickly.
- Calamitous Cry: 🤝 Stack for line AoE while 🏃 dodging conal AoEs.
- Tulidisaster: ☠️ Potential wipe mechanic.
- Perpetual Conflagration: 🤢⬆️ Damage taken up debuff.
- Eruption: 💥 Baited circle AoEs.
