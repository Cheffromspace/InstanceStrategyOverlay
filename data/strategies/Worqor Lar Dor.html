Tural Vidraal: Valigarmanda
General Mechanics
- Watch for body animation attacks with late 🟠⚠️ AoE telegraphs.
- Strangling Coil: 💥🍩 Donut AoE. 🏃 Middle safe.
- Slithering Strike: 💥 Point-blank AoE. 🏃 Stay out of melee range.
- Susurrant Breath: 💥📐 Wide conal AoE. 🏃 Top corners safe.
- Ice Talon: 🛡️ Dual tank AoE busters. Inflicts 🤢 frostbite.
- Skyruin: 💥💥 Party-wide damage. Transitions arena. Inflicts 🤢 DoT.

Thunder Phase
- Arena becomes 4x3 grid. Corner and middle tiles grant Levitation.
- Thunderous Breath: 💥 Arena-wide AoE. 🏃 Stand on levitation tiles.
- Hail of Feathers: 💥💥 Party-wide damage from feathers.
- Blighted Bolt: ⚡💥 Lightning on levitation tiles, feather explosions. 🏃 Avoid both.
- Arcane Lightning: 💥📏 Line AoEs from adds. 🏃 Find safe lane.

Ice Phase
- Avalanche: 💥 Knockback AoE from SE/SW. 🏃 Avoid arrows.
- Freezing Dust: 🤢 Freezing debuff. 🏃 Keep moving or be stunned.
- Arcane Chilling Cataclysm: 💥📏 Starburst line AoEs from adds. 🏃 Find safe spot.

Normal Arena
- Ruinfall: 🛡️💥 Tank tower, knockback, AoEs. 🛡️ Tanks soak, 🏃 others avoid.
- Ruin Foretold: 👾 Kill adds before Ruinous Power full. 🏃 Dodge AoEs, 🤝 stack.
- Tulidisaster: 💥💥 Massive party-wide damage. 💀 Wipe if adds not killed.
- Eruption: 💥 Baited circle AoEs. 🏃↔️🏃 Targeted players spread.
