Guardian
- Avoid the outer arena wall to prevent the Shocked debuff which causes damage over time.
- Watch the monitors outside the arena for visual hints on upcoming mechanics when Guardian uses Load Program.
- Magitek Ray: ➡️⬅️ Straight-line AoE on a random player. Sidestep to avoid the beam and Vulnerability Up debuff.
- Arm & Hammer: 🛡️ Tankbuster on Guardian's current target. Mitigate with cooldowns.
- Diffractive Laser: 💥 Raidwide AoE attack. Often used as a filler during loaded programs.
- Prey Missile: 🎯 Marks a random DPS player. Avoid the marker to prevent moderate damage and a flaming debuff.
- Load Program: 💻 Loads one of four programs represented by sprites on the monitors - Dadaluma, Ultros, Air Force, or Bibliotaph.

Program: Dadaluma
- Shockwave: 💨 Unavoidable knockback. Position to avoid being pushed into the dangerous outer arena. Can be prevented with Arm's Length or Surecast.
- Chakra Burst: 💥 At least one player must stand in each of the 8 yellow circles before the orbs descend to avoid raid damage and debuffs.
- 👾 Dadaluma mini-boss spawns. Off-tank pick up immediately. Aura Cannon: ➡️⬅️ Straight-line AoE to avoid.

Program: Ultros
- 🔴 Simultaneous AoE circles on all players force movement. Stack together to bait circles into one location for easier avoidance.
- Wallop: ➡️⬅️ Straight-line AoEs from tentacles that erupt from additional circles. Avoid the telegraphs.
- 👾 Ultros mini-boss spawns. Off-tank grab enmity and pull to corner. Chain Cannon: 🔗 Guardian targets random player with tether and AoE barrage. Keep moving to avoid.
- Main Cannon: 🎯 Proximity marker dropped on Chain Cannon target's location. Burn Ultros while moving far from impact point.

Program: Bibliotaph
- 🔴 6 demonic circles spawn. Form 3-player groups to quickly dispel them. Undispelled circles summon demonic adds.
- 👾 Bibliotaph mini-boss spawns. Off-tank pick up immediately. Avoid massive point-blank AoEs.

Program: Air Force
- 💥 Point-blank AoE from Guardian. Melee DPS and tanks retreat to avoid high damage.
- Missile Simulation: 💣 Missiles travel across arena. Weave through gaps to avoid moderate damage and debuffs.
- Bomb Deployment: 💥 Two defusable bombs spawn. Stand on the brighter colored wire (red or blue) to defuse. Stepping on wrong wire detonates bomb early.
- 👾 Air Force mini-boss spawns. Off-tank pick up immediately. Avoid huge cone AoE.
