Phoenix
- The fight has 4 phases with a soft enrage around 13 minutes. Assign locations to drop Blackfires and a debuff swapper before starting.

Phase 1: 100%-80%
- Revelation: 🛡️ Heavy tankbuster that requires a tank swap after the debuff to avoid death.
- Bennu Spawns: 👾 Adds that must be moved away from the boss and killed quickly. In phase 3, they will revive and buff nearby adds.
- Blackfire: 💥 Drops mines that deal damage and 🤢 Vulnerability Up if stepped on. Drop them at assigned locations.
- Whitefire: 🔥 Drop on top of Blackfires to debuff and make them easier to kill.

Phase 2: 80%-52%
- Brand/Chain of Purgatory: 💀 Debuff that needs to be swapped between the marked player and debuff swapper. Healers top off the original target before the cast finishes.
- Bluefire: 💦 Puddle dropped away from the boss. Stepping in it debuffs the player.
- Redfire: 🔥 Orb that tethers to a player and explodes on contact. Destroy it by running into it with the Bluefire debuff.

Phase 3
- Phoenix becomes invulnerable and summons a deadly fire wall.
- Flames of Rebirth: 💥 Room-wide AoE that revives 👾 Bennu adds and buffs the boss. Defeat adds quickly to avoid a wipe.
- Revived Bennus: 👺 Larger, stronger adds that will tether and buff each other if too close. Defeat them separated from each other.

Phase 4: 52%-0%
- Brand of Purgatory: 💀 Debuff swapped between tanks for the rest of the fight.
- Fountain of Fire: 💥 Wide puddle that players must take turns standing in to prevent buffing the boss with Attack Up.
- Phoenix Egis: 👾 Adds that drop avoidable AoEs or tether and charge a player. Avoid cleaving other players.
- Plumes: 💥 AoEs to dodge along with Phoenix Egis.
- If a player dies, Phoenix will gain stacks of Flames of Rebirth Damage Up until they are raised. Healers must resurrect quickly.
- Enrage will wipe the party if Phoenix is not defeated in time.
