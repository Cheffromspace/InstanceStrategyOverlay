Rathalos:
- ⚠️ 3 player deaths = Mission Failed
- Roar: 💥 Unavoidable party-wide damage, ~1 min cooldown
- 🎯 Targets same player multiple times, 🏃 move if targeted
- Whipping Bite: 🏃 Avoid short-range front/rear attack
- Tail Swipe: 🏃 Move to left flank or out of range, 💨 knockback
- Charging Bite: 🎯 Targets player, 🏃 sidestep charge path
- Fireball: 🎯 Marked player move away, 💥 splash damage, 🤢 burns
- Evasive Breath: 🏃 Avoid short-telegraph frontal blast

Phase 2 (80% HP):
- 👾 Adds appear, grab aggro
- Focus Coeurl add first, 📐 frontal AoE
- 🐂 Garula charges Rathalos at 70% HP
- 🏇 Mount Rathalos, 🩹 heal mounted player
- 💀 Defeat Garula before mount fails or wipe to King of the Skies

Phase 3:
- 🤢 Scalebound debuff: healing reduced to 0, use 🧪 Mega Potions
- 🎚️ Fill Down Counter gauge to knock Rathalos down
- 🎯 Focus damage while Rathalos is downed
- Claw Swipe: 🏃 Sidestep charge, 🤢 poison, 💨 knockback
- Mangle/Sweeping Flames: 🏃 Avoid frontal cones
- Fireball: 🤝 Stack to share damage (3 times), 🧪 use Mega Potions
- ⏳ Enrage: King of the Skies wipe, defeat before then
