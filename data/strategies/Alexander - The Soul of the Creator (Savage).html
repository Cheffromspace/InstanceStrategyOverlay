Alexander Prime
Phase 1
- Divine Spear: 📐 Frontal cone attack. 👉 Point away from party.
- Punishing Heat: 💥 High damage tankbuster. 🛡️ Main tank uses heavy cooldowns. Followed by another Divine Spear.
- Blazing Scourge: 🎯 2 players marked with red orbs. 🏃<>🏃 Separate to avoid overlapping damage.
- Incinerating Heat: 🎯 Random healer marked. 🤝 Stack to mitigate damage.
- Mega Holy: 💥 Raid-wide AoE. 🧯 Mitigate and heal through.
- Gravitational Anomaly: 🔴 Drop AoE markers. 💨 Move out quickly and towards boss.
- Radiant Sacrament: 🍩 Large donut AoE. 🏃 Move close to boss to avoid. Applies 🤢 Infirmity and Damage Down debuffs.
- Sacrament: ➡️⬅️ Line AoEs from boss. ⬊ Dodge on diagonals. Applies 🤢 Infirmity and Damage Down debuffs. Followed by Divine Spear and Punishing Heat.
Boss disappears and ads spawn.

Phase 2
- Arrhidaeus's Lanner: 👾 Two groups spawn east and west. 📥 Pick up, group together and AoE down.
- The General's Wing: 💥 Casts Almost Holy constantly. 🎯 Focus two at a time to mitigate 🤢 Magic Damage Up stacks.
- The General's Time: 🟢 Large green circle buffs other ad. 🛡️ Off tank grabs and pulls apart. Casts Half Gravity 🤢 on tank.
- The General's Might: 🛡️ Main tank pulls this ad away. Casts Smash on tank.
- Divine Judgment: ☠️ Wipe if ads not killed before cast finishes. 💥 Tanks use LB3 after killing ads.

Phase 3
- Chronofoil: 🔄 Boss returns to center and grows wings. 🛡️ Main tank provokes and establishes enmity.
- Gravitational Anomaly, Sacrament, Divine Spear, Punishing Heat, Divine Spear.
- Temporal Stasis: 🧊 Players frozen for 8 seconds and hit. Receive debuffs requiring specific mechanics:
- Defamation: 💥 Massive AoE. 💨 Move away from party. 🤢 Magic Vulnerability Up debuff.
- Shared Sentence: 💥 Small AoE. 🤝 Stack with others without this or Defamation debuff.
- Restraining Order: 🔗 Two players tethered. 🏃<>🏃 Move as far apart as possible.
- House Arrest: 🔗 Two players tethered. 🤝 Stack close together.
Satisfy debuff requirements before cast finishes to survive.

Phase 4
- Time Gate: 👾 4 ads spawn in time gates at set locations:
- The General's Wing: 🟊 NE & NW. 🏹 1 DPS per gate, kill ads casting Almost Holy.
- The General's Might: 🟋 SW. 🛡️ Off tank, mitigate Smash.
- The General's Time: 🟉 SE. 💚 Healer, heal through 🤢 Half Gravity.
Defeat ads within 60 seconds or wipe.
- Judgment Crystal: 🔮 Crystals drop on random players. 💥 Drop at intercardinals to avoid TetraShatter.
- Inception: 🔄 Boss teleports based on exploding crystal location. ⬊ Dodge Sacrament at diagonals.
- Inception: 🔄 Boss teleports again, applies debuffs. 💥 Group in safe zones based on exploding crystal.
Debuffs always attach to same players in same order. Preposition to satisfy debuffs.

Phase 5
- Holy Bleed: 💥 High damage raid-wide AoE. 🧯 Mitigate and heal through.
- Communion: 🎯 Players targeted with lasers, drop 6 puddles. 💥 Drop puddles at edge to avoid 🤢 Bleed.
- Void of Repentance: 🌊 Two puddles spawn. 💥 Soak 3 times to remove, else explodes. Inflicts 🤢 Magic Vulnerability Up and Reduced Immunity.
- Incinerating Heat, Holy Bleed, Holy Scourge, 4-hit heavy tankbuster, Chastening Heat
- Void of Repentance repeats.
Phase repeats until boss defeated or enrage.
- ☠️ Enrage: Judgment Protocol wipe if not defeated.
