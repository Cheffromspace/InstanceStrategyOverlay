Note: Green acid inflicts stacking poison damage over time. Avoid running through it. Watch out for arena edges.

Silkspit
- Web AoEs: 🕷️ Tether players. 🏃<>🏃 Spread out to avoid tethers.
- Binding Soul Snare: 🔗 Binds tethered players, kills at 5 stacks. 🏃<>🏃 Unbound players run through webs to break tethers. Later casts will have more tethers.

Pandæmonic Pillars
- 👾 6 towers appear, need 1 player in each to soak.
- Point-blank AoE (cage) or Donut AoE (spinning lasers): 💥 Inflicts removable Doom if hit. 💠 Corner(s) without towers are safe spots.

Ultima: 💥 Raidwide damage and bleed.
Pandæmonic Meltdown: 🤝 Line stack.
Soul Grasp: 🛡️ Multihit tankbuster, tanks stack to share damage.

Entangling Web
- Web AoEs: 🕷️ Form bridges to dodge upcoming mechanics and Silkspits.

Pandæmonic Ray
- Cleave: 💥 Hits half of east or west arena in boss's facing direction. 🏃 Avoid cleave.
- Hysteria: 🤪 Hit players run uncontrollably, likely off arena. 🆘 Use Rescue to save them.

Parted Plumes
- Conal AoEs: 💥 Move in circle around center. 🚶 Stand on last marker, then move to first marker safe spot.
- Paralysis: 🚫 Inflicted if hit, removable.

Touchdown
- Large AoE: 💥 Covers entire center platform, instant kill and knockoff. 🕷️ Use web bridges to move to sides.

Harrowing Hell
- Multihit Raidwide: 💥 Deals heavy damage.
- Knockback: 💨 From boss after raidwide. 🤺 Stand very close to boss.

Wicked Step
- Dual Tankbusters: 💥 Two towers, one tank each.
- If one tank is KO'd/DC'd, other tank can soak both with Sprint. Left tower resolves first.
