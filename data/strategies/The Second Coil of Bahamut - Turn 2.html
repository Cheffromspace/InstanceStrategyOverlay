Melusine
- Circle Blades: 🔴 Circular AoE with indicator. Does 1,000 - 2,000 damage.
- Cursed Voice: 🎤 Targets 1 tank, 1 healer, 1 DPS. Petrifies enemies in front with 📐 cone AoE when debuff ends. Use to petrify Renauds.
- Cursed Shriek: 🎤 Targets 1 healer/DPS. Petrifies party when debuff ends. 🏃 Hide behind frozen Renaud to avoid.
- Fireballs: 🔥 Spawns fireballs periodically, targets ranged DPS/healers. 🏃<>🏃 Spread out to avoid overlapping AoEs.
- Petrifaction: 👀 Petrifies players looking at boss after Lamia Prosector add dies. Look away to avoid.
- Tail Whip: 🌪️ 📐 Frontal cone AoE with no indicator.
- Venomous Tail: 💚 Heavy damage and 🤢 Poison debuff. Cleanse Poison ASAP.

Adds
- Renaud: 👾 🧝 Giant slow cyclops. Petrify with Cursed Voice and 🏃 hide behind to LoS Shriek. Kill old Renaud when new one spawns.
- Lamia Deathdancers: 👾 🐍 Melee snakes with ➡️⬅️ frontal cleaves. Kill quickly.
- Lamia Fatedealers: 👾 🏹 Ranged snake adds. Use mitigation and 🩹 heal through.
- Lamia Prosector: 👾 🧙 Caster snake add. Look away from Petrifaction cast. Can LB this or boss.

Melusine Strategy
- 🏃<>🏃 Spread out for Fireballs to avoid overlapping AoEs.
- Identify Cursed Voice (triangle) vs Cursed Shriek (circle) debuffs. Turn away from party for Voice, 🏃 hide behind Renaud for Shriek.
- 💠 Tank Melusine north, party in semi-circle. Kite Renaud around edge, petrify with Voice when debuff ends.
- OT provokes Renaud, closest with Voice petrifies it. Avoid killing petrified Renaud immediately.
- Kill Lamia Deathdancer (melee) adds quickly. 🩹 Heal through Lamia Fatedealer (ranged) adds.
- Look away when Lamia Prosector casts Petrifaction. Kill it fast before Melusine absorbs it.
- At 50%, avoid glowing poison areas before they explode.
- Dispel 🤢 Poison debuff from Venomous Tail tankbuster.

The key to this fight is properly managing the Renauds and Cursed Voice/Shriek mechanics. Coordinate petrifying Renauds and hiding behind them to avoid party-wide petrification. Focus down adds quickly, especially the Lamia Prosector before Melusine absorbs it. Stay spread for Fireballs and avoid poison areas in the later phase.
