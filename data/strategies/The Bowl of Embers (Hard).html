Ifrit:
Phase 1 (100-75% HP):
- Incinerate: 🎯 Frontal breath attack, moderate damage
- Vulcan Burst: 💥 AoE damage + 💨 knockback near Ifrit
- Eruption: 🔴 Targets random player, 💥 heavy damage to those in AoE. 🚫 Stun up to 3 times
- Crimson Cyclone: Ifrit dashes across room, 🏃 avoid being in front

Phase 2 (75-50% HP):
- Sear: 💥 AoE damage near Ifrit
- Radiant Plume: 🔴 Glowing circles on ground, 💥 explode after a few seconds
- Plume order: Outer → Inner → Eruption → Crimson Cyclone

Phase 3 (50% HP):
- Ifrit summons 4 Infernal Nails 👾, destroy before Hellfire or wipe
- 🌟 Use caster LB on nails, melee LB if no caster
- Hellfire: 💥 Heavy party-wide damage, 🤝 stack together but not on tank

Phase 4 (49-0% HP):
- Plume order: Outer → Inner → Eruption (🚫 stun) → Zone-wide (safe behind Ifrit)
- Crimson Cyclone: 3 Ifrits, left always dashes first, 🏃 avoid being in front
