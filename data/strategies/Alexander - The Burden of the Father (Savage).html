The Manipulator
- This fight has a ⏳ hard enrage timer of 13 minutes and 10 seconds. Push DPS to get through phases faster.
- 👾 Destroy the Manipulator's legs and attack the boss in between phases.

Phase 1
- Hydrothermal Missile: 💥 High damage AoE cleave targeting highest enmity player. Tanks hold aggro, healers mitigate.
- Discoid: 🔴 Orbs spawn around arena and move towards targeted player. Intercept orbs to avoid high damage.
- Seed of the Sky: 🚫 Avoid AoEs spawning under random players.
- Perpetual Ray: 🎯 Targets random player, deals high damage and drains MP. 🛡️ Can be stunned when leg emits steam.
- Quarantine: 🎯 Off tank and DPS fight Panzer Doll in separate arena.
- Mortal Revolution: 💥 Raid-wide attack after leg is shattered. Mitigate and heal through.

Phase 2
- Carnage: 🎯 Targeted player gets 5 tethers, 3 fire Diffused Laser. 🛡️ Intercept lasers or boss gets Damage Up buff.
- Diffused Laser: 💥 Explodes in small AoE. Avoid overlapping.
- Quarantine and Mortal Revolution mechanics repeat.

Phase 3
- Straf Doll: 💥 Cleaves target, gives Slash Resist Down debuff. Wirbelwind drains MP after 30s.
- Jagd Doll: 💥 Pulse Luminous Aetheroplas debuff. Explode if tethered to Straf Doll. Bring to 10% HP to defeat.
- Shatter leg quickly to avoid enrage and second wave of ads.
- Mortal Revolution: 💥 Hits harder in this phase.

Phase 4
- Judgment Nisi: 🔴🔵 Two players get red and blue debuffs that pass by touch. 🚫 Never get both colors.
- Royal Pentacle: 💥 Destroy crystals matching your Nisi debuff color.
- Other mechanics repeat. Mitigate and heal Mortal Revolution.

Phase 5
- Carnage Zero: 🎯 Three random players targeted with Carnage lasers. Spread and avoid AoEs.
- Fracture: 🔴 Additional orb to Discoid, debuffs with Vulnerability Up. Mitigate.
- Royal Pentacle: 🔴🔵 Two methods - juggle Nisi debuffs or sacrifice players.
- Straf Doll and Jagd Doll mechanics repeat.
- 🥊 Burn boss before enrage. Avoid mechanics and handle adds.
