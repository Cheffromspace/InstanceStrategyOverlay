Chaos
- The fight has four main phases with different elemental attacks.
- First Phase: Inferno 💥 followed by Cyclone 💨, or Tsunami 🔴 followed by Earthquake 📐.
- Second Phase: Chaos Orb DPS check 👾.
- Third Phase: The two elemental attacks not used in the first phase.
- Final Phase: Enrage ⏳ with all four elemental attacks cast in succession.

Inferno Phase
- Inferno: 💥 Raidwide damage, safe spot in center 💠.
- Entropy: 🤢 Exploding debuff, 💨 knocks back players. Tanks/healers explode first, then DPS.
- Latitudinal/Longitudinal Implosion: ➡️⬅️ 📐 Avoid front/rear or sides.
- Chaotic Dispersion: 🛡️ Tankbuster, applies Physical Vulnerability Up 🤢.
- Knock Down: 🎯 Stack markers on tanks/healers 🤝.
- Big Bang: 💥 AoE explosions from Knock Down locations.
- Dancing Orbs: ➡️⬅️ Straight-line AoEs targeting players 🏃.

Cyclone Phase
- Cyclone: 💥 Raidwide damage, Headwind 🤢 (face away ⬅️) on tanks/healers, Tailwind 🤢 (face towards ➡️) on DPS.
- Umbra Smash: 👾 Chaos leaps to furthest corner 🏃.
- Damning Edict: 📐 Frontal attack, must be 💨 knocked back by Tornado to cleanse Head/Tailwind.
- Chaotic Dispersion: 🛡️ Tankbuster.
- Knock Down: 🎯 Proximity markers on healers 🤝.
- Big Bang: 💥 AoE explosions from Knock Down locations.
- Fiendish Orbs: 🔗 Tethers that tanks must grab.

Tsunami Phase
- Tsunami: 💥 Raidwide damage, applies Dynamic Fluid 🤢.
- Dynamic Fluid: 🔴 Donut AoE detonates when debuff expires, 🤝 stack to avoid cleave.
- Umbra Smash and Damning Edict.
- Chaotic Dispersion: 🛡️ Tankbuster.
- Knock Down: 🎯 Proximity markers on healers 🤝.
- Big Bang: 💥 AoE explosions from Knock Down locations.
- Fiendish Orbs: 🔗 Tethers that tanks must grab.

Earthquake Phase
- Earthquake: 💥 Raidwide damage, Accretion 🌟 (heal to full) on tanks/healers, Primordial Crust 🤢 (lethal) on DPS.
- Landslide: 💨 Knockback towards unsafe southern area 🏃.
- Latitudinal/Longitudinal Implosion: DPS must take lethal damage to cleanse Primordial Crust.
- Chaotic Dispersion: 🛡️ Tankbuster.
- Knock Down: 🎯 Stack markers on tanks/healers 🤝.
- Big Bang: 💥 AoE explosions, DPS must take lethal damage to cleanse Primordial Crust.
- Dancing Orbs: ➡️⬅️ Straight-line AoEs targeting players 🏃.

Bowels of Agony
- Interlude between first and second elemental phases.
- Players reduced to 1 HP and afflicted with debuffs based on roles 🤢.
- DPS must take lethal Dynamic Fluid damage to cleanse Primordial Crust.
- Tanks/healers must be 💨 knocked back by Entropy to cleanse Headwind.
- Orb of Chaos DPS check 👾, failure leads to wipe.

Enrage Phase
- Chaos casts all four elemental attacks in succession.
- Inferno: Applies Entropy 🤢.
- Tsunami: Applies Dynamic Fluid 🤢.
- Cyclone: Applies Head/Tailwind 🤢, leads to damage and Damage Down 🤢.
- Earthquake: Applies Accretion 🌟 to tanks/healers, Primordial Crust 🤢 (killing DPS).
- Bowels of Agony: Hard enrage ⏳, unavoidable wipe.

The fight has a small element of RNG based on whether Chaos opens with Damning Edict or Longitudinal & Latitudinal Implosion, which determines the order of the elemental phases. Pay attention to the opening ability to predict the flow of battle.
