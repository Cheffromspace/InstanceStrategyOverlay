The Demon: Zurvan

Phase 1:
- Platform falls counterclockwise, starting from east
- Metal Cutter: ⚔️ Tank cleave, face boss away
- Flare Star: 🔴 Avoid circular AoEs on random players

Phase 2:
- Soar: Zurvan jumps away, avoid 2 line AoEs from boss and clones
- Flaming Halberd: 🎯 Targeted players move away, leaves flaming puddle
- Demonic Dive: 🤝 Stack on targeted healer
- Wave Cannon: Unmarked line AoE, sidestep to avoid
- Ice Puddle: 💨 Slowing AoE around boss
- Biting Halberd: 📐 Massive cone AoE, move behind boss
- Southern Cross + Ciclicle: 🤝 Stack for AoEs, then stay close for donut AoE
- Tail End: 🏃 Move to arena edges

Phase 3:
- Adds spawn in waves, tanks pick up and DPS focus

Phase 4:
- Broken Seal:
- Infinite Fire/Ice: Stand in 🔴 red (Fire) or ⚪ white (Ice) meteors
- Unsoaked or wrong meteor = party 🤢 Vuln Up
- Tyrfing: 🛡️ Multi-hit tankbuster, increases by 1 hit each use
