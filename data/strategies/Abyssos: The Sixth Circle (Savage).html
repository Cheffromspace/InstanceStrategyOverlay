Hemitheos
- Assign spread locations, light parties (tank, healer, 2 DPS), and pairs before the fight. Avoid the instant death border around the arena.
- Hemitheos's Dark IV: 💥 Unavoidable high damage raidwide AoE. Mitigate and heal through it.
- Chelic Synergy: 🛡️ Shared tankbuster requiring tanks to stack. Anyone else hit will die. Inflicts Bleed debuff.
- Synergy: 🛡️ Tankbuster hitting both tanks. Tanks must spread away from each other and the party. Inflicts Bleed and Magic Vulnerability Up on main tank, requiring a tank swap.
- Aetheric Polyominoid: ➡️⬅️ AoE line attacks from aetherial nexuses. Determine safe zones based on line directions.
- Polyominoid Sigma: 🔗 Tethered Aetheric Polyominoid. Aetherial nexuses will switch locations before firing.
- Unholy Darkness: 💥 Shared attack on healers. Inflicts Dark Resistance Down II debuff, making overlap lethal.
- Exocleaver: 📐 Conal AoEs firing twice, once telegraphed and once in previous safe zones.
- Pathogenic Cells: 📐 Players marked 1-8 for massive cleaving AoEs. Organize around boss to handle cleaves. More than one Consumption stack incapacitates players.
- Aetherial Exchange: 🛡️ Buffs the boss, augmenting its next attacks.
- Exchange of Agonies: 🎯 Role-based markers (stack, donut AoE, dropped AoE). 🔗 Some tethered players will swap markers. Handle mechanics based on markers and tethers.
- Choros Ixou: 📐 Conal AoEs from boss' hands, firing N/S or E/W then swapping. Move to dodge.
- Transmission: 🔗 Players tethered to boss' snake (front AoE) or wing (rear AoE). Position to avoid Choros Ixou and fire AoEs safely. Inflicts Sustained Damage debuff.
- Dark Dome: 🔴 Invisible AoEs dropped under players. Bait then spread to avoid.
- Dark Ashes: 🔴 Telegraphed AoEs on each player. Spread out to avoid overlap. Inflicts Dark Resistance Down II.
- Cachexia: 💥 Players get Aetheronecrosis (delayed explosion), Chelic or Glossal Resistance Down. Handle Dual Predation (tank closest AoEs) based on debuffs, then quickly swap sides for Ptera Ixou (half arena AoEs).
- Dark Sphere: 🔴 Telegraphed large AoEs on each player. Identify safe spots and spread. Inflicts Dark Resistance Down II.
- Enrage: Hemitheos's Dark IV will wipe the party if the boss is not defeated before the cast finishes.
