Ratfinx Twinkledinks
Syringes/Puddles:
- Someone runs into purple ring to spawn puddles. Duration depends on damage ticks taken.
- Overgulp debuff timer restarts lower each puddle. Refresh before 12 stacks/timeout. Cleanse: Green (Snake debuff), Blue (Lion tether), Red (Monkey for Bombs Away), Purple (group Boost)
- Keep boss and adds out of puddles to avoid damage up buff.

Phase 1 (100%-89%)
- Boss starts small, quickly goes big.
- Headache: 🤢 Stacks on MT. At 4 stacks, 🛡️ MT stunned 10s. OT provokes.
- OT gets Cleave or Stack: 🤝 Stack with group, or 💠 face boss away.
- Prey: 🎯 Player marked. 🏃 Prey runs, 🛡️ shielded (30s damage down if hit).

Phase 2 (89%-70%)
- Boss small. Poison Puddles x2: 🔴 Place away from party/east wall.
- Shock Therapy x2: 💥 Raid-wide damage.
- Snakes x2: 👾 NE/SE. 🤢 Stacking debuff, 🟢 cleanse in green. 🚫 Stun damage buff.
- Bombs Away: 💣 1 spiked, 2 smart. 🐵 Monkey pushes smart to spiked.

Phase 3 (70%-60%)
- Boss big. Boost: 💨 🐦 Purple puddle to avoid knockback.
- Spiked Bombs x4: 💥 Diagonal plus. 🐦 Bird to first/last, move to safe.
- Prey: 🎯 Same as Phase 1.
- Gobhook: ➡️⬅️ 180° cleave. 🏃 Behind boss.
- Headache: 🤢 Same as Phase 1. 🛡️ OT provokes.
- OT gets Cleave or Stack: 🤝 Stack with group, or 💠 face boss away.

Phase 4 (60%-50%)
- Boss small. Gobbledygroper: 👾 Add. 🔗 Tether cleanse in 🔵. 🛡️ OT grabs.
- Poison Puddle: 🔴
- Bombs Away: 💣 2 spiked opposite. 🐵 Monkey pushes to 1st. 🏃 Party spreads there.
- Shock Therapy x5: 💥 Raid-wide. Push DPS.

Phase 5 (50%-25%)
- Boss big. Boost: 💨 🐦 Purple puddle.
- Spiked Bombs x3: 💥 4x4 grid, 1 safe.
- Prey x2 + Stack: 🎯 1st 🏃 runs, 2nd 🤝 stacks then 🛡️ shielded.
- Headache: 🤢 🛡️ OT provokes, gets buster or stack.

Phase 6 (25%-15%)
- Minotaur: 👾 NW, OT grabs. ➡️⬅️ Cleave, 🍽️ eats adds (damage up).
- Yorn Pigs: 👾 NE, MT grabs.
- Gobbeldygawkers x2: 👾 SW/SE. 🧭 MT grabs w/pigs. 👀 Watch for Oogle (⛓️ petrify).
- 💥 Caster LB adds.
- 🐍 Minotaur eats NE snake (damage up, debuff). 🐷 More pigs NW.

Phase 7 (15%-5%)
- Shock Therapy x5: 💥 Raid-wide. 🌈 Check debuff stacks.
- Glassy-Eyed Shabti: 👾 Crit buff. 🛡️ OT grabs, 💪 cooldowns, 💠 faces away.
- Boss big. Headache: 🤢 🛡️ OT provokes, gets buster or stack.

Phase 8 (5%-0%)
- Boss small. Gobbledygroper: 👾 🔗 Tether cleanse in 🔵.
- Poison Puddle: 🔴
- Bombs Away: 💣 🐵 Monkey pushes to 2nd spiked.
- Shock Therapy: 💥 Enrage, heavy raid-wide.
