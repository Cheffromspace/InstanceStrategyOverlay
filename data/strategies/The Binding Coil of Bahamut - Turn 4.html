Gauntlet Fight
- 👾 Multiple phases with various adds and mechanics.
- 🏃 Combat starts after interacting with a terminal.

Phase 1
- 👾 Bugs spawn in a semicircle, draining life and restoring it to themselves.
- 🎯 DPS should group and AoE the bugs down before next phase.

Phase 2
- 👾 2 Knights (🔴 reflect magical damage) and 2 Soldiers (🛡️ stoneskin negates physical damage) spawn.
- 🎯 Physical DPS kill Knights, magical DPS kill Soldiers.
- 💠 Main tank (preferably PLD) tanks Knight and Soldier on west for Dreadnought positioning.
- Tanks use DPS stance until adds are low/dead. PLD should put shield stance back on before Dreadnought spawns.

Phase 3
- 👾 Dreadnought #1 spawns, best tanked by PLD.
- 👾 Bugs spawn and get eaten by Dreadnought, 🌟 buffing its damage.
- 🎯 DPS and healers position behind Dreadnought against wall. After it eats bugs, tank moves it to spawn point while DPS burn it down.

Phase 4
- 👾 Bugs and 2 Rooks spawn.
- 💠 Bard tanks one Rook, off-tank takes other adds.
- 📐 Rooks have frontal Pox attack that reduces max HP.
- 🎯 Kill Rooks first, then remaining adds. Tanks point Rooks away from raid.

Phase 5
- 👾 Dreadnought, Knight and Soldier spawn.
- 💠 Main tank takes Dreadnought to north corner, off-tank takes Knight and Soldier opposite side.
- 🎯 Kill Knight and Soldier, then damage Dreadnought before final phase.

Phase 6
- 👾 2 Dreadnoughts, 2 Bugs, Knight, Soldier and Rook spawn.
- 💠 Main tank takes both Dreadnoughts, calling out cooldowns. Off-tank kites remaining adds.
- 🏃 Bard kites Rook away from raid.
- 🎯 Kill Rook first, then burn weakened Dreadnought from P5. Feed bugs to full HP Dreadnought. Kill Soldier then Knight.
- 💥 Intense AoE damage at the end, healers spam heals while DPS burn final Dreadnought.
