Bahamut Prime
- This is a multi-phase fight with different mechanics in each phase. Players need to react quickly to repeating mechanics and mitigate heavy-hitting attacks. There is a 14 minute enrage timer.
- Set a marker for players to stack for certain mechanics before pulling.

Phase 1: 100%-76%
- Flare Breath: ➡️⬅️ Large frontal cleave on main tank. Turn boss away from party.
- Megaflare: 🤝 💥 🎯 Marked players stack at predetermined spot. Avoid splash damage AoEs. Players marked with beam must split damage.
- Flatten: 🛡️ Heavy tankbuster requiring mitigation. Followed by 3 Flare Breaths.
- Earth Shaker: 🔗 🏃 Tethered players move left/right of boss, dropping puddles around edge of arena.
- Push DPS to get through phase quickly. Use melee LB1 if needed. Prepare tank LB3 for later.
- Gigaflare at 76%: Unavoidable raid-wide damage. Mitigate and heal through.

Phase 2: 76%-52%
- 👾 Off-tank grab Shadow of Meracydia add and keep it north, away from boss. Burn it down before it restricts movement with puddles.
- Flare Star: 🔗 Tethered orbs move to players. Communicate to take tethers and avoid lethal stacks of Suffocated Will debuff.
- Megaflare: 💥 One player must soak pillar AoE to prevent raid wipe.
- Rage of Bahamut: 🤢 🛡️ Raid-wide damage based on Suffocated Will stacks. Healers be ready.
- Repeats with adds spawning SE and W until 52%. Gigaflare cast, boss leaves.

Phase 3: Adds
- Divebomb: 🏃 Boss and Storm of Meracydia charge across arena. Move to safe zones based on markers. Followed by Megaflare.
- 👾 Off-tank grab Pain of Meracydia west. Main tank grab Blood of Meracydia and burn. Handle Pain's cleaves and Tail Swipe.
- 👾 Tank grab Gusts of Meracydia to avoid them buffing Pain.
- 👾 Tanks grab Sins of Meracydia to center, AoE down before Evil Eye cast.
- 👾 Tank grab Storm of Meracydia south. Grab Gusts. Other tank pick up remaining adds and burn near Storm.
- Teraflare: Defeat Pain for Neurolink 2.0 💥 puddle to mitigate. Tank LB3 at 4 seconds left.

Phase 4: 52%-0%
- Akh Morn: 🛡️ 🔗 Multi-hit tankbuster tethering a player. Off-tank grab tether and stack to split damage. Hits increase each cast.
- Megaflare: 💥 🌪️ Up to 2 pillars need soaking. Tanks grab Tempest Wing tethers and stay apart to avoid knockbacks into instant-kill edge.
- Earthshaker: 🔗 🌪️ Earthshaker targets also get Tempest Wing twisters. Don't overlap others. Followed by Flare Breath.
- Gigaflare every 2 rotations followed by Akh Morn. Mitigate and heal.
- ⏳ 15-second Gigaflare enrage will wipe the raid. Defeat before then!
