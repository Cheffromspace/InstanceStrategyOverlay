Kefka
- Players must stay on the circular platform, falling off results in KO.
- Hyper Drive: 💥 Tankbuster on Kefka's target. 🛡️ Use defensive cooldowns.
- Blizzard Blitz: 🔴 Donut or point-blank AoE. 🏃<>🏃 Move close or away from Kefka.
- Flagrant Fire: 🎯 Marks 4 players with orange markers. 🏃<>🏃 Spread out from party.
- Thrumming Thunder: ➡️⬅️ Two line AoEs. 💠 Stand between the lines.
- Ultima Upsurge: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide AoE damage.
- Timely Teleport: 🔄 Kefka teleports, 📐 cone AoE or 💨 Aero Assault knockback. 🏃 Move behind teleport circle, then in front if no AoE.

Trick Attacks
- Abilities have question mark (???) telegraphs. 🔄 Do the opposite of normal mechanics.
- (Tricky) Thrumming Thunder: ➡️⬅️ Stand inside the line AoEs.
- (Tricky) Blizzard Blitz: 🔴 Move into the AoE telegraph.

Statue of the Gods
- Statue appears as Kefka's HP decreases, raid ascends higher.
- ➡️⬅️ Tower shoots line AoEs across platform. 🏃 Avoid lines.
- 🟣 Dark orb on one side, explodes covering half with dark magic. 🏃 Move to other half.
- 🟡 Bright orb knocks back raid. 💠 Move close to orb.
- 🔴 Orb on left/right side of statue, that side blasted with elemental magic. 🏃 Move to orb-less side.
- 👁️ Purple eye icon, Gaze Attack. 💠 Turn back to tower.
- ❓👁️ Yellow eye with ?, Tricky Gaze. 🔄 Look at tower.
