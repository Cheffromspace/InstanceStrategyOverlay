Terminus Snatcher
- Last Gasp: 🛡️ Tank buster.
- Note of Despair: 💥 Room-wide AoE.
- Mouth Off: 🔴 5 mouths around arena edge. 🚶 Stand on/behind closed mouths to avoid AoEs.
- What Is Left/Right: 💥 AoE from boss's raised arm side. Can accompany Mouth Off or Wallow.
- Lost Hope: 🏃 Limits movement direction. ⏳ Time movement to reach Mouth Off safe zones.
- Wallow: ❌ Point-blank AoE markers. 🏃<>🏃 Spread out. Only used during What is Left/Right.

Terminus Wrecker
- 🤢 Arena edge applies Burns DoT after 10 seconds.
- Total Wreck: 🛡️ Tank buster.
- Meaningless Destruction: 💥 Room-wide AoE.
- Poison Heart: 🤝 Random stack marker.
- Unholy Water: 💧 Water Orbs around edge. 🚶 Run into orb for fire immunity but Fetters/Water Res Down.
- Aether Siphon: 🔋 Absorbs aether from fire/water sources. Alternates after first random cast.
- Aether Spray - Fire: 🔥 Room-wide fire AoE. 💧 Stand in Water Orb before cast finishes.
- Aether Spray - Water: ➰ Radial knockback marker. 💠 Stand in center or use knockback resistance.

Blasphemy: Svarbhanu
- 🤢 Arena edge Bleed DoT AoE after 10 seconds.
- Gnashing of Teeth: 🛡️ Tank buster.
- Flames of Decay: 💥 Room-wide AoE.
- Aetherial Disruption: ➡️⬅️ 4 line AoEs divide arena. 🚶 Move to opposite color quadrant based on marker.
- Crumbling Sky: ➰ Radial knockback and/or ❌ point-blank AoEs after most Aetherial Disruption casts.
- Meteor Phase: ☄️ Boss leaves, fires 3 sets of meteors in columns. 🚶 Avoid meteors and random AoEs. Safe spots: 2/3 first, opposite second, farthest third.
- Both Crumbling Sky effects occur after meteor phase.
