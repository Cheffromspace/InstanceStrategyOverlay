Titan (Extreme)
General Strategy
- Everyone except tank stack behind Titan.
- Gaol: 🔗 Targets DPS and healer. ⚔️ DPS under Titan, 💚 healer far edge.
Phase 1
- Landslide: 💥📐 Frontal cone. 🛡️ Face away from group.
- Weight of the Land: 💥 AoE on players. 🤝 Stack to dodge.
- Tumults: 💥💥 4 raid-wide AoEs.
- Mountain Buster: 🛡️ Tank buster. 🛡️🔁 Swap every 2-3 stacks.
Phase 2
- Landslide: 💥📐 Now 3 directions.
- Granite Gaol: Free ⚔️ DPS first, then 💚 healer.
- Bomb Boulders: 💥🟠 Covers 2/3 arena. 🏃 Move to safe spot.
- Upheaval: 💨 Knockback. 🤝 Stack behind Titan.
Phase 3 (55% HP)
- Focus Titan's Heart.
- Landslide: 💥📐 Now 5 directions.
- ⏱️ Destroy heart before 3rd Weight of the Land or wipe.
Phase 4
- Granite Gaolers: 👾 Adds appear.
- Bomb Boulders: 💥🟠 Clock and multi-area patterns. 🏃 Move to safe spots after first explosions.
- ⏳ Enrage at 10 minutes: Repeated Upheaval knocks off platform.
