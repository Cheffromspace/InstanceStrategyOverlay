Barbariccia:
Phase 1:
- Void Aero IV: 💥 Unavoidable roomwide AoE
- Savage Barbery: 🏃 Avoid line or donut AoE based on red blade pattern, boss throws sword
- Hair Raid: Boss tethers to sword, 🏃 move behind sword to avoid 📐 wide cone AoE
- Void Aero III: 🛡️ Split tank buster, high damage small AoE, tanks use mitigation
- Teasing Tangles: 4 large AoEs spawn, hair tethers pull players in, inflicting 🤢 Tangled debuff (slowed movement)
- Secret Breeze: 2 sets of 📐 conal AoEs, stand between 1st set, move to 1st set's location for 2nd
- Deadly Twist: 🤝 Stack marker on random player
- Hair Spray: 🎯 AoE markers on tank, healer, 2 DPS
- Repeat until boss <70% HP
- Curling Iron: 💨 Knockback and stun

Phase 2:
- Catabasis: High damage, 🩹 healers be ready
- Boss fires line AoEs + 🔴 AoE with curved lines, 🏃 stand in safe zones
- 👾 Clones charge across arena twice, 🏃 move to safe zones between charges
- Deadly Twist + Boulder: 🤝 Stack, then 🏃 avoid large rock AoE
- Boulder Break: 🛡️ High damage tank buster, tanks separate and mitigate
- Knuckle Drum: Repeated ground strikes, increasing damage
- Bold Boulder: 🎯 Tank targeted, high damage + party-wide rubble
- Tousle: Slow 🔴 AoEs from center, knock players up if hit
- Tornado Chain: Large 🔴 AoE + outer donut AoE, 🏃 stand outside then move in
- Blow Away: 👾 Clones charge + random 🔴 AoEs
- Teasing Tangles + Touchdown: Tethered to AoEs, 🏃 avoid knockback into arena edge
- Repeat until boss defeated
