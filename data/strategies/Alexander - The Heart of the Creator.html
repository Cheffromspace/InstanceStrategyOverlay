Cruise Chaser
Phase 1:
- 👾 🛡️ OT picks up flying add (E.D.D.) and ensures it dies before self-destructing.
- Optical Sight:
- 🔴 Avoid overlapping green AoE circles.
- 🔴 Move to outer ring to avoid giant light circle.
- 🔴 Wait in middle, then move to outer ring as light circles connect.
- Photon: 🩹 Heal 4 players who drop low on HP.
- Propeller Wind: 🛡️ 🤢 Hide behind objects to avoid Confusion debuff.
- GA-100: 🛡️ OT takes heavy damage, party takes AoE damage (less if far away).
- 💨 🔄 At 50%, boss teleports to center, knockback, become untargetable. Click duct to avoid damage during cutscene.

Phase 2:
- Limit Cut: ⏳ 🗡️ If low DPS, kill Lapis Lazuli Orbs to avoid wipe.
- Eternal Darkness: 💥 Raidwide AoE damage and bleed. Kill orbs to avoid wipe.
- 👾 ⚠️ Untargetable flying adds shoot ➡️⬅️ line AoEs during Optical Sight.
- 💠 🛡️ Be in front of Plasma Shield to damage Cruise Chaser.
- 🏃 Cruise Chaser rushes MT after shield.
- Optical Sight + Prey: 💥 3 large AoEs on target. Healers shield/heal through. Usually happens at same time.
