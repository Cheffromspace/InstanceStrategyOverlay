Queen Eternal 👑
Phase 1
- ⚠️ Players may fall off the arena to their deaths.
- Legitimate Force: 💥📐 Two sequential half-room AoEs from glowing hands. 🏃 Move to safe side.
- Aethertithe: 💥 Continuous light magical damage. 💚 Requires healing.
- The absorption of aether accelerates!: 💥📐 Three conal AoEs on distorted grid sections. 🏃 Avoid AoEs or instant death.
- Coronation: 💥🎯 Moving AoEs follow players. 🏃↔️🏃 Spread out.
- Prosecution of War: 🛡️ Physical tankbuster. Use cooldowns.
- Virtual Shift: 💥💥 Party-wide damage, arena changes to hourglass shape.
- Coronation (Line): 💥📏 Moving line AoE tethered to players. Position carefully.
- Downburst/Powerful Gust: 💨 Radial/horizontal knockbacks. Position diagonally or use knockback immunity.
- Brutal Crown: 💥🍩 Late telegraph donut AoE. Watch appendages, move to safe spot.
- Royal Domain: 💥💥 Party-wide damage.

Phase 2 (After second Virtual Shift)
- Players float, but avoid arena borders.
- Castellation: 💥 Castle wall with holes. Align with correct holes (top/bottom).
- Gravity increases/decreases: Players fall or float. Land on platforms or align with holes.

Phase 3 (Arena reverts)
- Absolute Authority: ⚙️ Fast mechanics sequence:
• 💚 Healers mitigate, ⚔️ DPS spread flares
• 🤝 Stack, 🏃 dodge AoEs
• 👁️ Look away from healer gazes
- Divide and Conquer: 💥📏 Line AoEs on players. 🏃↔️🏃 Spread out.

Final Phase (30% HP)
- Dynastic Diadem: 💥🍩 Donut AoE in front.
- Royal Banishment: 💥💥 Party-wide damage + 💥📐 conal AoEs. 💚 Heal, 🏃 dodge.
- Dawn's Resolve: 🌟 Increases player damage.
