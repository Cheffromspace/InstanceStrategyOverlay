Melusine
- Circle Blade: 🔴 Small AoE attack.
- Tail Slap: ➡️⬅️🛡️ Untelegraphed cleave attack.
- Circle of Flames: 💥 Two untelegraphed AoEs targeting ranged/casters. 🏃<>🏃 Avoid overlapping AoEs.
- Cursed Voice: 🤢 Inflicts 6-9s status. 📐 Face away from party when it expires to avoid petrifying cone.
- Cursed Shriek: 💥 Petrifies line of sight after 10s. 🤝 Hide behind petrified ally.
- Petrifaction: 🎯 Gaze attack that petrifies players looking at boss. 👀 Turn away to avoid.
- Remember the rotation: Cursed Voice, Cursed Voice, Shriek. Renauds that spawn after 2nd voice are used for Shriek.

Phase 1 (100% - 80%)
- 👾 Adds - Renaud. 🤢 Cursed Voice on tank/healer/dps.
- Kite Renauds to south, stone them near healers. Kill first set of Renauds. Wait for voice before 80% to ensure Renauds spawn with 2nd voice for Shriek.

Phase 2 (80% - 65%)
- 👾 Adds - Renaud, Lamia Deathdancer. 🤢 Cursed Voice, 💥 Cursed Shriek on healer/dps, 🎯 Petrifaction.
- Wait for Shriek before 65% to reset cycle. Kill at least 2 Fatedealers during 2x Cursed Voice.

Phase 3 (65% - 50%)
- 👾 Adds - Renaud, Lamia Fatedealer. 🤢 Cursed Voice on both healers, 💥 Cursed Shriek, 🎯 Petrifaction.
- Venomous Tail: 💥 Raidwide DoT, 🩹 Esuna required.
- Poison Floor: 🔴 Explodes after 15s, 🏃 Avoid standing in it.
- Kill Fatedealers. Wait for Shriek before 50% to reset cycle.

Phase 4 (50% - 0%)
- 👾 Adds - Renaud, Lamia Prosector. 🤢 Cursed Voice, 💥 Cursed Shriek on tank if Prosector dead, 🎯 Petrifaction.
- Venomous Tail targets 4 if Prosector dead.
- Poison Floor.
- DPS Prosector but wait for Shriek to kill. Assign kiters for double Shriek to position Renauds opposite each other. Increased voice frequency. Adapt positioning based on Petrifaction RNG. Remove Venomous Tails and tank swap during double Shrieks.

Enrage timer: 11 minutes
