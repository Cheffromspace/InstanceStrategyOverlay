Erichthonios
- 💠 Set markers and assign positions: tanks/melee at cardinals near boss, healers/ranged at intercardinals in corners.

Phase 1
- Heavy Hand: 🛡️ Single target tankbuster.
- Warder's Wrath: 💥 Unavoidable room-wide AoE. Mitigate and heal through.
- Aetherial Shackles: 🔗 Red debuff on player far from boss, purple debuff on player near boss. Players spread to corners/cardinals to avoid overlap damage.
- Gaoler's Flails: 🌀 Throws spike ball (point blank AoE), chakram (donut AoE), or hooks (directional cleave). Avoid based on telegraphed attacks.
- Pitiless Flails of Grace/Purgation: 🛡️ Tankbuster with knockback and debuff. Tanks swap after. Grace: share damage. Purgation: avoid tank's AoE.

Phase 2
- Intemperance: 🔳 Changes arena to 3x3 grid with elemental crystals.
- Intemperate Torment: 💥 Destroys crystals, applying fire/ice debuffs. Stand in square to get opposite debuff of 1st crystal. Avoid purple.
- Shining Cells: 💥 Raidwide damage, divides arena into red/blue wedges.
- Aetherflail: 🌀 Colored orbs show safe wedge. Avoid hook/spike/chakram and stand in safe wedge.
- Shackles of Time: 🔗 Debuff on player. Party stands on opposite color wedge before tankbuster.
- Fourfold Shackles: 🔗 4 sets of debuffs. Preassign corners/cardinals based on debuff timers.

Phase 3
- Intemperate Torment: 💥 Same as before, but with Gaoler's Flails between crystals.
- Shining Cells: 💥 Pitiless Flails or Aetherial Shackles while resolving wedge mechanics.
- Boss repeats Aetherflail and Warder's Wrath until enrage.
- Lethe: ⏳ Enrage cast around 10 minutes. Wipes raid.
