Warder of the Condemned: Erichthonios
- Gaeoler's Flail: ➡️⬅️ Erichthonios spins his flail on either left or right side. 🏃 Move to the opposite side to dodge. Later in the fight, this will happen without a cast bar during other mechanics.
- Pitiless Flail: 🎯 Line AoE targets random player. 🤺 Targeted player stays close to boss to avoid knockback, others avoid the line. Stack marker appears on targeted player after.
- Heavy Hand: 🛡️ Tankbuster attack on the main tank.
- Warden's Wrath: 💥 Heavy unavoidable raidwide damage. Heal and mitigate.
- Intemperance: 🔥❄️ Players receive either Overwhelmed by Fire (red triangle) or Overwhelmed by Cold (blue triangle) debuff. Arena divided into quarters with colored crystals.
- Intemperate Torrent: 💥 Bottom crystal of each quarter explodes with Fire or Cold damage 3 times. 🔄 Stand in quarter with element opposite your debuff. Debuff swaps to match damage taken each time. Avoid purple cross between zones.
- After first Intemperance, Gaeoler's Flail will strike alongside Intemperate Torrent explosions. Move to safe quarter and side of boss.
- Shining Cells: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide damage. Arena shrinks and divides into alternating 🔴🔵 wedges.
- Aetherchain: 🔴🔵 Orbs circle boss. 🏃 Move to wedge opposite the color of orbs to avoid explosion when cast ends.
- Aetherflail: ➡️⬅️ Gaeoler's Flail strikes alongside Aetherchain explosions. 🔴🔵 Find safe wedge on safe side of boss.
- Slam Shut: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide damage. Ends the Shining Cells phase.
