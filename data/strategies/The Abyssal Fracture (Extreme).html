Zeromus:
Assign light parties (1 tank, 1 healer, 2 DPS) and corner partners (DPS + support). Tanks/melees go north.

Phase 1:
- Abyssal Nox: Reduces HP to 1, inflicts 🤢 Doom (heal to full to remove). Dodge 2 sets of 🔴 AoEs (N or W safe).
- Sable Thread: 🛡️ Heavy tank stack (use mits)
- Dark Matter: 🛡️ Three-hit tank buster on both tanks
- Visceral Whirl: ➡️⬅️ line AoEs into ❌ cross AoEs (brief indicators)
- Flare: 🤝 NW tower (tanks/melee), SE tower (healers/ranged). Dodge 📏 line AoEs after.
- Void Bio: 👾 Poison orbs move S (dodge gaps). 🤢 Pollen DoT if hit. Overlaps with Visceral Whirl.
- Big Bang: 🏃<>🏃 Spreads + 🔴 AoEs (lethal overlap). 💥 Raidwide (mitigate). Debuffs applied (see below).
- Void Meteor: 💥 Proximity AoEs (stack mid), leaves meteors
- Meteor Impact: Tethers tanks/healers then DPS (or vice versa) to 2 meteor markers. Stretch tethers without overlapping 🔴 on meteors. Place 1st group's ⏳ meteors far N. Dodge point-blank 🔴 AoEs after.
- Bonds of Darkness: 🔗 Chains (run to break). Visceral Whirl (DPS N, support S). 🏃<>🏃 Divisive Dark spreads. 🛡️ Dark Matter on tanks, 🤝 Beckoning Dark stack (no Forked Lightning).
- Black Hole: 1 player drops expanding ⚫ AoE, boss casts 📏 Fractured Eventide laser (dodge with group).
- Big Crunch: 💥 Raidwide, removes HP penalty. Only 💫 Big Bounce DoT applied.
- Sparking/Branding Flare: 🤝 W/E towers in light parties. Go to assigned corners after (always safe). 🏃<>🏃 Spreads if Sparking, 🤝 stacks if Branding. Dodge 🎯 Nox.

Phase 2 (at ~25% HP):
- Rend the Rift: 💥 Raidwide, transitions arena
- Void Thunder: Baited 🔴 AoEs
- Nostalgia: 💥 7-hit raidwide (4 phys, 3 mag)
- Flow of the Abyss: Boss tethers to fissure (📏 line AoE), plus one of:
- 🎯 Stack marker (Umbral Rays)
- 🏃<>🏃 Spreads (Akh Rhai) leaving persistent 🔴 AoEs
- 1️⃣2️⃣ Partner stacks on tanks or healers (Umbral Prism)
- Flow of the Abyss repeats with Void Thunder and 📐 Chasmic Nails conal AoEs (apply 🤢 DoT, cleanse with Esuna).

Repeat until boss dies or enrages with Big Bang (hard enrage possible before phase 2).
