Sasquatch:
- Chest Thump: 💥 Arena-wide AoE, interrupt with 🍌 Banana Bunch from Wild Banana Tree
- Smallfoot Sifaka: 👾 Adds that eat 🍌 Banana Bunches, kill quickly
- Stool Pelt: 🎯 Targeted 🔴 AoE on random player
- Interact with only one Wild Banana Tree at a time

Sjoorm:
- Upwell: ➡️ Frontal line AoE
- Water Puddle: 🎯 Targeted 🔴 AoEs on each player
- Watery Grave: 💨 Bubbles in 3x3 grid, use to avoid Whirlpool
- Burrow: Boss untargetable, uses Whirlpool or Tunnel
- Whirlpool: 💥 Arena-wide AoE, avoid by standing in Watery Grave bubbles
- Tunnel: 🏃 Boss moves towards random player with 🔴 AoEs
- Surface Breach: 🛡️ Heavy damage when boss returns from Burrow

Kraken:
- Kill 10 Kraken's Arms (2 per platform)
- Waterspouts: 🏃 Move between platforms, applies 🤢 Suppuration debuff
- Wallop: 📐 Frontal cone AoE
- Inkblot: 🎯 Targeted 🔴 AoE, applies slow and DoT
- Clearout: 💨 Donut AoE, stand inside hitbox to avoid knockback
- Tentacle: 👾 Spawns after 4 arms killed, focus over arms
- Scoop: Picks up and throws players onto random platforms
- World's End: 🎯 1-2 players marked, drop 🌪️ tornado on edge of platform
