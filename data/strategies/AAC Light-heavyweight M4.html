Neoteric Witch
Phase 1
- ⚠️ Players may fall off the arena. Avoidable AoEs inflict 🤮⬆️ Vulnerability Up or Sustained Damage.
- Wrath of Zeus: 💥💥 Raid-wide magical damage.
- Sidewise Spark: 💥📐 Half-room cleave. 🏃 Avoid side with spinning cubes.
- Shadows's Sabbath: 👥 Summons clones for half-room cleaves. 🏃 Dodge cleaves, watch for boss's own cleave.
- Wicked Jolt: 💥📏 Line AoE tankbuster.
- Stampeding Thunder: 💥🎯 Multi-hit AoE from cannon. 🏃 Move to safe column to avoid falling.
- Threefold/Fourfold/Fivefold Blast: 💥📐 Alternating half-room cleaves. 🏃 Dodge based on cannon markers.
- Thunderstorm: 🏃↔️🏃 Spread markers. Spread to avoid overlap (lethal).
- Wicked Bolt: 🤝 Five-hit stack AoE.

Phase 2 (Boss equips wings)
- Bewitching Flight: 💥📏 Thin line AoEs with gaps. 🏃 Stand in gaps, move to exploded lines. Dodge baited AoEs.
- Shadow's Sabbath (Winged): 👥 Winged clone, cross pattern AoEs. 🏃 Dodge expanding line AoEs.
- Witch Hunt: 💥🎯 Spiraling circle AoEs. 🏃 Move to avoid.
- Soaring Soulpress: 🤝 Physical stack marker.

Final Phase
- Arena reduced to one column. Boss gains omnidirectional hitbox.
- Wicked Hypercannon: 💥📏 Persistent split damage AoE (soft enrage). Heal and mitigate. Dodge baited AoEs between casts.
