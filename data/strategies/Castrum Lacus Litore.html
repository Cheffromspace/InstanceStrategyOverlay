Brionac & 4th Legion Helldiver
Simultaneous Fights
- Split raid into two groups. Engage bosses simultaneously to avoid enrage.
- Electric Anvil/Magitek Missiles: 🛡️ Tankbusters. Mitigate.
- False Thunder: 🪓 Move to side arm points to avoid cleave.
- Anti-warmachine Weaponry: 👾 Kill Magitek Core add quickly.
- Energy Generation: ⚙️ Light/Dark Orbs with various AoEs. Adjust position.
- Chain Cannon: 💥📏 Avoid hitting Tunnel Armor.
- Lateral Dive/Dive Formation: 🟠⚠️ Soak blue rectangular AoEs.
- Infrared Blast: 🔗 Intercept tethers to protect Tunnel Armor.

Prisoner Rescue Event
- Split into 6 parties, prioritize intercardinal branches first.
- Albeleo the Maleficent: 👾 Defeat adds, stun/silence large AoEs.
- Find and defeat executioners ASAP to save prisoners.
- Step on glowing platforms in miniboss cells to proceed.

Adrammelech
Main Phase
- Curse of the Fiend: ⚙️ Handle elemental orb mechanics (memorize effects).
- Burst II & Warped Light: 🏃 Avoid chained orbs and final AoE.
- Meteor: ⏱️ Kill Arcane Spheres quickly to prevent wipe.
- Flare: 🛡️ Tankbuster. Mitigate.

Lyon rem Helsos & Dawon
Simultaneous Fights
- Designate a party for Lyon (harder fight). Rest fight Dawon and adds.
- Molting Plumage: 💥💥 Raidwide damage. Position for Feather AoEs.
- Obey: 👀 Remember attack order and types (+ or o).
- Winds' Peak: 💨 Avoid AoE and knockback.
- Heart of Nature: 💥 Move into initial AoE, then avoid donuts.
- Taste of Blood: 🪓 Get behind Lyon to avoid cleave.
- The King's Notice: 👁️ Look away from gaze attack.
