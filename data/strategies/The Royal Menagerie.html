Shinryu:
- Corrupted Aether bar: Fills over time, triggers elemental attacks at 100%
- Water (Tidal Wave): 🏃 Run to edge near waterspout, avoid 💨 knockback
- Wind (Aerial Blast): Stay near center, avoid 💨 knockback pulses
- Fire (Hellfire): 💥 Party-wide damage, stand in water puddles to reduce damage
- Lightning (Judgment Bolt): 💥 Party-wide damage, avoid water puddles
- Earth (Earthen Fury): 💥 Moderate party-wide damage, targets and damages platforms
- Ice (Diamond Dust): 🥶 Freezes platform, don't move to avoid sliding off
- Right Wing: 🎯 Marks players, 🏃<>🏃 spread to avoid lightning bolts
- Left Wing: Icicles or low-damage platform-wide attack
- Earth Breath: 🎯 Marks 2 players, 📐 avoid cone AoEs
- Ahk Morn: 🤝 Stack to reduce damage. Phase 3: Avoid secondary blue beam
- Tail Slam: Large attack, breaks platforms in Phase 3
- Line Charge: Wide line AoE, 🏃 avoid or soak
- Ground Pound: Targets center platform at ~80% Corrupted Aether, 🏃 avoid
- Burning Chains: 🔗 Chained players take DoT, break by moving apart

Phase 1:
- Transition after 2 Corrupted Aether attacks

Phase 2:
- Avoid large AoEs, no knockback
- 👾 Kill adds: 3 waves of Hakkinryu and Ginryu
- Active Time Maneuver: Mash buttons to avoid starting Phase 3 with 0 HP

Phase 3:
- 3x3 grid of platforms, outer platforms can break
- Tail Slam: Attack tail to damage Shinryu when vulnerable
- Shinryu switches sides every 2 Corrupted Aether attacks
