Antithesis Conscient: The Idol of Darkness
Players can fall off the edge of the arena. Raised players will return after a moderate delay. The arena is segmented into a 4x4 grid - pay attention to safe and unsafe squares for upcoming mechanics.

Phase 1
- Empty Wave: 💥 Unavoidable AoE damage on the entire raid.
- Unshadowed Stake: 🛡️ Tankbuster Tank is tethered and hit with two beams. First (dark) beam should be directed away from raid. Second (light) beam damage is shared based on proximity. Tank can take both hits with immunity, or share second hit with group.
- Words of Motion: 💨 Knockback from boss. Face boss away from party.
- Away With Thee: ➡️⬅️ Line AoEs target players. Spread out to avoid overlap.
- Silver Sledge: 📐 Cone AoE attack from boss. Get behind boss to avoid.
- Words of Motion + False Moonlight: 💨 Knockback + 🔴 AoE puddles appear. Handle knockback first, then avoid puddles.
- Away With Thee + AoE Circles & DPS Stack: ➡️⬅️ Line AoEs + 🔴 circle AoEs + 🤝 stack marker. Spread for lines, avoid circles, stack on marker.

Phase 2
- 👾 Adds spawn: 1 Blasphemy (north) and 2 Idolatry (east/west). Kill Idolatry adds first and at the same time, then focus Blasphemy. Don't let adds touch each other or they gain a damage buff.
- Advent Of Light: 👾 Idolatry adds cast wipe mechanic. Interrupt both casts or raid wipes.
- Empty Flood: 💥 Vicious raidwide damage once all adds are dead. Heal and shield.

Phase 3
- Unjoined Aspect: Raid gets even number of light/dark debuffs. Get hit by mechanics of the opposite color to your debuff. This flips your debuff color. Getting hit by same color is lethal.
- Words Of Unity: 💨 Knockback from boss. Face boss away from raid.
- Boundless Light & Boundless Dark: 🤝 Stack markers for light/dark groups. Two groups stack separately based on debuff.
- Words Of Entrapment: 🔗 Binds players. Spread out to break binds.
- Red & Blue Portals + Words Of Night: 🔴🔵 Colored portals spawn, then 💨 knockback. Enter portal matching your debuff color to avoid knockback.
- False Dawn: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide AoE damage.
- Empty Wave: 💥 Unavoidable AoE damage.
- Unshadowed Stake: 🛡️ Tankbuster Same mechanic as Phase 1.

Phase 4
- Boss disperses and arena darkens. After delay, knockback circle in center. Position near circle aiming at arena corner. Knockback immunity won't prevent it.
- Boss becomes a bird-like tornado in arena center with light/dark lightning.
- Unjoined Aspect: Same light/dark debuff mechanic as Phase 3.
- Threefold Grace: 🤝🤝🤝 Three stack markers. Raid splits into three groups to stack.
- Empty Wave (x2): 💥💥 Two unavoidable AoE attacks. Heal and shield.
- Enrage: Boss casts two more Empty Waves, then an enrage wipe with Empty Flood.
