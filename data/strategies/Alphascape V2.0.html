Midgardsormr
- 💠 Fight takes place in a square arena with no death walls or passive hazards.
- ⏳ The fight has multiple phases with various mechanics.

Phase 1
- Ambient Aether: 🔴 Midgardsormr spins and performs an AoE attack (point-blank AoE, donut AoE, or '+' shaped line AoEs). 💨 Avoid the AoE areas.
- Ambient Aether + Earth Shaker: 🎯 Four random players marked, 🌊 shockwaves fire from Midgardsormr to marked players. 🏃 Marked players run away.
- Akh Morn: 🎯 One player marked, 💥 multi-hit attack requiring stack.
- Ambient Aether + Thunderstorm: 🎯 Four random players marked, ⚡ AoE lightning blasts them and leaves ground AoEs. 🏃<>🏃 Marked players spread out.
- Ice-Storm: 🎯 Two players drop growing ice puddles that inflict Frostbite. 🏃 Move out of the puddles.
- Cauterize: ➡️⬅️ Midgardsormr darts across half the arena, dealing high damage. 💨 Avoid the path.
- Scarlet Thread: ➡️⬅️ Fireballs spawn straight-line AoEs in a checkerboard pattern. 💨 Avoid the AoE lines.
- Ice puddles persist until next phase, drop them out of the way.

Add Phase
- 👾 Midgardsormr leaves, an Ancient Dragon and eight Immortal Keys spawn.
- 👾 Keys must be killed to remove the dragon's invulnerability.
- Frost Breath: 📐 The dragon uses a cone attack. 💨 Avoid the cone.
- Rime Wreath: 💥 Raidwide damage.
- ⏳ If Midgardsormr's Aether reaches 100%, Protostar wipes the raid.

Final Phase
- Tail End: 🛡️ Tankbuster on main target.
- Exaflare: 💥 Staggered explosive arrow AoEs move across the arena. 💨 Avoid the AoE areas.
- Northern Cross: 💥 Raidwide damage and 🤢 Thin Ice (players slide when moving).
- Akh Rhal: 🎯 Two players take multi-hit AoE damage, 🌀 Ambient Aether combo follows.
- ⏳ Mechanics loop until Midgardsormr is defeated, including Cauterize, Exaflare, Ambient Aether + Earthshaker, Akh Morn, and Northern Cross.
