Hemitheos: Hesperos
- Elegant Evisceration: 🛡️ Tankbuster
- Setting the Stage: 🎭 Hesperos drops curtains dividing the arena into quadrants. Pay attention to which quadrant glows to prepare for the next mechanic.
- Pinax: 🌩️⛰️☔🌿 One quadrant activates with an element (lightning, lava, water, poison). 🚫 Avoid touching elemental wells to prevent status effects.
- Levinstrike Mekhane: ⚡ Lightning quadrant. 💠 Move to a corner to minimize damage from the proximity attack. Always the first Pinax attack.
- Well Mekhane: ☔ Water quadrant. 💨 Knockback from the center, avoid the middle. Always the second Pinax attack.
- Acid Mekhane: 🌿 Poison quadrant. 🏃<>🏃 Spread out, cannot survive stacking. Always the third Pinax attack.
- Lava Mekhane: ⛰️ Lava quadrant. 🤝 Stack marker on a player, share with others. Only appears after all other Pinax attacks.
- Decollation: Heavy raidwide damage
- Hell Skewer: 📐 Frontal cone AoE
- Direction Shift: 🗡️ Marker with sword = Extreme wide cone attack. 💥 One-shot if vulnerability stacked. 🧥 Marker with cloak = 💨 Knockback from marker. 💠 Avoid being pushed into spikes.
- Bloodrake: Moderate raidwide damage, then:
- Belone Coils: 🔴🟠🟢🔵 Take meteors of roles other than your own. Each missed meteor inflicts a vulnerability stack.
- Belone Bursts: 🔴🟠🟢🔵 Pop orbs of roles other than your own to avoid Thrice-come Ruin stacks.

- Later in the fight, Setting the Stage and Bloodrake mechanics will overlap, requiring handling Pinax and Belone mechanics simultaneously.
