Nophica
- Transitions arena between pink flower field and green forest, spawning damaging brambles at the edges.
- The Giving Land: 💥 Point-blank AoE if flower field, donut AoE if forest. Knocks up and inflicts Vulnerability Up.
- Matron's Harvest: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide damage, transitions arena back to yellow field.
- Reaper's Gale: ➡️⬅️ Two sets of crisscrossing line AoEs.
- Floral Haze: 🤢 Assigns Faced direction debuff, forcing players to run in that direction when debuff expires.
- Matron's Breath: 💠 Stand in same color circle as resolving beacon to avoid damage.
- Abundance: 💥 Unavoidable raidwide damage.
- Landwaker: 💥🔴 Raidwide damage and circular AoEs, used with Reaper's Gale.
- Sowing Circle: 🏃 Dodge first two "exaflare" AoEs, then move into previously unsafe area.
- Furrow: 🤝 Telegraphed stack marker.
- Heavens' Earth: 🛡️ Telegraphed AoE tankbusters on all tanks.
