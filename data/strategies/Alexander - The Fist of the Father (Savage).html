Oppressor and Faust
- This fight has a hard enrage of 8m30s. DPS needs to maximize activity. The boss has more health and damage output compared to normal mode.
- Resin Bombs: 🔴 Drops damaging green puddles that apply a strong Heavy debuff. 🏃 Watch for the cast and move to areas where puddles won't be in the way.
- Adds and Missiles: 👾 4 adds spawn. 💥 4 missiles drop from above. 🎯 Assign players to kill each add under a specific missile. 🚫 Avoid reaching 6 stacks of debuff or gain Mini.
- Hypercompressed Plasma: 🛡️ Very heavy magical tankbuster. Tanks use cooldowns before, during and after. 🩹 High raid damage occurs before and after, requiring focused healing. Happens maximum 3 times per fight.
- Jump Phase: 🦘 Both oppressors jump and land on adjacent or same side of platform. 8 resin bombs drop. 🤝 Stack middle, then 🏃<>🏃 zigzag to specified corner as bombs drop over time to avoid damage.

The damage mitigation and healing requirement for this fight is very strict. Healers need to be aware of critical health levels on tanks and raid members throughout the encounter.
