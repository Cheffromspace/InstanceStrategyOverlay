Antithesis Manifest: Voidwalker
- Players can fall off the edge of the arena. Fallen players can be raised after a moderate delay.
- Doomvoid Cleaver: 📐 Cone attack on all players. 🏃<>🏃 Spread in a circle around boss to avoid overlap. 👾 Kill adds spawned at beam ends.
- Unholy Darkness: 💥 Stack marker on random player. 🤝 Stack to soak damage. Followed by either:
- Doomvoid Guillotine: ➡️⬅️ Front/back line AoE. 💠 Move to sides.
- Doomvoid Slicer: 🔴 Donut AoE. 💥 Move into boss hitbox.
- Adds spawn in a pattern relative to Guillotine (line) or Slicer (circle). Avoid adds to prevent damage and debuffs.
- Dark Fire III: 🔴 4 players marked with AoE circles. 🏃<>🏃 Marked players spread to avoid splash damage.
- Punishing Rays: 💥 Spawns pulsating pools. 💥 Stand in pools to seal. Unsealed pools cause raid damage and debuffs.
- Spell In Waiting: ⏳ Delays the next mechanic. Listen for audio cue (rewinding clock sound) and look for special delay markers.

Spell In Waiting Overlaps
- Unholy Darkness (In Waiting): 💥 Delayed stack marker. 🤝 Prepare to stack.
- Dark Fire III (In Waiting): 🔴 Delayed 4 player AoE markers. 🏃<>🏃 Marked players prepare to spread.
- Shadow Eye (In Waiting): 🎯 Delayed look-away marker. 👥 Stand behind marked player, look away when it resolves.
- Hell Wind: 💀 Reduces 2 random players to 1 HP. Marked players cannot soak Unholy Darkness until healed.
- Position for Unholy Darkness stack. Dark Fire III move away to spread. Shadow Eye stands behind stack, all look away. Hell Wind moves away from stack until healed.

- Shadowflame: 🛡️ Tankbuster on both tanks. Mitigate when possible.
- Entropy: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage.
- Hand of Erebos: Appears on east or west side of arena.
- Empty Rage: 🔥 Massive AoE tethered to boss. 🏃 Move far from hand.
- Empty Hate: 💨 Massive knockback tethered to boss. 💥 Move close to hand or use knockback immunity.
- Doomvoid Guillotine + Doomvoid Slicer: Avoid front/back, then move into boss, while dodging adds.

More Spell In Waiting Overlaps
- Hell Wind (In Waiting): 💀 Delayed 1 HP on 2 players.
- Proximity Flare: 💥 3 players marked to spread. 🏃<>🏃 Marked move to corners.
- Shadow Eye (In Waiting): 🎯 Delayed look-away on 1 player.
- Punishing Ray: 💥 Spawns pulsating pools. 💥 Stand in pools to seal. Hell Wind players cannot soak until healed.

- Equilibrium: ⚫⚪ Black/white circle markers. 🤝 Pair up with opposite color or fatal damage.
- Doomvoid Cleaver: 📐 Quickly spread after Equilibrium to avoid overlap.
- Unholy Darkness: 💥 Stack, avoid adds from Cleaver.
- Doomvoid Slicer OR Doomvoid Guillotine: Move to sides (Guillotine) or into boss (Slicer). Avoid adds.

Final Spell In Waiting Overlaps
- Proximity Flare (In Waiting) x2: 💥 Delayed spread on 3 players twice. 🏃<>🏃 Marked move out around 8-10s left on debuff.
- Hand of Erebos (Empty Hate/Rage): Adjust based on tether (close for knockback, far for AoE).
- Unholy Darkness (In Waiting): 💥 Delayed stack marker. First Flare set moves in to stack after exploding.
- Shadow Eye x2 (In Waiting): 🎯 Delayed look-away on 2 players. 👥 Flares to 3 corners, Shadow Eyes to last corner, look away.
- Dark Fire III (In Waiting): 🔴 Delayed 3 player AoE markers. Flare/Eyes resolve, then Dark Fire spreads in middle.
- Punishing Ray + Equilibrium: Resolve Equilibrium pairs then soak pools. Failure to pair may cause wipe.

- Quietus: 💥 Raid-wide damage. ☄️ Damage Up buff on boss. Soft enrage loop:
- Cycle of Chaos: Doomvoid Guillotine > Doomvoid Slicer > Doomvoid Cleaver
- Cycle of Retribution: Doomvoid Slicer > Doomvoid Cleaver > Doomvoid Guillotine
- Cycles loop after each Quietus. Defeat before final Quietus or wipe.
