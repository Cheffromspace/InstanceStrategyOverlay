Blaster

Brawler
- 💠 Tank faces boss away from group, others stack behind boss
- Single drill: ➡️⬅️ Proximity line AoE targets random player
- Single laser: 🛡️ Tankbuster, use mitigation to solo soak
- Double laser: 💥 Tank joins group to split laser damage
- ⚠️ Lethal laser damage bypasses tank invulnerability
- 🤢 Tank swap after 3-4 stacks of Magic Vulnerability Up debuff

Swindler
- 🤢 Debuff indicates high (purple) or low (red) altitude
- 🏃 Go to opposite elevation of debuff to avoid damage and Vulnerability Up
- 💥 Unavoidable AoE damage occurs after elevation mechanic resolves
- 🤝 Orbs above player indicate number of people needed to split and nullify ring damage
- 🤢 Tanks swap if they receive Vulnerability Up from incorrect elevation

Vortexer
- 🎯 Marked player summons a water sprite at their location
- 🔴 Avoid spawning lava pools
- Knockback: 💨 Boss knocks players back
- 🌟 Use Shiva ice rings to freeze water sprite into an ice boulder
- Knockback: 💨 Another knockback from boss
- 💠 Hide behind ice boulder to avoid Ultra Flash wipe mechanic
- 🤢 Tank swap after 2 stacks of Vulnerability Up debuff
