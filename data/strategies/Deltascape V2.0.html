Catastrophe
- 👀 Watch the boss's cast bar to prepare for abilities.
- 🏃 Use the "Anti Gravity Gimbal" duty action to levitate at the start to avoid Earthquake 🔴 and Tremors 💥.
- Paranormal Wave: 📐 Frontal cone attack. 💠 Keep boss facing away from raid.
- Evilsphere: 💥 Moderate tank buster.
- Demon Eye: 🎯 👀 Turn away before cast finishes to avoid Vulnerability Up.
- 100G: ⬇️ Cancels levitation.
- Antilight:
- 🟣 Purple orbs: ⬆️ Levitate to avoid.
- 🟡 Gold orbs: ⬇️ Stay grounded.
- 🟣🟡 Both: 🤝 Stack in center safe zone. Boss will use 100G before gold orbs explode.
- Aetherial Rift: 🕳 ⬆️ Use duty action to levitate and avoid sinking. May be cast just after gold Antilight, so let them explode before levitating.
- Gravitational Explosion: 💥 ⬆️ Stack with targeted player while levitating to share damage.
- Maniacal Probe + Epicenter:
- 👾 Spawns tentacles with blue icons.
- 📍 Stand on tentacle without blue icon to avoid massive AoE from others.
- Each rotation has one more tentacle keeping its blue icon.
- -100G:
- 🟣🟡 Spawns purple and gold Antilights.
- 🏃<>🏃 Move to safe zones at opposite ends.
- 💥 Flings party into air dealing 10-15k damage.
- 🩹 Healers prepare AoE heals for follow-up AoEs.
