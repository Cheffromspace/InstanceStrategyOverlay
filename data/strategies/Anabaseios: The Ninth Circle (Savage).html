Mythic Creation: Kokytos
- Multiple phases with various mechanics. Failing any mechanic results in Damage Down debuff and other effects.
- Gluttony's Augur: 💥 High raidwide damage, mitigate.
- Ravening: 💥 Signals phase transition based on boss color, coupled with raidwide damage (Soul Surge) that should be mitigated.

Mage Phase
- Duality of Death: 🛡️ Diffuse AoE tankbusters hitting tanks with Fire (inflicts Fire Resistance Down II) and Wind (inflicts Wind Resistance Down II) AoEs, requiring tank swap or invulnerability.
- Dualspell: ➡️⬅️📐💥 Elemental attacks (Ice, Fire, Lightning) with different patterns and amplifiers. Handle Fire + Ice or Lightning + Ice combos based on assigned clock positions.
- Boss releases Mage soul with Disgorge (no cast bar) and casts Ravening to transition.

Martialist Phase
- Ascendant Fist: 🛡️ Heavy telegraphed tankbuster inflicting Concussion (DoT) and Physical Vulnerability Up, requiring tank swap.
- Archaic Rockbreaker: 💨➡️⬅️📐💥 Knockback to barriers, split damage on DPS/supports, and two sets of exploding crack AoEs to avoid.
- Front/Rear Combination: ➡️⬅️ Telegraphed point-blank/donut AoE, untelegraphed 180 degree cleave, then opposite untelegraphed AoE coinciding with 2nd set of crack AoEs.
- Archaic Demolish: 💥 Untelegraphed split damage on healers, split into light parties.

Mage + Martialist (Chimera) Phase
- Levinstrike Summoning: ⚡️🤝 Lightning orbs 1-3-5-7 and players 2-4-6-8. Orbs kicked to barriers explode, towers spawn and must be soaked (inflicts Magic Vulnerability Up) in 6-8-2-4 order.
- Scrambled Succession: 💥🤝 Players sequentially marked 1-4 with massive AoEs (Icemeld), must be isolated. Unmarked players receive medium AoEs (Firemeld) and must avoid overlapping others when their number is up.
- Two Minds: ➡️⬅️📐💥 Similar to Dualspell with random elemental pair and amplifier (Fire + Ice or Lightning + Ice).

Behemoth Phase
- Charybdis: 🔴 Four large persistent AoEs at cardinals/intercardinals.
- Comet: 🔴💠 Proximity AoEs spawn 4 rocks (2 cracked, 2 undamaged). Protect undamaged rocks.
- Beastly Bile: 💥 Split damage on furthest player, soak with 4 on a cracked rock to destroy it.
- Thunderbolt: ➡️⬅️ Large conal AoEs on closest players, aim towards persistent AoEs to avoid hitting rocks.
- Ecliptic Meteor: 💥 Raidwide meteor AoE drops on marked rock, hide behind an undamaged rock to block LoS.

Chimera Phase 2
- Duality of Death: 🛡️ Tankbuster
- Archaic Rockbreaker: 💨➡️⬅️📐💥 Knockback to barriers, split damage, two sets of exploding crack AoEs.
- Dualspell: ➡️⬅️📐💥 Always Fire + Ice, combined with exploding crack AoEs. Go to partner stack spots based on amplified element.
- Chimeric Succession: 🔴 Players 1-4 marked for massive isolated AoEs (Icemeld).
- Front/Rear Firestrikes: 🤝➡️⬅️ Stack on unmarked player (mitigate proximity damage) + 180 degree cleave.
- Two Minds: ➡️⬅️📐💥 Dualspell-like with random elements/amplifier.

Final Phases
- ⏳ Repeats mechanics from previous phases.
- Disintegration: Enrage. Defeat boss before cast finishes to avoid party wipe.
