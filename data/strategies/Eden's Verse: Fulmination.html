Ramuh
- Players can fall off the arena edge. Fallen players can be raised after a delay.
- Lane patterns on the arena floor help with various mechanics.
- Crippling Blow: 🛡️ Vicious tank-buster. Mitigate with cooldowns.
- Stratospear Summons (1): ⚡🔴 AoE circles drop lightning rods. 🏃 Move to side with shortest rod. 💥 Largest rod causes massive AoE. 💡 Collect orbs for Surge Protection (up to 3 stacks).
- Stormcloud Summons: ☁️🎯 Marks 2 players with storm clouds. 🏃<>🏃 Drop clouds apart at arena edges to avoid merge and raid damage.
- Judgement Volts: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage.
- Fury's Bolt: 🌟 Buffs Ramuh with 3 purple orbs. ⚠️ Next mechanic is more potent.

Fury's Bolt Mechanics
- Divine Judgement Volts: 💥 Fatal raid damage. 💡 Requires Surge Protection to survive. Grab an orb if needed.
- Tribunal Summons (1): 🏃➡️ Will of Ramuh dashes from south. ⚡ Stand directly beside the charge line to avoid knockback.
- Tribunal Summons (2): 🏃➡️➡️➡️ 3x Will of Ixion dash from east/west. 🚶 Move to safe lanes between charge lines.
- Stratospear Summons (2): ⚡🔴🔴🔴🔴 4 AoE circles drop rods. 🏃 Move to corner with shortest rod. 💥 Avoid large rod AoEs.

Combo Mechanics
- Thunderstorm: 💥 Constant barrages of AoEs. 🏃 Dodge and move constantly.
- Tribunal Summons (1) + Stormcloud Summons: ⚡ Stand beside Ramuh charge line. ☁️🎯 Drop clouds apart.
- Tribunal Summons (2) + Thunderstorm: 🚶 Stay in safe lanes for Ixion dashes. 🏃 Dodge AoE barrages simultaneously.
- Stratospear Summons (1) + Stormcloud Summons: ☁️🎯 Drop clouds in corners of safe side. 🤝 Stack in middle of safe side to avoid rod AoE.
- Tribunal Summons (1) + (2): 🚶 Stand in safe lanes for Ixion. ⚡ Also stand near Ramuh's center charge line.

Ramuh loops previous mechanics until defeated. Maintain Surge Protection, avoid AoEs, follow positioning for Tribunal/Stratospear Summons.
