Mythic Creation: The Phoinix
- The edge of the arena is covered in a deadly ring of fire. Form tank/healer/DPS pairs and "light parties" of tanks/melee and healers/ranged.

Phase 1
- Scorched Exaltation: 🔴 Unavoidable roomwide AoE. Mitigate and heal through.
- Heat of Condemnation: 🛡️ Tankbuster tethering two random players. Tanks take tethers and spread.
- Experimental Fireplume: 📐 Two variations - avoid multiple small orbs moving clockwise, or bait and dodge one large center orb.
- Left/Right Cinderwing: 🏃<>🏃 Cleaves left or right half of arena. Move to opposite side.
- Darkened Fire: 🔴 DPS pairs intercardinal baiting. Half get Darkened Fire, half Brightened Fire 1-8 AoEs. Position in order to debuff and kill adds.
- Devouring Brand: 🎯 Line AoEs burn in from cardinal edges. Avoid and handle with Experimental Fireplume.
- Searing Breeze: ➡️⬅️ Point blank AoEs on all players. Stack to bait then spread. Cinderwing and Heat of Condemnation follow.
- Trail of Condemnation: 🔴 Two variations - avoid fire breath and spread AoEs, or split into tank/healer and DPS pairs to handle fire lines and AoEs.

Phase 2
- 👾 Four Sunbird adds spawn. Tanks grab two each and pull apart. Kill one by one clockwise.
- Flames of Undeath: 🔴 Roomwide AoE that revives adds. Mitigate.
- Fireglide Sweep: 📐 Adds tether to and leap between players. Stretch tethers to minimize damage and debuff.
- Dead Rebirth: 🔴 Roomwide AoE. Mitigate and heal.
- Fledgling Flight: 🔴 Tanks/healers or DPS targeted by adds. Face them outward spread apart to avoid tethers.
- Ashen Eye: 🎯 Each add fires conal AoE in faced direction.
- Experimental Gloryplume: 📐 Like Fireplume plus dark orbs. Large orb splits groups, small orbs spread AoEs.
- Fountain of Fire: 🔴 Tethered puddles spawn clockwise. Soak for healing buff. Boss also drops AoEs and dashing adds.

Phase 3
- Firestorms of Asphodelos: 🔴 Heavy roomwide AoE and 3 Darkblaze Twister adds.
- Flames of Asphodelos: 💥 Conal AoEs in sequence. Stand in 2nd set then move to safe spot.
- Experimental Ashplume: 📐 Stack for large orb, spread for small orbs.
- Storms of Asphodelos: 🔴 Adds target closest player, boss tethers and cleaves. Precise positioning to bait.
- Darkblaze Twister: 🔴 Boss tethers to adds, one black. Handle Searing Breeze, Ashplume, knockback and stack/spread.
- Death's Toll: 🛡️ Players get 1, 2 or 4 stacks indicating hits needed to resolve. Position adds to take hits.
- Life's Agonies: 🤢 Players die if not healed to full when cast ends.
- ⏳ Defeat boss before Final Exaltation enrage.
