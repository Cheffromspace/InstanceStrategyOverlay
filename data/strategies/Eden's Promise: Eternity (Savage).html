Aggressive difficulty r6.png Utopian Vision: Eden's Promise
- Assign clock positions and split into light parties (1 tank, 1 healer, 2 DPS each).
- 💠 Identify safe zones and stack/spread based on attack combinations from various primals.

Phase 1
- Maleficium: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage.
- Junction (Random): 👾 Boss junctions with Shiva or Titan, adding their mechanics.
- Rapturous Reach: ➡️⬅️ Massive attack on one side, move to safe zone.
- Cast: 💠 Boss combines two random primal attacks, move to safe zones.
- Formless Judgment: 🛡️ Tankbuster on two highest enmity targets with resistance down debuffs. Swap after first hit.

Phase 2
- Junction: Shiva: 💥 🔴 📐 Boss uses Shiva mechanics like Diamond Dust (slide to safe zones), Ice Floe (stack in light parties), Ice Pillar (spread, avoid tethered ice pillars).
- Junction Titan: 💥 🔴 Boss uses Titan mechanics like Earthen Fury, Bomb Boulders (3 phases, detonate marked floor segments to destroy bomb boulders).

Phase 3
- Guardian of Eden: 👾 Boss summons a clone casting Obliteration. Stack to share damage.
- Cast/Stock: 💥 🔴 📐 Boss uses random primal attacks, either immediately or stores for later.
- Classical Sculpture: 🤝 ➡️⬅️ Healers take stack markers, statues fire numbered line attacks at players.
- Palm of Temperance: 💨 Clone's knockback attack. Move to attack side or use knockback resistance.
- Beastly Sculptures: 🔴 🤢 Lion sculptures tether and fire breath attacks applying Fire Resistance Down. Coordinate positioning to avoid overlap.

Phase 4
- Recap of previous mechanics.
- Beat enrage cast to trigger checkpoint cutscene.

Aggressive difficulty r6.png Oracle of Darkness
Phase 1
- Hell's Judgment: 🤢 HP reduced to single digit. Heal up.
- Shockwave Pulsar: 💥 Raid-wide damage.
- Spell-in-Waiting: ⏱️ Delayed spell cast.
- Dark Water III/Dark Eruption: 🏃<>🏃 🤝 Split into light parties for stack markers / spread to assigned positions for AoEs.
- Darkest Dance: 💨 Knockback attack on furthest player. Main tank baits, party stacks on boss.
- Basic Relativity: 🤢 ⏳ Debuffs based on roles (stack/spread). Position and turn to handle hourglass lasers. Yellow hourglass has point-blank AoE.
- Singular Apocalypse: ➡️⬅️ 💥 Avoid light pillar moving around arena.
- Cataclysm: 🏃 Boss leaps to one side for massive AoE. Move opposite.
- Black Halo: 🛡️ Frontal cone tankbuster. Use invuln.
- Shellcrusher: 🤝 Stack to split damage.
- Spirit Taker: 🏃<>🏃 Spread to assigned positions to avoid splash damage.

Phase 2
- Intermediate Relativity: 🤢 ⏳ More debuffs (stack/spread, knockback, gaze). Use floor markers to position for multiple Return IV snapshots.
- Dual Apocalypse: ➡️⬅️ 💥 Two light beams, avoid their intersection. Dodge Cataclysm.
- Somber Dance: 🏃 Boss jumps to furthest then closest player. Tanks bait.

Phase 3
- Advanced Relativity: 🤢 ⏳ Combines Basic and Intermediate Relativity. Position using floor markers for debuff order.

Phase 4
- Triple Apocalypse: ➡️⬅️ Three light beams. Find safe spot at beam intersection.
- Terminal Relativity: 💥 🤢 Heavy raid damage and Quietus debuffs. Mitigate and heal through.
- Memory's End: ⏳ Enrage. Defeat before cast finishes.
