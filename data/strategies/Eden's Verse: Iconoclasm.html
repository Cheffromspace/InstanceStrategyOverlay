Antithesis Conscient: The Idol of Darkness
- Players can fall off the edge of the arena and die, but they can be raised after some time.

Phase 1
- Empty Wave: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage via an outward travelling shockwave of dark energy.
- Unshadowed Stake: 🛡️ Tankbuster tethers the main tank for a devastating straight-line AoE. Aim the tether away from the group and use mitigation cooldowns.
- Words of Motion: 🔴 Summons damaging bird clouds that move down lanes. Move out of the lane of the first two clouds, wait for them to fire, then move into an empty lane.
- Red & Blue Portals: 🔴🔵 Creates portals that bird clouds enter and fire from. Stand in the side-lane that matches the second portal the clouds entered to avoid damage.
- Away With Thee: 🎯 Places teleportation markers on all players. Ensure you do not teleport off the edge or into danger.
- False Twilight: 🔴 Spawns AoE circles under random players. Spread out to avoid damage.
- Silver Sledge: 💥 Targets two players with stack markers. Split into two groups to absorb damage. Marked players should not stack together.
- Unjoined Aspect: 🌗🌚 Applies a light or dark effect to all players.
- Words of Night: 🌗🌚 Spawns unavoidable light/dark bird clouds. Stand in the lane that matches the opposite color of your aspect to swap aspects and avoid fatal damage.

Phase 2
- Two Idolatry Adds spawn that must be killed within a time limit. Tanks should grab enmity and the adds can be stacked together.
- Away With Thee: 🎯 Aim your teleport marker to avoid the adds' donut AoEs.
- Silver Shot: 🔴 Targets all players with AoE circles. Spread out to avoid splash damage on teammates.
- Explosion: ⏳ Wipes the raid if either add finishes casting.
- Empty Flood: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage signals the end of the phase.

Phase 3
- Unjoined Aspect + Red & Blue Portals: 🌗🌚🔴🔵 Find the inner lane opposite your aspect debuff color, then stand on a tile intersected by two opposite color portals.
- Black Smoke: 💨 Spawns meteor towers that must be soaked. Use Away With Thee to teleport into a tower. If towers are not soaked, the raid takes fatal damage.
- Mechanics will now repeat until the boss is defeated.
