Cruise Chaser
- 🔄 Fight has multiple phases with different mechanics and abilities.

Phase 1
- Left/Right Laser Sword: ➡️⬅️ Move to the safe side to avoid the laser beam AoE.
- Optical Sight: 🎯 Three different patterns: 1) Move to edge for center AoE, 2) Stack center then spread for Hawk Blasters, 3) Stay center then dodge rotating blasts.
- E.D.D: Off-tank picks up add, destroy armor to prevent Overboost buff, then burn down.
- Spin Crusher: Move behind boss to avoid frontal cleave.
- Whirlwind: 💥 Raid-wide damage, mitigate and heal through.
- Optical Sight (Super Hawk Blasters): 🎯 Marked players spread to avoid stacking Magic Vulnerability Up debuffs.
- 🔄 Mechanics repeat until boss reaches 75% HP.

Phase 2
- Limit Cut: 🛡️ Boss becomes invulnerable and gains damage up buff.
- Numbers: Players numbered 1-8, odds blue, evens red. Pair up in order.
- Alpha Sword: Odd players look away from center to avoid boss frontal cone.
- Super Blassty Charge: 💥 Line charge from odd to even player. Even players face attack to avoid knockback.
- Assign arena locations for odd/even pairs to handle Alpha Sword/Blassty Charge mechanics.
- Photon: Reduces HP to single digits, heal quickly.
- Whirlwind: 💥 Raid-wide damage.
- Laser Sword: ➡️⬅️ Avoid side AoE.
- Blast Off: Interact with ducts to jump on boss's back before arena is destroyed. Avoid center AoE when landing in new arena.

Phase 3
- Limit Cut: 🛡️ Boss invulnerable, gains damage up, spawns 4 orbs.
- Lapis Lazuli: Destroy orbs before Eternal Darkness or wipe.
- Laser X Sword: ➡️⬅️ 🛡️ Heavy tankbuster, split between tanks using cooldowns.
- Aetheroplasm: Tethered orbs move to random players, drop puddles. Don't overlap puddles to avoid explosions.
- Assault Cannons: 📐 Avoid line AoEs.
- Eternal Darkness: 🤢 Pyretic debuff if orbs dead, otherwise wipe. Manage HP to avoid death from debuff.
- Mechanics like Whirlwind, Spin Crusher, Optical Sight, Photon, Laser X Sword, E.D.D add repeat.
- Limit Cut (Plasma Shield): 🛡️ 🌟 Boss invulnerable, spawn Plasma Shield attackable from front.
- Super Blassty Charge: 💥 Spreads heavy debuff, avoid overlap.

Phase 4
- Blast Off: Use ducts again to reach new arena with 3 Multifield Pillars. Avoid destroying all pillars or platform collapses.
- GA-100: Off-tank marked for proximity blast, separate from group.
- Optical Sight: 🎯 Avoid AoEs and hitting pillars.
- Laser X Sword: ➡️⬅️ 🛡️ Heavy tankbuster.
- Assault Cannons: 📐 Avoid line AoEs.
- Photon/Whirlwind: 💥 Raid-wide damage.
- Propeller Wind: 💨 Hide behind pillars to avoid confusion debuff.
- Limit Cut: 🛡️ Same as before, watch pillars.
- Limit Cut (Plasma Shield): 🛡️ 🌟 Burn Plasma Shield, one Super Blassty Charge hit will destroy pillars.
- Repeat mechanics until boss is defeated or enrage wipes the party.
