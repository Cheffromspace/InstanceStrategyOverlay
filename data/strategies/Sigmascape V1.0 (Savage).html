Phantom Train
- Watch for missing arena railings to avoid falling off.
- Main tank must stay close to boss to avoid Connectivity debuff stacks.
- Ghost Blocks: 🕳️ Avoid impassable obstacle squares. Players inside when ghosts spawn will die.
- Spotlights: 🎯 🏃 Marked players must run to avoid circular AoEs that chase and detonate. Use to destroy Ghost Blocks strategically.
- Diabolical Whistle: Triggers various ghost-related mechanics:
    - 💨 Knockback Ghost on side of arena, use Ghost Blocks to avoid knockback
    - Doom Strike 🛡️ Tank buster on main target
    - 👻 Grabby Ghosts move across arena, avoid or get sent to Passenger Car
    - 👻 Stalker Ghosts tether and chase players, destroy with Spotlights
- Head-On Collision: 💨 Ghost Train rams arena for proximity damage and knockback.
- Diabolical Wind: 🔴 Marked players drop AoE circles, spread to avoid overlap damage.
- Acid Rain: 💥 Unavoidable raid-wide damage.
- Diabolical Headlamp: ➡️⬅️ 🤝 Marked player drops line stack marker, causes knockback in savage.
- Diabolical Light: 💠 Marked tank and healer drop delayed proximity AoEs, move south. Followed by Doom Strike tank buster.

Add Phase
- 👾 Ghost Train pulls up alongside, enemies jump to arena.
- 🔴 Diabolical Wind AoE circles on all DPS, then tanks/healers. Spread out.
- ⚔️ After defeating adds, destroy Doom Chimney on Ghost Train for DPS check.
- Throttle: 💨 Arena fills with smog. Get caught by correct ghost for role to enter Passenger Car:
    - 👻 Tanks: Large black ghosts
    - 👻 Healers: Small purple ghosts
    - 👻 DPS: Medium red ghosts
- Defeat ghost inside Passenger Car to escape and resume fight.

Phantom Train
- Mechanics repeat and combine in more punishing ways after add phase.
- Avoid Ghost Blocks, Grabby Ghosts, Stalker Ghosts, and arena knockbacks.
- Handle tank busters, line stacks, proximity AoEs, and raid damage.
- Destroy Stalker Ghosts with Spotlights while avoiding Diabolical Wind AoEs.
- Enrage will wipe platform with unavoidable beam. Survive in Passenger Car.
