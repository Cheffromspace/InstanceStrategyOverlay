Hydaelyn:
Phase 1:
- Heros's Radiance: 💥 Raid-wide AoE
- Shining Saber: 🤝 Stack to spread damage
- Crystallize: Boss displays element + weapon, weapon attack happens first
  - 💧 Water: 🎯 Healers, 🤝 stack in light parties
  - 🪨 Rock: 🎯 Random player, 🤝 party stack
  - 🧊 Ice: 🎯 All players, 🏃<>🏃 spread to clock positions
  - 🔴 Dancer Chakrams: 🍩 Donut AoE, stand close
  - 🟢 White Mage Staff: 🔴 Circle AoE, move away
  - 🔵 Paladin Sword & Shield: ➕ Plus AoE, stand in intercardinals
- Magos's Radiance: 💥 Raid-wide AoE
- Mousa's Scorn: 🛡️ Shared tankbuster
- Aureole: 💥 Wide AoE, safe on boss' sides
- Lateral Aureole: 💥 Wide AoE, safe in front/behind boss
Boss uses Magos's Radiance/Mousa's Scorn > Aureole > Crystallize x2, then the other ability > Aureole > Crystallize, then Phase 2

Phase 2:
- 6 crystals spawn (3 north, 3 south), one set glows 🎯 DPS/healers focus glowing crystals
- 2 Echo adds spawn (east/west), ⚔ tanks grab one each, pull away from glowing crystals
- Echoes tether to closest 3 crystals, tethering to live/glowing crystals raises Conviction meter
- After glowing crystals die, tanks move Echoes, finish crystals, then kill Echoes
- 🩹 Heal tanks, Echoes have strong auto-attacks
- Conviction: Cast at 100 meter (raid wipe) or phase end, 💥 heavy raid-wide damage (25k at max)

Phase 3:
- Halo: 💥 Raid-wide AoE
- Lightwave 1: 4 crystals (3 north/south, 1 opposite), 2 light waves (center + west/east)
  - 🏃 Avoid light waves, 📐 hide behind crystals to avoid line of sight damage
- Infralateral Arc: Boss cleaves cone at random healer > tank > DPS
  - 🤝 Group up by role, stand behind different crystals to split damage and avoid next attack
- Lightwave 2: 5 crystals (center + corners), 2 edge light waves + 1 center wave
  - 📐 First 4 collisions: hide behind center crystal, 5th: use corner crystal behind boss
- Hero's Glory: 📐 Boss cleaves in front, stand behind her (used twice in phase)

Phase 4:
- Mechanics from Phase 1 return with additions:
- Heros's Sundering: 🛡️📐 Cone tankbuster
- Parhelion/Subparhelion: Parhelion: 3 sets of chakrams drop line AoEs in order
  - 💧 Water Crystallize resolves with first Subparhelion (chakram line AoEs), 🤝 split into light parties and 🏃 run
- Parhelic Circle: 🟢 Snowflake symbol spawns 🔴 AoEs in middle and line tips, avoid middle or stand on smaller lines

Phase 5:
- Lightwave: 4 light waves (2 each direction, 1 center + 1 left/right), 1 safe corner
- Echoes: 🤝 Stack for multi-hit marker, then spread (except tanks who 🛡️ share tankbuster)
- 4 more light waves (center safe) + 💧 Water Crystallize > Shining Saber (resolves first)
- Phase repeats, then hard enrage (Heros's Radiance)
