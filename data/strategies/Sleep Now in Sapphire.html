Sapphire Weapon
Phase 1: Initial Attacks
- Tail Swing: 💥 Massive point-blank AoE. 🏃 Dodge with Pyretic Booster.
- Optimized Judgement: 💥🍩 Donut AoE.
- Activate: 👾 Spawns Regula's Image adds. 🏃 Avoid their 270° AoEs.
- Optimized Ultima: 💥💥 Unavoidable damage. Mitigate with Aetherial Aegis.
Phase 2: Disappearing Act
- Swiftbreach: 💥💥 Unavoidable damage. Mitigate with Aetherial Aegis.
- Sapphire Ray: 💥📏 Line AoE. 🏃 Move to sides.
- Siderays: 💥🪓 Side cleave AoEs. 🏃 Move in front.
- Plasma Shot: 💥💥 Heavy unavoidable damage. Mitigate with Aetherial Aegis.
Phase 3: Turret Deployment
- Turret: 👾 Spawns Magitek Turrets. Destroy rows with Ultima Buster.
- Plasma Cannon: 💥🎯 Massive circle AoE. 🏃 Dodge with Pyretic Booster.
Phase 4: Final Phase
- Flood Ray: ⏱️ Boss casts slowly. Defeat adds before cast.
- Magitek Turrets: 👾 Two groups spawn. Defeat with Ultima Buster.
- Ceruleum Servants: 👾 Four spawn with line AoEs. Defeat all to expose boss.

General G-Warrior Strategy:
- Use Pyretic Booster unless low HP
- Keep Aether Mine debuff up
- Spam Final Flourish Combo
- Use Aether Cannon if low EP
- Heal with Auto Restoration
- Mitigate unavoidable damage with Aetherial Aegis
