Rubicante (Extreme)

Players can fall off arena edges 💀, but can be resurrected

Assign spread positions:
- Tanks/healers on cardinals, DPS on intercardinals
- Form 2 light parties: 1 tank, 1 healer, 1 melee DPS, 1 ranged DPS
- Assign 4 adjacent arena slices, each with 1 melee/tank + 1 ranged/healer

Phase 1:
- Inferno: Moderate 💥 raidwide damage
- Ordeal of Purgation: Boss summons 3 concentric circles (red, purple, red) with blue fuse segments
- 1-2 fireballs travel along fuses, hitting 🔺 (blue) or 🟧 (orange) panels
- 🔺 = large conal AoE, 🟧 = half-room AoE on closest side
- Red circle fuses always straight, purple can be straight or bent
- Circles may ↪️ rotate 1/8th, changing fuse and panel positions
- Find safe area before 🤢 Penance debuff expires and 🔗 immobilizes
- Arch Inferno: Persistent AoE in middle + 2 rotating outer AoEs
- Split into light parties N/S, rotate to dodge AoEs
- Handle mechanics: healer stack, role stack (tank/melee, healer/ranged), or 🎯 spread
- 🏃<>🏃 Spread for Radial Flagration conal AoEs at end

Adds Phase:
- Defeat 5 Flamesent + 1 Greater Flamesent before Rubicante's Power reaches 100
- Greater Flamesent: Kill first to stop 💥 raidwides + 🤢 Burns DoT
- N Flamesent: Avoid AoE telegraphs
- NW/NE Flamesent: Tanks grab 🔗 tethers (tankbusters)
- SW/SE Flamesent: Non-tanks grab 🔗 tethers, face away to avoid cleaving
- Blazing Rapture: Unavoidable 💥 raidwide + 🤢 Burns DoT after adds

Phase 2 (Winged Form):
- Flamespire Brand:
- 4 players (all DPS or tanks/healers) get 💥 flare debuff
- 1 other role player gets 🤝 stack debuff
- All get 🎯 spread debuff
- Resolve flare/stack → spread
- Flamerake: Cross AoE (cardinals or intercardinals) cascades outward 2x
- Move to dodge AoEs while resolving Flamespire Brand debuffs
- Scalding Signal / Scalding Ring: Dodge point-blank or donut AoE + line AoEs at clock spots
- Sweeping Immolation: Dodge half-room cleave + 🏃<>🏃 spread (multi flowers) or 🤝 stack (1 flower)
- Position to avoid follow-up line AoEs from prior mechanic
- Dualfire: 🛡️ Massive tankbuster cleaves
- Inferno: 🎯 Untelegraphed AoEs + 🤢 Bleed & Magic Vuln Up

Cloaked Form:
- Handle more Ordeal of Purgation mechanics
- Inferno: Normal 💥 raidwide (no spread)

Final Winged Form:
- Flamespire Claw: Boss cleaves players 1-8, tethers 7 & 8
- Tethered players explode at 5 stacks 💀
- After cleave, grab tether to reset stacks
- Odd numbers NW, even NE, party + tethers SW/SE
- Inferno: 🎯 Spread
- Scalding Signal/Ring, Scalding Fleet, Sweeping Immolation again

Final Cloaked Form:
- Handle final Ordeal of Purgation mechanics
- Inferno (enrage): Defeat boss before cast ends ⏳
