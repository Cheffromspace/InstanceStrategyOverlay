Ratfinx Twinkledinks
- 💠 Tank boss in the corner to avoid large 💥 AOE from bombs.
- When boss changes to giant form:
- Boss applies 💥 debuff 4x, 🛡️ stunning MT on 4th.
- 🛡️ OT provoke to intercept tankbuster after 4th debuff.
- MT can retake boss or 🛡️ swap on each Concussion.
- 👾 Tank adds that spawn in corners.
- Red-orange marker: 🏃 💥 Run away from party to drop large damaging puddle.
- Tower circle:
- Standing near spawns 💥 puddles needed for mechanics.
- 💥 Avoid standing too long to prevent damage.

Red Puddle Phase
- 💥 Red puddle turns you into gorilla with knockback skill.
- Bombs: 💨 Use knockback to push bombs into far corner before 💥 explosion.
- 💥 More bombs spawn each time (1 > 2 > 3).
- 💠 Hug opposite wall if bombs near adjacent corners.

Violet Puddle Phase
- 💥 Violet puddle turns you into bird.
- Boost: 🏃 Become bird to avoid groundpounds when boss is giant.
- Bird skills:
- Skill 1: Deal damage
- Skill 2: Turn back to normal form

This is one of the few normal difficulty duties where a tank swap is recommended to safely resolve the Concussion mechanic.
