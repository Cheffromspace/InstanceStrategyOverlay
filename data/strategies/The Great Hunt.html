Rathalos:
- Cannot be tanked, watch animations and move accordingly
- Stick to left flank to avoid 🔄 Tail Swipe and frontal/rear Whipping Bite

Phase 1 (Grounded):
- Charging Bite: 🎯 player, 🏃 dodge charge
- Fireball: 🎯 player, move away to avoid 💥 splash and 🤢 Burns
- Evasive Breath: Short tell, 🏃 avoid frontal 📐 fire breath

Phase 2 (Adds):
- 👾 Adds enter, tank them near Rathalos for mutual damage
- Focus 👾 Coeurl first, turn away from party
- At 70%, 👾 Garula enters, 🛡️ tank it facing away
- 🎯 Mount Rathalos, pass QTE while party kills Garula
- Fail to kill Garula or mount = raid wipe

Phase 3 (Flying):
- 🤢 Scalebound debuff: healing reduced to 0 (except 🌟 Mega Potions)
- Fill Duty Gauge to down Rathalos, focus damage on tail
- Claw Swipe: 🎯 player charge, 🏃 dodge or get 🤢 Poison
- Mangle/Sweeping Flames: Frontal 📐 AoEs, 🏃 behind or to sides
- Fireball: 🤝 Stack marker, leaves 🔥 puddle
- Tanks can invuln Fireball solo if targeted
