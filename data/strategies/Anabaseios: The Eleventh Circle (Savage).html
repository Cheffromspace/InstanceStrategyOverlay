Ephemeral Justice: Themis
- The arena has diagonal lines and diamonds for positioning. Assign clock positions, light parties, and DPS/support partners. Most failed mechanics inflict Damage Down.
- Eunomia: 💥 High raidwide damage with Bleeding DoT. 🩹 Mitigate and heal through damage and DoT.
- Dike: 🛡️ Telegraphed heavy tankbusters on highest enmity targets. 💥 Tank swap and use heavy mitigation. Inflicts Dark/Light Resistance Down.
- Jury Overruling: ➡️⬅️ Line AoEs with secondary effects based on Light/Dark imbue. 🏃 Spread to clock positions, avoid AoEs, then stack with light party (light) or partner (dark).
- Upheld Overruling: 🔗 Tethers and jumps to a player with follow-up AoEs. 🏃 Non-tank tether: stack mid then max melee in light parties. Tank tether: tank goes mid, party max melee, then stack with partners.
- Divisive Overruling: ➡️⬅️ Line AoE with additional effects based on Light/Dark imbue. 🏃 Max melee in light parties (light) or dodge into original AoE and stack with partners (dark).
- Styx: 💥 Multi-hit heavy stack damage on a random player, increases by 1 hit each time. 🩹 Mitigate and heal the target.
- Arcane Revelation: ➡️⬅️ Summons dark/light panels that shoot line AoEs based on imbued element. 🏃 Split into light parties on safe sides, knockback from center, resolve secondary effects.

Shadowed Messengers
- 👾 Summons adds that cast Divisive Ruling and Upheld Ruling. Tank faces boss towards/opposite light add.
- 🏃 Split into light parties on safe spots. Dodge dark AoEs from adds, resolve boss mechanic, stack mid for light tether then dodge point-blank/donut AoEs.
- Jury Overruling immediately follows. Find clock positions.
- Styx hits 6 times. Mitigate.
