Rafflesia

Phase 1 and 2
- Tank Rafflesia in the center with tank in front and party behind.
- Bloody Caress: 📐 Frontal conal AoE applying 🤢 Vulnerability Up debuff to tank. 🛡️ Tank swap every 2-3 stacks.
- Briary Growth: 👾 Spawns 🔴 Dark Matter Bulbs at edges dealing damage and slowing. 🔥 Burn center bulb quickly.
- Floral Trap: 🎯 Marks a player, 🔗 pulling others to boss. Marked player stays behind boss, others move in front temporarily.
- Devour: 😮 Absorbs players in melee range after Floral Trap, gaining damage buff.
- Spit: 💥 Releases and damages absorbed players.
- Blighted Bouquet: ☠️ 🚫 Instant kill at 70% HP if moving, attacking or using abilities.
- Viscid Emission: 🍯 Applies 🤢 Honey debuff, 👾 spawning Dark Matter Hornet add to kill quickly. Player with debuff must be Devoured before phase ends.

Phase 3
- Leafstorm: 💥 AoE damage to all players at phase start.
- Dark Matter Slugs: 🐌 3 adds spawn after Leafstorm. Kill with caster Limit Break or let one eat 3 Acidic Honey to become super slug, then kill.
- Acid Rain: 👾 Spawns 🍯 Acidic Honey adds under 3 players applying 🤢 stacking Acid Cloud debuff. Move out of AoEs. 🤝 Stack to let slug eat Acidic Honeys.
- Swarm: 💥 Damages tank and applies 4 debuffs.
- Thorn Whip: 🔗 Tethers increasing number of players. Move apart vertically to break.
- Rotten Stench: ➡️⬅️ Linear AoE during Thorn Whip, damage split between hit players. 🤝 Stack up and down.

Ignore Acid Cloud debuffs and burn boss, or have slug eat Acidic Honeys. Rafflesia stops using several abilities in final phase.
