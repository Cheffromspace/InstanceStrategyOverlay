Zoraal Ja 🐲
Preparation
- Form light parties: 1 🛡️ tank, 1 💚 healer, 2 ⚔️ DPS each.
- Pair groups: 1 support & 1 DPS each.

Phase 1
- Actualize: 💥💥 Raid-wide damage. Mitigate.
- Multidirectional Divide: Splits arena into quadrants (non-damaging).
- Forward/Backward Half: 🪓 Cleave + 💥📐 quadrant AoEs. 🏃 Dodge cleave and AoEs.
- Stack for 2nd Multidirectional Divide.
- Regicidal Rage: 🔗 Tank tethers. Tanks grab tethers, split up.
- Dawn of an Age 1: 💥💥 Massive raid-wide, arena shrinks, platforms appear.
- Vohk: Places sword patterns on platforms.
- Sync: Highlights 2 adjacent platforms, slides sword patterns onto arena. Find safe spot or react last second.
- Half Full: 🪓 Cleave + 💥 synced AoEs. 🏃 Dodge.
- Greater Gateway: 🔗 Tethers connect outer platforms to main (2 💜 standard, 1 🔥 fire, 1 💨 wind).
- Blade Warp: Swords pass through tethers, gain properties.
- 💜 Standard: 2 swords, 💥📏 line AoEs where they exit.
- 🔥 Fire: 💥📏 line AoE taking 3 rows ➡️ 2 safe spots.
- 💨 Wind: 💥📏 line AoE that pushes players ➡️ 6 safe spots. Can 💨🚫 knockback resist narrow safe spot.
- Players get 🔺 pyramid marker: 💥 pulse AoE on their square. Spread to different squares.
- Actual Eyes: 💥💥 Raid-wide damage.

Phase 2
- Projection of Turmoil: 🤢 Debuff on all players for ~50s.
- Beam passes through arena triggering stack explosions. Grant 2s 🤢⬆️ magic vuln.
- Intended: split into light parties, stagger hits (💚 > 🛡️ > ⚔️).
- Cheese 🧀: stand in wall as beam passes.
- Bitter Whirlwind: 🛡️💥 Triple tankbuster. 🛡️🔁 Tank swap or invuln.
- Dawn of an Age 2: Single platform appears.
- Form DPS/support pairs on 3rd row.
- 4 DPS or 4 supports marked with "Enumeration".
- Marked player knocked up, unmarked to adjacent platform.
- Vohk: Larger sword appears on each platform.
- Sync: Slides sword to other platform in same position. Always a safe melee spot near boss.
- Aero III: 🔺 Pyramid spread. Groups take specific positions.
- Tornado pushes players based on touch angle, grants 💨🚫 wind resist down.
- Zoro Jaw faces a platform for Forward/Backward Half. Unsafe side uses tornado to cross.
- Duty's Edge: 💥📏 4-hit party stack line AoE. 🤝 Stack, mitigate and heal.
- Burning Chains: 🔗 Tethers DPS to random support. Spread to break. One side uses tornado to cross if needed.
- Actual Eyes: 💥💥 Raid-wide damage launches players, returns to original arena.

Phase 3
- Projection of Triumph 2: Donuts & Point blank lines passing through.
- Paired with Half Circuit 🪓 cleave + in/out mechanic.
- Tanks grab 🔗 tethers, circle boss then split. Party dodges beams + Half Circuit.
- Projection of Turmoil 2: Same as first, Zoro Jaw cleaves during. Cheese 🧀 strat still works.
- 🛡️💥 Tankbuster. Invuln or swap.
- Dawn of an Age 3: Modified Dawn 1.
- Vohk + Sync (no half room AoE).
- Spread for Aero III 🔺 pyramid while dodging sync AoE.
- Duty's Edge: 🤝 Stack.
- Greater Gateway: Same as first.
- Multidirectional Divide & Forward/Backward Half like start.
- Final Actual Eyes - enrage at 11:00.
