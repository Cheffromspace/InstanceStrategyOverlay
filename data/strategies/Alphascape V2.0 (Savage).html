Midgardsormr
- Fight takes place in a square arena with no death-walls or passive hazards.
- Ambient Aether: Midgardsormr frequently draws in aether by spinning horizontally or vertically. The spin combination determines the upcoming AoE attack:
    - Horizontal + Horizontal: Point-blank AoE. Move away.
    - Horizontal + Vertical: Donut AoE. Move into hitbox.
    - Vertical + Vertical: '+' shaped AoEs. Move to far corner.
    - Vertical + Horizontal: 'x' shaped AoEs. Move to N/S/E/W edge.
- Getting hit inflicts high damage and debuffs. React to spin combinations.

Opening Phase
- Tail End: 🛡️ Tankbuster on MT requiring mitigation or tank swap.
- Time Immemorial: Applies 🔵 Death from Above (attacks from flying apply Doom) or 🟡 Death from Below (attacks from grounded apply Doom) to tanks.
- Other abilities: 🔴 Ice-Storm, 📐 Akh Rhal, 🤢 Horrid Roar, ➡️⬅️ Northern Cross.

Add Phase
- Ancient Dragon adds spawn. Handle add mechanics.

Third Phase
- Crimson Breath: 💀 Fatal cone AoE on random player. Requires Ancient Bulwark to survive. Bulwark holder should be away from group and pass to marked player.
- Mechanics: 🔴 Crimson Orbs, 🛡️ Hot Tail.
- 🛡️ Cauterize + 🤢 Horrid Roar + 💥 Exaflare: Midgardsormr leaves arena and dives with AoEs. Players drop periodic AoEs. Arrow AoEs explode across arena.

Final Phase
- Time Immemorial: Reapplies Death from Above/Below. Tanks react if debuffs swap.
- 🤢 Horrid Roar + 💥 Thunderstorm: Players marked with thunder AoEs and drop AoEs. Dodge without overlapping.
- Combos: 💥 Exaflare + 🔴 Ice-Storm + 📐 Akh Morn.
- 📐 Akh Rhal + 🛡️ Tail End: AoEs erupt under 2 DPS, then tankbuster.
- ⏳ Enrage: 💥 Exaflare + 🔴 Ice-Storm + hard-hitting 📐 Akh Morn. Spread to survive longer.
