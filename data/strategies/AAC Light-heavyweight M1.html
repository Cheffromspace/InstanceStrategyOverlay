Black Cat 🐈‍⬛
Main Phase 🔄
- Arena is a 4x4 grid. Players may fall off to their deaths. ☠️
- Bloody Scratch: 💥💥 Raid-wide physical damage.
- One-two Paw: 🪓🪓 Two sequential half-room cleaves. 👀 Watch for glowing claws.
- Black Cat Crossing: 💥📐💥📐 Two sets of late telegraphed conal AoEs. 👀 Watch for glowing lines.
- Biscuit Maker: 🛡️ Telegraphed physical tankbuster. Mitigate, ⚔️💚 avoid.
- Clawful: 🤝 Stack marker AoE on random player. Stack to share damage.
- Mouser: 9️⃣ Sequential AoEs hitting arena tiles. 🏃 Identify and move to safe tiles.
- Elevate and Eviscerate: 🔗 Tether and knockup on random players. Position for safe knockback onto non-damaged tiles.
- Shockwave: 💨 Telegraphed radial knockback. Position near boss or use knockback resist.
- Predaceous Pounce: 💥📏💥🎯 Sequential line and circle AoEs. 🏃 Dodge in order of appearance.
- Leaping One-two Paw: 🪓🪓 One-two Paw with boss movement. 👀 Watch for tether indicating new position.
- Leaping Black Cat Crossing: 💥📐💥📐 Black Cat Crossing with boss movement. 👀 Watch for tether indicating new position.
- Grimalkin Gale: 🏃↔️🏃 Spread AoE markers on all players. Spread out.

Copycat 👾
- Boss summons untargetable clone.
- Clone: Elevate and Eviscerate. Boss: Overshadow (🤝 stack line AoE).
- Elevate targets ignore stack and position for knockback. Others 🤝 stack.
- Second use: Clone does Predaceous Pounce instead.

Avoidable AoEs inflict 🤮⬆️ Vulnerability Up or Sustained Damage. 🏃 Dodge!
