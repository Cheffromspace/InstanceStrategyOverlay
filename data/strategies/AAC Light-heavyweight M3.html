Brute Bomber
Phase 1
- Arena surrounded by 🔥 fire. Avoid 💥 AoEs or get 🤮⬆️ Vulnerability Up or 🤢 Sustained Damage.
- Brutal Impact: 💥💥 Four-hit raid-wide damage.
- Knuckle Sandwich: 🛡️ Dual tankbuster on off-tank. 🛡️🔁 Tanks stack to share.
- Brutal Lariat: 🪓 180° cleave from glowing arm. 🏃 Go to opposite flank.
- Murderous Mist: 🪓 270° frontal cleave. 🏃 Get behind boss.
- Barbarous Barrage: 🔗💥 Four towers to soak, knockback. 1 player per tower, 🏃 position for knockback.
- Fire Spin: 💥📐 Rotating frontal cone AoE. 🏃 Move to first hit area after it resolves.
- Fuses of Fury: 👾💥 Bombs with short/long fuses. 🏃 Avoid AoEs, short fuses explode first.

Phase 2 (Doping Draught 🌟)
- Lariat Combo: 🪓🪓 Two Brutal Lariats. 🏃 May hit same or opposite sides.
- Brutal Burn: 🤝 Stack marker AoE. 🤝 Stack to share damage.
- Brutal Impact: 💥💥💥 Now six-hit raid-wide.
- Infernal Spin: 💥📐🍩 Fire Spin + cascading AoEs + player-targeted circles. 🏃 Dodge cones, donuts, circles.
- Fuses of Fury + Lariat Combo: 👾💥🪓 Bombs with boss cleaves. 🏃 Dodge short-fuse bombs, then move to safe spot from long-fuse and cleave.
- Fuses of Fury + Barrage + Mist: 👾💥🔗🪓 Bombs with towers and cleave. 🔗 Soak towers, 🏃 dodge bombs & Mist cleave.
