Nidhogg:

Phase 1 - Normal Dragon:
- Flyover AoE: 🏃 Avoid AoE
- Hot Wings: 🏃 Stand in safe line along Nidhogg's body
- Deafening Bellow: 💥 Party-wide damage, stay in AoE heal range
- Holes: 🏃 Run out of holes that spawn under players
- Fire Trail: 🏃 Avoid Nidhogg's fire trail, stack center then spread

Phase 2 - Adds (DPS Check):
- Kill adds before Nidhogg's blast or wipe
- Middle add: 🎯 Avoid unmarked frontal AoE swipe
- Side adds: ⚔ Tank to sides, 🩹 assign one healer per tank
- Use LB and healer DPS if needed

Phase 3 - Humanoid Form:
- Akh Morn: 🛡️ Tank buster, 🩹 single target heal/shield
- Geirskogul: 🏃 Avoid line AoE from Nidhogg to random player
- Meteor Circles: 🏃<>🏃 Spread to sides, run out immediately after hit
- Room-wide AoE: 🏃 Run far from target, avoid additional AoEs, 🩹 AoE heal/shield

Phase 4 - Fire Dragon:
- Akh Morn: 🤝 Stack and stay on targeted player until all bolts strike, 🩹 heavy damage
- Fireballs (3): 💠 Stand in row/column without bombs, 🏃 sprint to center after
- Hot Wings + Hot Tail: 🏃 Avoid room-wide AoEs, stand in safe zones
- Holes + Fire Trail: Same as Phase 1
- Mechanics repeat until boss dies
