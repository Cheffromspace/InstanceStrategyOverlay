Living Liquid
Players should assign specific positions around the arena to handle mechanics throughout the fight.

Phase 1
- Fluid Swing: 📐 Wide frontal cleave. 💠 🛡️ Tanks position boss away from party.
- Protean Wave: 🔴🔴🔴 Three telegraphs, 6 players marked. 🏃 Marked players move to assigned positions.
- Splash: 💥 Raid-wide AoE damage. 🛡️ Mitigate and heal through.
- Sluice: 🎯 Marked players, AoEs on the floor. 🏃 Marked players move to assigned positions, avoid splash damage and floor AoEs.

Phase 2
- Fluid Strike: 💥 Frontal tankbuster. 🛡️ Tanks must split damage to survive.
- Wash Away: 💨 Players pushed back. 🏃 Stack in center to avoid knockback off edge.
- Digititis: 🤢 Debuffs on tank, healer, DPS. 🧪 Quickly swap debuffs - Blue to DPS, Red to healer, Green to tank.
- Fluid Strike x2: 💥💥 Two tankbusters. 🛡️ Tanks with Vulnerability Up debuff avoid stacking.
- Hand of Prayer/Hand of Parting: 🤝🏃<>🏃 Hands separated or brought together based on open hand/fist. 🏃 Separate hands if fist, bring together if open. Raid-wide damage.
- Equilibrium Concentration: ⚖️ Balance hands' HP within 25 seconds. 🔄 Focus damage on hand with more HP.
- Hand of Pain: 💥 DPS check. ⏳ Hands must be within 4% HP or wipe.

Phase 3
- 👾 Ads spawn in waves and move to edge. 🔥 Burn ads before they reach edge or Paralyze and massive damage to raid.
- Piston Lubricant: 💦 Mucilage causes Pistons to sprint. 🚫 Interrupt Mucilage cast and focus Pistons.
- Gear Lubricant: 💦 Slower with less HP. 🔥 Still kill quickly.
- Drainage: 🔗 Tethers on 2 random players explode. 🏃 Tanks take tethers and move away from group.
- Ferrofluid: ⊕⊖ Positive/Negative tethers. 🧲 Same sign = close, opposite = far. Resolves with push/pull.

Phase 4
- Cascade: 💥 Raid-wide damage, spawns mechanic puddles. 🛡️ Mitigate and heal, identify mechanics based on puddle positions.
- Ferrofluid: ⊕⊖ Positive/Negative tethers on all players. 🧲 Move to assigned positions to handle tethers.
- Fluid Swing: 🤢 Debuffs tanks. 🩹 Tank swap around 4 debuff stacks.
- Sluice/Protean Wave: 🎯 Marked players, 🔴 telegraphs. 🏃 Spread for Sluice, dodge 1st Protean Wave, then separate.
- Sluice/Digititis: 🎯 Marked players, 🤢 debuff swap. 🏃 Both healers take Red, free DPS takes Blue debuff.
- Liquid Gaol: 🚫 Marked healer stunned, Liquid Limb spawns. 🩹 Healer moves away from group, kill Liquid Limb before healer reaches edge.
- Splash x6: 💥💥💥💥💥💥 Massive raid-wide damage. 🛡️ Healers be ready to heal through.
- Throttle: 🤢 Debuff on random player. 💚 Dispel immediately or player dies on resolve.
- Embolus: 🔴 Ball moves towards boss. 🏃 Move boss and avoid ball touching players or boss.
- Phase 4 is a loop of mechanics that repeats until boss is defeated. Burn boss before enrage wipes raid.
