Quickthinx Allthoughts
- The fight involves various mechanics, including spike hazards, AoE attacks, cage mechanics, and add management.
- 🚫 Avoid stepping in spikes to prevent Bleed and Vulnerability Up debuffs
- Sizzlebeam: 💥 When marked with purple, stay away from others. Boss shoots a beam inflicting Electrocution DoT
- Huge AoE attack: ⚡️ Watch for ground markers. Avoid to prevent MP drain and stacking Bleeding DoT
- Uplander Doom: 🛡️ Multi-hit tankbuster inflicting stacking Physical Vulnerability Up on the tank
- Cage Mechanics: 🔒 Boss targets a player, flies up, and sends them to a cage
- Red Cage (DPS): 🔴 Pyretic damage when moving or using actions. Others kill Padlock add
- Purple Cage (DPS): ⏳ Throttle (Doom) debuff. Kill Alaruma add quickly
- White Cage (Off-tank): ❄️ Frostbite DoT. Avoid walls (Deep Freeze). Kill Gobwalker add
- Green Cage (Healer): ☠️ Stand in gas vent to prevent Poison DoT on others
- Add Phase: 👾 Kill Panzer-Doll and Sturm-Doll adds quickly
- Sturm-Doll: 🚫 Interrupt Kugelblitz to prevent raid-wide damage
- Shanoa Phase: 🐱 Black cat spawns True Heart adds
- 💗 DPS focus True Heart adds before they reach and heal/buff the boss
- Weight of the World: 💨 Avoid the rolling steel ball to prevent massive damage and knockback
- Bomb Tether: 💣 Tethered player drags bomb to a corner to minimize damage

Important Notes:
- Most debuffs in this fight cannot be removed with Esuna
- Coordinate tank swaps if necessary due to stacking vulnerability
- Prioritize add management and positioning throughout the fight
