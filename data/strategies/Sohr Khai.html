Chieftain Moglin
- 🔴 Knock away reviving adds with abilities or auto-attacks to prevent resurrection.
- Holy: 💥 Unavoidable AoE damage to entire party. 🩹 Healers be ready.
- ⚔️ Kill Captain Mogsun ASAP to prevent Moglin's invincibility tether.
- 🔴 Attack moogle corpses to knock them out of purple AoE revival circles.
- Kill the 3 moogles when they spawn, then when Moglin casts revive (the circles), shoot the dead moogles out of the circle. Continue to kill the boss, and repeat the process until the boss is dead!

Poqhiraj
- ⚠️ Avoid standing in electrified pools behind destructible walls.
- Jump: 💥 Party-wide damage based on proximity. 🏃 Run away from landing indicator.
- Charge: 💥 More damage in center. 🚶 Stand by walls to avoid knockback into pools.
- Quasar: 💨 ➡️⬅️ Unmarked line AoE. 📍 Stack behind boss to avoid walls.
- Cloud Call: 🌩️ Thundercloud marker deals heavy damage and 🤢 vulnerability debuff. 💥 Place clouds near destroyed walls.
- Stand in the center unless targeted, to prevent Quasar from hitting walls. Place Cloud Calls away from the party, near a wall that will or has been destroyed.

Hraesvelgr

- Hallowed Dive: ➡️⬅️ Line AoE across middle. 🏃 Move to edges, then back to middle.
- Akh Morn: 🔴 🤝 Multi-hit stack marker
- Diamond Storm: 🥶 Arena freezes, 🏃 players slide in direction moved
- Frigid Dive: ➡️⬅️ Line AoE across middle with 🔴 red AoE circles. 🛷 Slide to avoid circles.
- Holy Breath: 💥 Purple 🔴 AoE circles on each player. 🏃<>🏃 Spread out.
