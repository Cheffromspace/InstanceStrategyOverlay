<span class="boss">Bismarck</span>:<br>
- Assign 2 players for cannons, 1 for shield (P1 only)<br>
- 🤢 Stacking debuff on Bismarck, 🏃 leave when he shudders<br>

<span class="boss">Phase 1</span>:<br>
- Same as Hard, kill melee and ranged adds<br>
- Use Dragonkillers when ready, board Bismarck<br>
- Focus DPS on carapace with cooldowns<br>

<span class="boss">Phase 2</span>:<br>
- Major DPS check, tank dragons So'Suwa and Ul'Sanuwa apart<br>
- 🏃 Avoid tornadoes and AoEs<br>
- <span class="ability">Dead Wind/Water</span>: Raid-wide 💥 AoE, applies debuffs<br>
  - 🔵 Will of the Water: Attack 🟢 enemy<br>
  - 🟢 Will of the Wind: Attack 🔵 enemy<br>
- ⚔️ Tanks swap if debuff changes<br>
- Use LB1 when available<br>

<span class="boss">Phase 3</span> (repeats until Bismarck dies or island destroyed):<br>
- 3 adds spawn: 2 small (melee & ranged), 1 big (Nai'maii Vendu)<br>
- Tactic 1: Kill ranged → melee → damage Nai<br>
- Tactic 2: Kill Nai → ranged → damage melee<br>
- Tank & healer stay on island to finish add<br>
- Kill Nai before he destroys island with AoEs<br>

<span class="mechanic">Weather Mechanic</span>:<br>
- 💨 Wind: 🏃 Center for knockback, then avoid center AoE<br>
- 💧 Water: Avoid center splash 🤢, kill Vapor Bubbles<br>
- ⚡ Thunder: 3 players get 2 attacks, Vapor Bubbles absorb 2nd hit<br>

<span class="note">Wait for debuff to expire before re-boarding Bismarck</span><br>
Use LB3 when available to defeat Bismarck
