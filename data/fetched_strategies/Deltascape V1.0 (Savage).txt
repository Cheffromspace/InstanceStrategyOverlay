The OT should stack with the MT and both should mitigate this accordingly.FlameA small raid AoE that summons flame orbs on the ground, indicated by Inside or Outside. These explode after a while, dealing damage and giving a vuln stack to anyone standing in them at the time. Stack for damage and mitigate accordingly.CharybdisDrops all players HP to critical levels.LevinboltA lightning AoE appears centered around every player. Stand right outside of the centre to avoid bad things happening.
