Players must first complete the quest "The Ultimate Ballad" and have completed The Second Coil of Bahamut - Turn 4.

Entering the Second Coil of Bahamut (Savage)
After forming a group of eight players, speak with Anaelle in the North Shroud (X:20.8 Y:27.4) to enter. Please note that all party members must have completed The Second Coil of Bahamut - Turn 4 before entering.
