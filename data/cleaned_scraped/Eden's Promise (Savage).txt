General Information
Eden's Promise (Savage) is 3rd section of Eden (Savage), the main 8-player raid in Shadowbringers. Released on December 8, 2020, Eden's Promise (Savage) requires players with item level of 500 or higher to enter. Once unlocked, players can use Party Finder or other means of forming a full premade party before entering the raid, which is located in Duty Finder→ Raids → Savage Raids (Shadowbringers). They can also enter solo or with a party of fewer than 8 players using an Unrestricted Party.

Unlock
To access Eden's Promise (Savage), players must speak with Lewrey in Amh Araeng (X:26.8 Y:16.4) with a level 80 Disciple of War/Magic after completing the quest, Where I Belong.

Floors
Eden's Promise: Umbra (Savage) - E9S, Item Level 500, Boss: Cloud of Darkness, Time Limit: 90 min
Eden's Promise: Litany (Savage) - E10S, Item Level 505, Boss: Shadowkeeper, Time Limit: 90 min  
Eden's Promise: Anamorphosis (Savage) - E11S, Item Level 510, Boss: Fatebreaker, Time Limit: 90 min
Eden's Promise: Eternity (Savage) - E12S, Item Level 510, Boss: Eden's Promise, Oracle of Darkness, Time Limit: 120 min

Item level restrictions do not apply when registering as a full party or an unrestricted party.
