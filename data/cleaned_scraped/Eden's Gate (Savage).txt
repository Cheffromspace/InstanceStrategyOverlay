General Information
Eden's Gate (Savage) is first section of Eden (Savage), the main 8-player raid in Shadowbringers. Released on July 30, 2019, Eden's Gate (Savage) requires players with item level of 440 or higher to enter. Once unlocked, players can use Party Finder or other means of forming a full premade party before entering the raid, which is located in Duty Finder→ Raids → Savage Raids (Shadowbringers). They can also enter solo or with a party of fewer than 8 players using an Unrestricted Party.

Unlock
To access Eden's Gate (Savage), players must speak with Lewrey in Amh Araeng (X:26.8 Y:16.4) with a level 80 Disciple of War/Magic after completing the quest, The Next Piece of the Puzzle.
