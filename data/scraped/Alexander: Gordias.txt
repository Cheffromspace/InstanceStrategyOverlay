Here is the cleaned up text for the Alexander: Gordias strategy guide:

General Information
Alexander: Gordias is the first section of Alexander, the main 8-player raid in Heavensward. Released on July 7, 2015, Gordias requires players with item level of 170 or higher to enter. Once unlocked, the raid can be queued from the Duty Finder.

Unlock
Completion of the level 60 quest Disarmed by talking to Slowfix in Idyllshire (x7,y6). Players are required to complete the Main Scenario Quest Heavensward. You do not need to have done any of The Coils of Bahamut.

To unlock each floor after the first, continue the quest line and complete the previous floor. To continue, you will need to talk to Biggs in Bigwest Shortstop (x21,y18). Each floor is listed separately.

Floors
Level Requirement: Disciple of War or Magic level 60
Party Size: Eight players

Item level restrictions do not apply when registering as a full party or an unrestricted party.

Rewards / Exchange
Treasure coffers that appear upon completing Alexander: Gordias will not yield gear, but instead yield tokens that can be traded for gear of your choosing. 

As of patch 3.1, weekly restrictions on rewards have been removed. Also note that there is no weekly restriction on entering Alexander: Gordias.
