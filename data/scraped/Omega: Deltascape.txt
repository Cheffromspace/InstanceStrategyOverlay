General Information
Omega: Deltascape is the first section of Omega, the main 8-player raid in Stormblood. Released on July 4, 2017, Deltascape requires players with item level of 295 or higher to enter. Once unlocked, the raid can be queued from the Duty Finder.

Unlock
Completion of the level 70 quest chain starting with The Hunt for Omega by talking to Wedge in Rhalgr's Reach (x12.6,y12.4). Players are required to complete the Main Scenario Quest Stormblood.

To unlock each floor after the first, continue the quest line and complete the consecutive floor. Each floor is listed separately.

Floors
Level Requirement: Disciple of War or Magic level 70
Party Size: Eight players

Duty | Abbreviation | Min IL | Boss(es) | Tomestones | Time Limit

Deltascape V2.0 | O2N | 295 | Catastrophe | 10 | 90 min
Deltascape V3.0 | O3N | 295 | Halicarnassus | 10 | 90 min
Deltascape V4.0 | O4N | 295 | Exdeath | 10 | 90 min

Item level restrictions do not apply when registering as a full party or an unrestricted party.

Rewards
Treasure coffers that appear upon completing Omega: Deltascape will not yield gear, but instead yield tokens that can be traded for gear of your choosing.

As of Patch 4.1, weekly restrictions on rewards have been removed.

Tokens Dropped on Each Floor:
Deltascape V1.0: 2x Deltascape Lens (Head), Deltascape Shaft (Body), Deltascape Crank (Hands), Deltascape Spring (Legs), Deltascape Pedal (Feet), 2x Deltascape Bolt (Accessories)  
Deltascape V2.0: Deltascape Lens (Head), Deltascape Shaft (Body), Deltascape Crank (Hands), Deltascape Spring (Legs), 2x Deltascape Pedal (Feet), 2x Deltascape Bolt (Accessories)
Deltascape V3.0: Deltascape Lens (Head), 2x Deltascape Shaft (Body), Deltascape Crank (Hands), 2x Deltascape Spring (Legs), 2x Deltascape Bolt (Accessories)
Deltascape V4.0: 2x Deltascape Shaft (Body), Deltascape Crank (Hands), 2x Deltascape Spring (Legs), Deltascape Pedal (Feet), 2x Deltascape Bolt (Accessories), Deltascape Crystalloid

Item Exchange
By speaking with Gelfradus in Rhalgr's Reach (x13.7,y12.0), players can exchange tokens obtained from Omega: Deltascape for corresponding gear.
