General Information
Omega: Sigmascape (Savage) is the 2nd section of Omega (Savage), the main 8-player raid in Stormblood. Released on January 30, 2018, Sigmascape (Savage) requires players with item level of 340 or higher to enter. Once unlocked, players can use Party Finder or other means of forming a full premade party before entering the raid, which is located in Duty Finder → Raids → Savage Raids (Stormblood). They can also enter solo or with a party of fewer than 8 players using an Unrestricted Party.

Unlock
To access Omega: Sigmascape (Savage), players must interact with the Magitek Terminal in Rhalgr's Reach (X:13.5 Y:12.4) after completing the following quest: Test World of Ruin.

Rewards
Upon completing Sigmascape (Savage) duties, a treasure coffer will appear containing a Diamond Coffer. Using this item transforms it into gear corresponding to the user's current job. In addition, the coffers that appear upon completing Sigmascape V4.0 (Savage) yield weapons.

Item Exchange
In addition to gear, players will receive a Sigmascape Datalog from each area, which can be exchanged for gear of your choosing.
By speaking with Gelfradus in Rhalgr's Reach (x13.7,y12.0), players can exchange Sigmascape datalogs from Omega: Sigmascape (Savage) for corresponding gear.
